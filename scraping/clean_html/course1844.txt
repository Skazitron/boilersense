ENGT 10300 - Industrial Technology Exploring Civil Engineering And Architecture

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Students learn various aspects of civil engineering and architecture, apply their knowledge to designing and development of a commercial property.  Students learn about documenting their project, problem-solving, and communications in the civil engineering community. Typically offered Summer Fall Spring.
