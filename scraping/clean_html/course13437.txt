AAS 57500 - Theories Of African American Studies

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course addresses the development of an African American intellectual tradition. The course will span disciplines and swathes of time in order to understand how scholars not only created new ideas but developed theories that shaped and changed academic inquiry.
