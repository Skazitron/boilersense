IET 21400 - Introduction To Supply Chain Management Technology

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course is an introduction to supply chain management technology.  Topics include supply chain functions including how to organize a supply chain, supply chain strategy, supply chain process mapping, and use of supply chain technologies, analysis, and performance measurements.  Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Describe the building blocks of supply chain strategy.
2.  Explain the steps involved in designing a global supply chain network.
3.  Plan and coordinate operations across the supply chain.
4.  Describe key elements required to manage product flow.
5.  Use enterprise resource planning (ERP) software to manage supply chain operations.
