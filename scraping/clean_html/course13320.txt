DANC 24500 - Practicum In Dance Performance And Production

Credit Hours: 0.00 to 2.00.  Open through audition. The study and practice of dance rehearsal, performance and basic concert production techniques, culminating in a main-stage dance concert. Permission of instructor required.

    0.000 TO     2.000 Credit hours

      West Lafayette


Repeatable for Additional Credit: Yes - May be repeated an unlimited number of times


Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Demonstrate understanding of professional dance company member responsibilities and processes by learning choreography as assigned and performing at the highest standard.
2.  Demonstrate personal artistry as a dancer and dependability as an ensemble member through rehearsals, technical preparations and performances.
3.  Relate experience of observing all of the works in the dance concert and write a critique relating to process of preparing for and performing work in the concert.
