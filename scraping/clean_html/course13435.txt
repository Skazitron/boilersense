AAS 49100 - Special Topics In African American Studies

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 4.00.  Special Topics in African American Studies addresses special topics or projects under the direction of the instructor in the field of African American or African Diasporic Studies.  The course may vary from 1-4 credit hours.  It may be taught as a lecture or as a lecture with a lab.
