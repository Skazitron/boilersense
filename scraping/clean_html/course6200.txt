VCS 80920 - Large Animal Surgery

Credit Hours: 2.50.  This course will cover the core fundamentals of surgical diseases of multiple large animal species, including the horse, bovine, small ruminants and camelids.  Case management approaches are utilized to provide examples of disease conditions affecting these large animal species. Typically offered Spring Summer.
