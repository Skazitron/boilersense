COM 32500 - Interviewing: Principles And Practice

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Theory and practice of methods in selected interview settings: informational, employment, and persuasive. Emphasis on communication between two persons, questioning techniques, and the logical and psychological bases of interpersonal persuasion.
