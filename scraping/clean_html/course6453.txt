OLS 58200 - Leadership And Organizational Change

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Explores issues in leadership and organizational change. Included are change theories, utilizing resistance to change, contemporary approaches to change, the future workplace, and researching best practices in organizational change. Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Attain an understanding of the major models of change theory.
2.  Gain skills in managing change through pragmatic applications of change theories.
3.  Complete a major change research project in which they investigate a change initiative, gather data, and make recommendations for successfully implementing the change.
