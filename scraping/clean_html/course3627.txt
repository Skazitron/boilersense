COM 65200 - Communication Education And Training

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course is designed to introduce fundamental concepts related to learning theory and learning design as applied to training and educational settings. The course will examine best practices and strategies with a focus on assessing and enhancing communication-related skills.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Define key communication skills and vocabulary.
2.  Understand and apply the basic tenets of learning theory.
3.  Design, develop, and evaluate an effective educational workshop or course.
