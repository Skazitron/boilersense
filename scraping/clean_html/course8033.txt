CPB 22700 - Microbiology For Veterinary Technicians-DL

Credit Hours: 2.00.  This course is designed to enable the veterinary technician to satisfactorily collect and process specimens, and characterize and identify microorganisms that may be encountered in veterinary practice situations. This course will introduce microorganisms commonly encountered in veterinary medicine. Different laboratory techniques will be illustrated.
