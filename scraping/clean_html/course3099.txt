MSL 10100 - Foundations of Officership

Credit Hours: 1.00 or 2.00.  Examines the unique duties and responsibilities of officers.  Discuss organization and role of the Army.  Review basic life skills pertaining to fitness and communication.  Analyze Army values and expected ethical behavior. Typically offered Fall.

    0.000 TO     2.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Demonstrate proficiency in mission command philosophy.
2.  Demonstrate proficiency in mission command leader and commander tasks.
3.  Demonstrate proficiency in mission command staff tasks.
4.  Demonstrate proficiency in mission command systems.
5.  Demonstrate capacity in creative critical thinking.
6.  Demonstrate proficiency in communications skills.
7.  Demonstrate proficiency in cultural awareness, cross cultural competencies in the strategic environment of 2025 and beyond.
8.  Pursue Comprehensive Fitness / Resiliency Skills and Performance Enhancement Skills.
9.  Pursue lifelong learning, self-assessment, and goal setting.
10.  Demonstrate proficiency in Army and Joint doctrine.
11.  Support Army policies, programs, and processes.
12.  Demonstrate technical and tactical competency.
