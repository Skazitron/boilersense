EDCI 21100 - Education Scholars II

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  This is the second course in a two course, introductory sequence for College of Education students enrolled in the Honors College and/or Devito Program scholarship recipients. In this course, students will work on a research project with a faculty mentor, will participate in reflection and discussion of that research experience, will participate in seminars with faculty to read and discuss research, and continue to explore their own research interests and campus resources. Typically offered Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Participate in a semester-long research project with a faculty mentor for 3-4 hours per week.
2.  Participate in structured written and oral reflection on their semester-long research  experiences.
3.  Interact with faculty in the College of Education through 3-4 seminars focused on discussion of their research and scholarship.
4.  Explore their research interests, and campus resources.
5.  Present their research projects during a public presentation session at the end of the semester.
