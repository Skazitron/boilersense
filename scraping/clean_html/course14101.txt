AD 42600 - Robotic Art

Credit Hours: 3.00.  The Robotic Art Studio is an experimental and interdisciplinary class that explores the aesthetics, technologies, ideologies and cultural impact of robotics.  Students build robots that beep, blink, bounce and walk.
