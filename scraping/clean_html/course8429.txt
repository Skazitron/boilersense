CGT 14100 - Internet Foundations Technologies And Development

Credit Hours: 3.00.  (CNIT 14100) The course explores the history, architecture, and development of the World Wide Web. Current tagging and scripting languages are covered in a tool-independent environment. Topics also include authoring tools, design, graphic and multimedia formats, and commerce, implementation, and security issues. PC literacy required.
