NUR 52500 - Informatics In Nursing

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Integrates nursing science with computer technology and information science to identify, gather, and manage information.  Emphasis on technology based health applications with support clinical, administrative, research, and educational decision making enhancing the efficacy of nursing endeavors.
