NUTR 54200 - Engagement Experience

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Application of previously acquired knowledge and communication skills in a culminating supervised practice experience in one of seven engagement tracks. Placements at professional sites incorporate individual student's preferred track in foodservice, industry, nutrition-fitness-wellness-energy balance, community/public health, long-term care, medical nutrition therapy or research. Students complete a major project demonstrating readiness to practice as an entry-level dietitian.
