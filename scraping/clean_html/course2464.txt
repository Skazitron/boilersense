BTNY 30500 - Plant Evolution And Taxonomy

Credit Hours: 3.00.  The principles of classification of seed plants, with emphasis on methods of identification in laboratory and field. Requires class trips. Students will pay individual lodging or meal expenses when necessary.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Become familiar with locally important plant orders and families.
2.  Learn the vegetative and floral characters (and the associated terminology) that are essential for the identification of flowering plants.
3.  Become proficient with the use of keys so that you can quickly determine the scientific name of any plant.
4.  Learn how DNA sequences are used to decipher phylogenetic relationships among plants.
