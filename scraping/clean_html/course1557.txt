MGMT 52500 - Marketing Analytics

Credit Hours: 2.00.  This course equips students with quantitative and analytical techniques, and statistical software tools required for implementing marketing concepts such as segmentation, targeting, product positioning and product design. It trains students to use a data and analytics driven systematic approach for solving real marketing decision problems.
