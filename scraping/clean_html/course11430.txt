PTEC 40602 - ePortfolio VIII

Credit Hours: 1.50.  Part two of students curating an electronic portfolio demonstrating proficient levels of competence across broad competencies. Students will meet individually and in small groups with faculty mentors to review and critique progress.  Typically offered Fall Spring.

    1.500 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1. Demonstrate proficient level of understanding of the primary competencies and their sub-competencies. 
2. Demonstrate proficient level of information literacy using a variety of effective communication techniques in an ePortfolio.
3. Demonstrate proficient level of skill in developing their individual ePortfolio.
