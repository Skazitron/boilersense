CEM 32100 - Construction Engineering Materials Lab

Credit Hours: 1.00.  The nature and performance of materials under load stress.  Important engineering materials for evaluation of physical and mechanical properties include ferrous and nonferrous metals, plastics, bituminous materials, Portland cement, aggregates, concrete, timber, and particulate systems.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Evaluate the behavior and properties of engineering materials.
2.  Assess the nature and performance of materials under load: Theory vs. Reality.
3.  Facilitate the understanding of the strength and weakness of major construction engineering materials and their application.
4.  Design and conduct experiments, and analyze and interpret data.
