PHIL 58000 - Proseminar In Philosophy

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  Designed primarily for majors in philosophy who have already successfully completed six hours in philosophy. Other students may be admitted to the course with the special consent of the instructor in charge. Topic to be selected by the department. Typically offered Fall Spring.
