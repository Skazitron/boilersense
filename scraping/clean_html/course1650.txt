MGMT 65200 - Entrepreneurship

Credit Hours: 2.00 or 3.00.  Examines entrepreneurship with a focus on the start-up process for high growth new ventures. Cases will be used, and students will conduct a feasibility analysis of a new venture concept. Prerequisites: MGMT 61000 and MGMT 62000. Concurrent Prerequisites: MGMT 65000. Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Learn how to sketch out a possible business idea and "pitch it" to gain team members and participants.
2.  Develop a business model and learn the advantages of bootstrap financing vs venture capital financing for new ventures.
3.  Describe a value proposition of a business plan and show the current valuation.
4.  Learn how to write a Business Plan.
