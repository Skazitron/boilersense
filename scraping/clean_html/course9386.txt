ECET 49500 - Intensive Project Design/Development

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Credit will not be granted for both ECET 495 and ECET 497. This course is an intensive conclusion to the senior design process begun in ECET 496. The course concludes with a formal demonstration and oral presentation on the finished product and a written report on the final design.
