Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Learn basics of reading and translating ancient Greek, completing their introduction to core vocabulary, grammar, and syntax.
2.  Improve understanding of English through increased sensitivity to grammatical structures and stylistic choices and by extending their vocabulary through etymological connections.
