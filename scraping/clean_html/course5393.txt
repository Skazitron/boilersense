NUTR 29700 - Introduction To Honors Research

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Introduces students to the design and implementation of an honors research project.  Typically offered Fall Spring.

    1.000 Credit hours


Syllabus Available

Levels: Graduate, Professional, Undergraduate 

Schedule Types: Individual Study, Lecture

Department: Nutrition Science 


Course Attributes: Honors, Lower Division 


May be offered at any of the following campuses:      

      West Lafayette


Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Identify the requirements for graduation with honors from Nutrition Science.
2.  Define specific goals for completion of their Honors Project.
3.  Recognize the benefits of graduating with honors.
4.  Discuss research currently being done in Nutrition Science.
5.  Identify faculty interested in serving as mentors for students pursuing Honors Research Projects in Nutrition Science.
6.  Locate and obtain research articles relevant to their research project.
7.  Write appropriate citations for research articles.
8.  Explain the scientific method and research ethics.
