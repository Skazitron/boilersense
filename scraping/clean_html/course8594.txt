CEM 29000 - Construction Engineering Seminar

Credit Hours: 1.00.  An orientation course to prepare the student for professional practice in construction engineering including information on Careers and Issues in Construction; History and Culture of the U.S. Construction Industry; Engineering Ethics and Preparation for Leadership.  Information and assistance is also provided related to Specialty Options for Plans of Study; Students Organizations; Opportunities for Construction Research and Community Outreach at Purdue.
