PSY 31400 - Introduction To Learning

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course attempts to make clear the theoretical and practical implications of learning principles and findings. Various theories of learning are examined and the implications of these theories, and the learning approach generally, for a variety of practical problems are emphasized.
