BIOL 54601 - Topics In Infectious Diseases

Credit Hours: 1.00.  The course will discuss the history and current progress in infectious diseases with a focus on the cell biology of infectious diseases. Scientific publications will be selected based on their impact in the field and how it facilitates student learning. Students will acquire up-to-date knowledge of infectious diseases and be given instructional direction on how to select, read and present scientific findings.
