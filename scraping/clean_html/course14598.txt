SYS 49000 - Topics In Systems

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 6.00.  This is an UG Level variable title course for topics related to Systems (Systems Thinking, Systems Engineering, Systems Dynamics, etc). UG students can enroll in this course to do independent study or individual project with the instructor. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
