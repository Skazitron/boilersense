AD 69300 - Historical Problems In Art Education

Credit Hours: 3.00.  (EDCI 63000) Examination of methods used to investigate art education's history, past theory, and practices. Class work will include readings and discussion and will culminate in guided research and a short historical study. Permission of instructor required. Prerequisite:  BA in art education.
