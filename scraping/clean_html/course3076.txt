ASL 39000 - Undergraduate Research In American Sign Language

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course is designed to allow for the investigation of topics related to the linguistic structure of ASL, populations of ASL language learners, sub-populations of the Deaf Community at the undergraduate level. Permission of instructor required.
