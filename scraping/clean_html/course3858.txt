AT 39500 - Turbine Aircraft Simulation Laboratory

Credit Hours: 1.00.  This course is a laboratory flight experience in a turbine aircraft flight simulator, emphasizing normal and abnormal aircraft operations. Emphasis is on FAA-required flight procedures for pilot certification and safe operation of turbine-powered aircraft. Students seeking an FAA type rating must receive a grade of at least a B- in AT 35300. Permission of department required.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Demonstrate proficiency in flight planning, instrument flight procedures, and simulated aircraft operations.
2.  Demonstrate proficiency in preflight, takeof, departure, holding patterns, approach/missed approach, and normal landing procedures.
3.  Demonstrate proficiency in operating all aircraft systems in both normal and abnormal situations.
