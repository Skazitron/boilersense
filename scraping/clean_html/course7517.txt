BIOL 39300 - Preparing For Your Future In Biology

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Biology 39300 is a one-credit course for junior and seniors in the Department of Biological Sciences. This course will help students begin to prepare for life after Purdue. Students will learn about interviewing, job searching, graduate and professional school searching, resumes, industrial practices, and how to evaluate job/school offers. Construct a polished image on top of your solid biology foundation! Get the job or get admitted to the school you want.
