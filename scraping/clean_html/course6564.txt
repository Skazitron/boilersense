IT 54500 - Biometrics Technology And Applications

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Examines biometrics technology as it relates to security, access control, and the authentication of individuals.  The course will examine biometric technologies including iris, face, finger, hand geometry, dynamic signature verification, skin print analysis, and voice recognition. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
