MGMT 42610 - Brand Management

Credit Hours: 3.00. This course is designed to provide an understanding of how to manage a brand, product, or product category. The course concerns issues related to customer behavior and customer decision making, with an emphasis on how to utilize such customer-oriented knowledge for building and maintaining brand equity. This course builds on concepts and topics covered in the core marketing course. Typically offered Fall Spring.
