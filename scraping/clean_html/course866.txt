IE 53000 - Quality Control

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Principles and practices of statistical quality control in industry. Control charts for measurements and for attributes.  Acceptance sampling by attributes and by measurements.  Standard sampling plans.  Sequential analysis. Sampling inspection of continuous production. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
