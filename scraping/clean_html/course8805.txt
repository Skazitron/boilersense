ECON 51300 - Economic Theory

Credit Hours: 3.00 or 4.00.  (West Lafayette, Calumet) 3.00 (North Central) Theoretical analysis of a market economy with an emphasis on decision processes of managers. Consideration is given to micro aspects of price determination, utilization of resources and market organization, and to aggregative concepts of national income and employment.
