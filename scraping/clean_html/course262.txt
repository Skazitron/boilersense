ENGL 40201 - English Capstone

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course will focus on the significant revision and completion of a research paper or substantial portfolio. Workshop, individual conferences, readings, lectures, short writing assignments, and discussion of various topics in English scholarship will form the center of the course. Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Construct sustained and persuasive arguments based on textual evidence by producing a research paper or substantive portfolio.
2.  Conduct independent research on a topic by gathering, evaluating, and synthesizing information and source materials.
3.  Produce effective written and verbal communication that demonstrates critical thinking and creative problem solving.
