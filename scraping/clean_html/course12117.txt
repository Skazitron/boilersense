EDCI 42300 - The Teaching Of Social Studies In Secondary Schools

Credit Hours: 3.00.  The purpose of this course is the development of a theoretical framework and pedagogical skills necessary to teach social studies to middle and high school students. Students are introduced to the general issues involved in planning and organizing a social studies curriculum. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Develop theoretical understanding and pedagogical skills necessary to teach social studies to middle and high school students.
2.  Develop understanding of cross collaboration, communication, and the characteristics of the community, school, and classroom.
3.  Develop specific and relevant understanding of student characteristics, approaches to learning, or skills and prior learning.
4.  Learn general issues involved in planning and organizing a social studies curriculum.
5.  Obtain the knowledge, skills, and attitudes necessary to become a reflective and skilled social studies teacher.
