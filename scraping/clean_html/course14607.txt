AGR 11200 - Introduction To Agricultural Economics Academic Programs

Credit Hours: 0.50.  An introduction to the academic programs offered in the Department of Agricultural Economics. Topics include, but are not limited to undergraduate plans of study, courses, experiential programs, internships, student organizations, career opportunities, academic policies, scholarships, and student services. Course meets during weeks 1-8.
