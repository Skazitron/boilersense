EDCI 52002 - Curriculum And Instruction Online MS Seminar II

Credit Hours: 1.00.  This seminar is designed to help you to understand the knowledge and skills necessary for your success throughout the Curriculum and Instruction program. In addition to providing you with information that will help you succeed in the program, you are also asked to think about ways to make yourself more marketable to current and future employers. Permission of department required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Investigate the skills and knowledge needed to be successful in a relevant career within the C&I field.
2.  Describe program and career goals.
3.  Apply research skills required to compose an annotated bibliography.
4.  Create the foundation of a C&I portfolio displaying programmatic achievements and course competencies.
