AAE 62600 - Turbulence And Turbulence Modeling

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Physical description of turbulence. Reynolds averaged equations. Scaling of basic turbulent flows. Homogeneous turbulence, spectra and two-point correlations. Hierarchy of turbulence models, including zero-, one-, and two-equation models, Reynolds stress models, large eddy simulations, and direct numerical simulations. Additional topics, such as compressibility effects on turbulence and probability density function methods, as time allows.  Prerequisite:  AAE 51100.
