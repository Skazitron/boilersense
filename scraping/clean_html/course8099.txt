CPB 88700 - Avian Medicine

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course is designed for senior veterinary students to receive advanced training in avian disease diagnosis and health management in commercial poultry flocks. Critical poultry infectious and non-infectious diseases will be discussed. The student will visit production facilities for layers, broilers, turkeys, or ducks. Vaccination and medication programs for these species will be discussed. Necropsy experience will be gained from poultry, game bird, and pet bird accessions to the Animal Disease Diagnostic Laboratory (ADDL). Total clinic hours 129.
