PSY 34200 - Introduction To Psychology Of Personality

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course integrates empirical studies (observational, correlational, experimental) with classical and current personality theories. Topics include the biological foundations of personality, self-esteem, extraversion, sex role orientation, authoritarianism, and personality disorders. Minimum competence is assumed in basic experimental design and correlations.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Define personality from a scientific psychological perspective.
2.  Discuss the strengths and weaknesses of the methods and sources of data for personality researchers.
3.  Describe biological and trait models of personality.
4.  Discuss the relationship between personality, psychopathology, personality disorders and psychotherapy.
5.  Describe how culture and environment interact with personality.
