LING 53100 - Semantics I: Lexical And Sentential Semantics

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Foundations of semantic analysis and survey of current linguistic semantic theories and methods. Semantics at the lexical and sentential levels.  Combinatorial, truth-conditional, pragmatic, contextual, and computational semantics. Offered in conjunction with ENGL 51100. Typically offered Fall Spring.
