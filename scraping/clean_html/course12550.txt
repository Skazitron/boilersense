FVS 44200 - Directing The Narrative Short Film

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Students will gain knowledge of all phases of film production in the course of completing multiple short films. This class will emulate standard film industry practices and guidelines.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Summarize the overall film production process.
2.  Create examples of each stage of the production process.
3.  Construct multiple complete films, in the roles of Director and/or Assistant Director, using professional and industry standard techniques based upon the production process.
4.  Evaluate completed films for content and technique.
