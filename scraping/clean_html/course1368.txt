CHNS 23000 - Chinese Literature In Translation

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Reading and discussion of selected texts from major writers and genres. The course provides a broad survey of the Chinese literacy tradition and its cultural and historical background. Emphasis may vary from year to year. Knowledge of Chinese not required.
