ECON 64000 - Economic And Social Policy

Credit Hours: 2.00 or 3.00.  The role of government in the American economy. Policies relating to the environment, energy, transportation, labor markets, the distribution of income, macro stability, and international trade are analyzed. Prerequisite:  ECON 51300, Master's student standing or higher and Management majors only.
