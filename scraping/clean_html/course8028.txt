CPB 15100 - Clinical Pathology II-DL

Credit Hours: 1.50.  This course is the second of three courses that provide basic instruction in the concepts of clinical pathology to the veterinary technician student. This course will place an emphasis on white blood cell production, function, as well as white blood cell morphology characteristics and identification.
