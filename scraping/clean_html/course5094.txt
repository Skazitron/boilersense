ECE 32300 - Electromechanical Motion Devices and Systems Laboratory

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Experiments closely coordinated with ECE 32100 involving measurement of fundamental parameters of various electromechanical devices using modern instrumentation techniques. Computer simulation is used to predict steady-state and dynamic operating characteristics. Comparison of predicted and measured performance is emphasized.
