AT 38500 - Design Support Analysis

Credit Hours: 2.00.  This course is designed to teach students interested in engineering design, design support, customer support, or aerospace manufacturing, the elements of product support and the analysis of design as related to the manufacturability, maintainability, and supportability of aerospace products. The students will be expected to complete a life cycle cost analysis and logistics plan for a design.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Able to break the system into its functional groups.
2.  Determine the Mean Time Between Failure of the system and the Availability of the system.
3.  Determine the Life Cycle Cost of ownership of the system.
5.  Determine the facilities, staffing, tooling, and spare parts requirements for a system.
