LATN 20200 - Latin Level IV

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Upper intermediate reading course in Latin prose and poetry, designed to consolidate students' grasp of grammar and syntax, broaden vocabulary, and develop precision and confidence in reading and translation.  Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Continue to reinforce knowledge of the basic grammar, vocabulary, and syntax needed for reading and translating Latin.
2.  Apply foundational knowledge to selections from complex and stylistically sophisticated Latin literature.
3.  Improve understanding of English by enriching their vocabulary and through increased sensitivity to grammar and style.
