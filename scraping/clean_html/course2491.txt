CHM 10000 - Preparation For General Chemistry

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An introduction to the basic ideas and laboratory techniques of chemistry, together with relevant parts of algebra and elementary physics. Intended for those whose background does not permit them to proceed directly with a general chemistry course. Not available for credit toward graduation in the School of Science.
