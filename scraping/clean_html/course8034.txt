CPB 24000 - Public And Occupational Health For Vet Techs I -DL

Credit Hours: 1.00.  This is the first of two courses that provide basic instruction in the concepts of workplace safety related to the veterinary technician. This course will place an emphasis on the basic information needed to recognize hazards commonly encountered and the steps that should be taken to reduce the risk of exposure to hazards. Other practical aspects of public health will be discussed.
