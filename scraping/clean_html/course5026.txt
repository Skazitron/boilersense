KOR 28000 - Special Topics In Korean Language

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course will be offered as a variable-title course focusing on Korean language topics such as history of Korean language, Korean linguistics, Korean dialects, Korean sociolinguistics studies, applied linguistic foci in Korean language, etc. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
