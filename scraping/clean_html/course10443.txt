MET 44500 - Applied Metalcasting

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course applies metalcasting principles from concept through final product. Students design and optimize essential elements of casting systems using both simulation and fabrication methods and testing.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Apply sound design and manufacturing principles in the production of metal-cast products.
2.  Design effective and efficient molds for multiple metal-cast products.
3.  Apply methods to effectively identify, prevent and correct common product defects.
4.  Demonstrate effective use of applicable software optimization tools.
5.  Safely demonstrate metalcasting production techniques and practices.
