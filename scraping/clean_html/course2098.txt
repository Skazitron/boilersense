ME 35200 - Machine Design I

Credit Hours: 4.00.  Introduction to the principles of design and analysis of machines and machine components. Design for functionality, motion, force, strength, and reliability. Review of fatigue failure theories and analysis of fatigue stresses. Laboratory experience provides open-ended projects to reinforce the design process. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
