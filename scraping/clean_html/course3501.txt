COM 41900 - Judgment And Decision Making

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course is designed to foster an understanding, critique and analysis on issues related to judgment and decision processes. Permission of instructor required.

    3.000 Credit hours

Repeatable for Additional Credit: Yes - May be repeated up to 1 times


Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Encourage the application of theories of judgment and decision making to applied decision contexts.
2.  Explore the interplay between communication and decision-making theories.
3.  Acquire some practical, general skills for decision-making that are useful in everyday life and work contexts.
