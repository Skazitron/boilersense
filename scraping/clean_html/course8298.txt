ANTH 37700 - Anthropology Of Hunter-Gatherer Societies

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Selective global survey of societies whose mode of subsistence is/was based on the collection of wild food resources. Topics to be covered include: the development and current state of theory, ecology, social organization, land use, demography, subsistence rights, and worldview.
