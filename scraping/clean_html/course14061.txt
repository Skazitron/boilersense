AD 36800 - Etching And Intaglio Printmaking

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An introduction to the techniques of etching, engraving, aquatint, drypoint, collagraph, monoprint, and related processes. Emphasis on fine art conceptual issues, creativity, matting and framing art, and professional practices.
