CSR 32200 - Field Experience In Apparel Design And Technology

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  Approved in-service in the apparel industry and any related sewn product company and/or theatrical production. This field experience is intended to provide the opportunity for a student to apply skills and expertise gained through the study in apparel design courses. Required report of daily responsibilities plus a final report. Minimum of 140 hours of work per credit hour; Required 2 credits minimum. Advanced approval of director and employer; and attendance at organization meeting. Permission of department required.
