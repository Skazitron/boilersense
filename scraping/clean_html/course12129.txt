EDCI 44600 - Directed Observation For Family And Consumer Sciences

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 4.00.  Field-based experience in a middle or high school, a Cooperative Extension setting, or other applicable career setting for family and consumer sciences educators.  Build professional capacity through focused, participatory experiences that include interactions with family and consumer sciences educators, other school/agency personnel, and students/clients.  Thirty-five hours of documented experience in approved setting needed for one credit.  Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Develop knowledge and awareness of family and consumer sciences education professional setting, including purposes, structures, day-to-day activities, and interactions among participants.
2.  Examine professional responsibilities of family and consumer sciences educators, and corresponding knowledge, skills, and capacities for professional effectiveness.
3.  Build professional experiences and capacity as a family and consumer sciences educator.
4.  Assess personal competence, goals, and areas for continued professional growth as a family and consumer sciences educator.
