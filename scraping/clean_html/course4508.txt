FS 16200 - Introduction To Food Processing

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Application of introductory food science principles (e.g. food chemistry, food microbiology, and unit operations) to food processing; emphasis will be placed on food formulation, pilot-scale process design, and resulting food quality. May require class trips. Typically offered Spring.
