CSR 41500 - Sales Force Management

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An introduction to sales force management. An examination of the sales force management process, including principles of recruiting and selecting, training, compensation, motivation, supervision, and evaluation. Planning skills, including sales force deployment and the role of the sales force in sales forecasting, are also covered.
