ENTR 46000 - Internship And Career Preparation Seminar

Credit Hours: 1.00.  This online course helps students develop a self-awareness and an entrepreneurial mindset to set them apart from other job seekers. Through self-assessment and reflection, they evaluate their talents, skills, and goals to enhance career preparedness. They develop a professional brand through enhancements to their LinkedIn profile, resume, interviewing skills, networking, and an e-portfolio. ENTR 46000 serves as an option course for the Certificate in Entrepreneurship and Innovation. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Compare existing knowledge and skills to those required in one's desired career.   
2.  Seek out resources and expertise necessary to transition to a professional environment.
3.  Develop a professional brand through an enhanced LinkedIn profile, resume review, interviewing skills, and networking.
4.  Showcase educational and professional skills and interests through a website or e-portfolio.
