FNR 44500 - Urban Forest Issues

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course presents an array of topics germane to the management of trees in the urban environment. This includes the benefits of trees and general tree care, tree appraisal, tree ordinances, tree inventory and management plans, and tree preservation and construction. Typically offered Fall.
