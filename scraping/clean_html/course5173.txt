ECE 44000 - Transmission Of Information

Credit Hours: 4.00.  Analysis and design of analog and digital communication systems. Emphasis on engineering applications of theory to communication system design. The laboratory introduces the use of advanced engineering workstations in the design and testing of communication systems.
