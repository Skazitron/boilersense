CGT 37207 - User Experience Design Experience Studio III

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course provides students with opportunities to explore opportunities in a company-like environment. Students are knowledgeable project members and beginning to transition into subject matter experts and managers. Projects utilize human-centered design principles to provide a rich user experience.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Create and manage teams, resources, and activities.
2.  Create and manage project strategy.
3.  Demonstrate depth in one or more UX specializations.
4.  Champion UX approaches to diverse stakeholders in a professional manner.
