SLHS 22700 - Elements Of Linguistics

Credit Hours: 3.00.  (ENGL 22700) Credit will not be awarded for both SLHS 22700 and LC 26100. A summary of what is known about human language, its structure, its universality, and its diversity; language in its social setting; language in relation to other aspects of human inquiry and knowledge.
