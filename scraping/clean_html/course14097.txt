AD 42200 - Advanced Studies In Photography  And Related Media II

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An advanced level course in photography and related media focusing on production of a formally and conceptually coherent body of work, articulated within an appropriate, informed critical context.  Culminates in curation of formal exhibition.
