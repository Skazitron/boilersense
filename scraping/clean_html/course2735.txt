TLI 45830 - Service Learning

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Service learning is a reflective experience in which students actively engage in the community and integrate that experience into the classroom. Permission of department required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Demonstrate an ability to apply leadership and interpersonal skills to work effectively with diverse populations in the community.
2.  Use written communication skills to synthesize the readings, class discussions, and service experiences.
3.  Reflect on civic responsibility and its role in society.
