THTR 23500 - Vocal/Physical Preparation

Credit Hours:  2.00.  Vocal/Physical Preparation is designed to increase and deepen student's physical awareness, develop physical and vocal stamina, flexibility and strength, modify habitual modes of expression, expand vocal and physical range, and increase students' ability to respond expressively.  Typically offered Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Aware of dysfunctional and limiting vocal and physical patterns.
2.  Reduce tension, increase flexibility, improve balance and coordination, and expand expressive range.
3.  Develop a grounded, focused and dynamic theatrical presence.
4.  Integrate breath, voice and movement in solo and group performance.
5.  Create a personalized training program that prepares you for the challenges of the profession.




Prerequisites: 

 Undergraduate level  THTR 20100 Minimum Grade of D- [may be taken concurrently]
