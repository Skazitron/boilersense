EAPS 53400 - Tropical Meteorology

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Tropical climatology; stability, convection, clouds, and precipitation processes; horizontal and vertical motions; life cycle and energetics of tropical cyclones; large-scale tropical circulations; interactions between small-scale and large-scale circulations; general circulations of the tropics; results of tropical experiments; outlook for future experiments. Prior course work in synoptic meteorology labs and atmospheric physics is required.
