VM 21300 - Microbiology Clinical Mentorship - DL

Credit Hours: 0.50.  This Clinical Mentorship will require the student to practice several tasks under the supervision of a clinical mentor (veterinarian, credentialed veterinary technician, or microbiologist). This Clinical Mentorship focuses on the motor skills required to obtain samples, prepare specimens for examination, and perform technician-level diagnostic techniques. Permission of department required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
