EAPS 42200 - Atmospheric Dynamics I

Credit Hours: 3.00.  (AGRY 43200) A study of the general system of equations governing mass and momentum changes in the atmosphere; special horizontal wind representations, thermal wind relationships; circulation, vorticity, divergence, and vertical motion. Prior course work in atmospheric thermodynamics and differential equations is required.
