PHYS 32300 - Research With Big Data I

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course is to conduct physics research in a class-based environment. As such, we will perform authentic research, defined as work with the potential for results that are of interest to the scientific community. We will work with large data sets, such as those collected by survey telescopes, dark matter experiments, or detectors at the Large Hadron Collider. Typically offered Fall.
