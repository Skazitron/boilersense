HIST 47005 - Women And Health In America

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course examines the historical relationship between women and health by exploring a number of critical themes that have affected women's health in the United States. Typically offered Fall Spring.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Analyze the ways in which societal assumptions about gender, health and sickness, along with race and class, help to determine the course of treatment.
2.  Analyze the role of primary sources in contributing to our understanding of women's health.
