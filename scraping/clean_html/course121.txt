ENGL 20300 - Introduction To Research For Professional Writers

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Introduction to research sources and methods useful for professional writers, including electronic resources. Focus on collecting print and online information, interviewing, surveying, and conducting observations; and on evaluating, summarizing, analyzing, and reporting research.
