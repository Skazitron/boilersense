MUS 16200 - Class Piano And Musicianship II

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A continuation of MUS 16100 that include reading intermediate level piano pieces, sight reading, scales, transposition and adding chords to a melody. The musicianship component includes intermediate level aural skills, rhythmic and melodic dictation, intervals, chordal and harmonic dictation.
