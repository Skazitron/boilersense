CE 61400 - Statistical And Econometric Methods I

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Basic and advanced statistical and econometric methods as applied to engineering-related problems. Introduction to ordinary least squares regression, count-data models including Poisson and Negative binomial regressions and their extensions, simultaneous equations models, multinomial logit models, ordered probability models, joint discrete/continuous models, and hazard-based duration models. Prerequisites:  Stat 51100.
