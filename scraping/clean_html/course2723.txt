TLI 35580 - The Individual And Organizational Performance

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course develops an understanding of the principles and theoretical foundations of individual and organizational performance management. Emphasis is placed on the perspectives, methodology, and theoretical frameworks as well as applications for performance management from the perspective of human resources professionals. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Develop an understanding of the principles and theoretical foundations of individual and organizational performance management.
2.  Identify and analyze performance problems in an organizational system.
3.  Identify and evaluate interventions in performance improvement at different levels (individual, team/process and organizational).
4.  Understand human resource approaches to individual and organizational performance management.
5.  Examine various technologies for managing individual and organizational performance.
