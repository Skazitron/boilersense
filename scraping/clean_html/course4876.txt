CNIT 55800 - Bioinformatics Computing And Systems Integration

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This is a graduate-level course for students interested in the application of computational methods and information technology in the pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and life sciences arena. An overall understanding of information technology and systems is assumed, as well as an in-depth knowledge of one or more areas of information technology. Permission of instructor required.
