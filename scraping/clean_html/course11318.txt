NUR 67400 - Quality Initiatives, Leadership And Advanced Practice Nursing

Credit Hours: 2.00.  This course develops concepts of the guiding principles of quality, system and leadership initiatives in the Nurse Practitioner Role.  The students explore principles and techniques of health coaching, interprofessional team work, delegation and prioritization that can be used in various healthcare settings.  Potential quality initiatives will be identified and an improvement plan will be developed using various quality initiatives modalities in the healthcare system to improve quality care, safety and patient outcomes. Permission of department required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Examine the role of Advanced Practice Nurses in healthcare quality initiatives.
2.  Explore principles of leadership and quality improvement including patient safety. Human factors, patient coaching and healthcare costs in practice management settings.
3.  Utilize principles of interprofessional teamwork, delegation and prioritization in the development of a quality improvement plan.
4.  Assess the impact of QI initiatives on quality care, patient safety and healthcare outcomes.
