IE 57000 - Manufacturing Process Engineering

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Theories and applications of materials forming and removal processes in manufacturing, including product properties, process capabilities, processing equipment design, and economics. A systems approach to all aspects of manufacturing process engineering. Typically offered Fall Spring.
