PHPR 45000 - Introduction To Institutional Pharmacy Practice

Credit Hours: 1.00.  An overview of the delivery of pharmaceuticals and the provision of clinical and administrative services to patients in institutional settings is presented along with the various roles pharmacists play both within the pharmacy department and the institution.  Course content focuses on the provision of services and career development rather than on drug therapy.  The course features a series of presentations by practitioners from a variety of practice settings.  Enrollment is limited to Doctor of Pharmacy students or by consent of course coordinator. Typically offered Fall.
