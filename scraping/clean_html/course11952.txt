CE 52400 - Legal Aspects In Engineering Practice

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Legal principles and landmark cases relevant to engineering. Subjects covered include contracts, torts, agency, real property, environmental and labor laws, expert testimony, arbitration, patents and copyrights, sureties and ethics. Three evenings may be required.
