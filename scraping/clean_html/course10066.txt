HSCI 54801 - Advanced Industrial Hygiene Instrumentation Techniques Project

Credit Hours: 1.00.  This course covers sophisticated field and laboratory instrumentation and techniques, sampling methodology for organic vapors, dust, fibers, noise, and heat; calibration and validation techniques; emphasis on critical analysis of data. Permission of instructor required.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Understand the limitations of sampling and analysis in real world situations and learn how to deal with them.
2.  Collect, analyze interpret and summarize laboratory data into a well-written report.
3.  Appreciate the advantages as well as the difficulties of working together on a team.
