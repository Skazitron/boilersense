Learning Outcomes: 
1. Demonstrate knowledge of a range of genres, topics, and literary conventions from the post-Civil War period on. 
2. Discuss those genres, topics, and conventions in relation to their diverse historical, cultural, and aesthetic contexts. 
3. Produce well-reasoned written arguments about post-Civil War literature based on appropriate use of textual evidence.
