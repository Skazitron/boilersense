GS 11900 - Introduction To Academic Programs At Purdue

Credit Hours: 1.00.  To provide a comprehensive program describing each of the baccalaureate degree-granting schools at Purdue. The course is designed to assist students in the clarification of their degree objectives. Not open to students who have completed G S 11500. Typically offered Fall Spring.
