ANTH 20300 - Biological Bases Of Human Social Behavior

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course is an introduction to human social behavior from the perspective of biological anthropology, with special emphasis on human evolution and non-human primates. Topics include aggression, communication, learning, maturation, sexuality, and the evolution of social systems.
