AT 26200 - Basic Aircraft Powerplant Technology

Credit Hours: 4.00.  A study of the design, construction, and operating practices of aircraft reciprocating and small gas turbine engines. Laboratory exercises emphasize airworthiness evaluation, fault-isolation techniques, and standard service/maintenance practices.
