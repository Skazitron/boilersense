MET 40200 - Capstone Projects II

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This is the second of two courses in a capstone project sequence. Project management and system engineering methods are applied to solving an engineering problem. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Create a functional prototype.
2.  Weigh design alternatives for requirements, efficiency, reliability, and cost.
3.  Apply formal test procedures, including data analysis.
4.  Present project results orally and in writing.
