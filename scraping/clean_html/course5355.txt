ECE 69400 - Electrical And Computer Engineering Seminar

Credit Hours: 0.00.  Seminar presentations by representatives from industry, members of the faculty of the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering, and other staff and faculty of Purdue University. The presentations introduce the student to a wide variety of current topics relevant to the technical and career aspects of electrical and computer engineering. Technical topics span the entire spectrum of electrical and computer engineering. Career topics include the importance of interpersonal communications, opportunities beyond graduate school, interviewing techniques, and descriptions of non-typical jobs. Required of electrical and computer engineering graduate students at Purdue during one of their first two semesters in residence.
