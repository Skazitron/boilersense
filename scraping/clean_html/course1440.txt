MGMT 37000 - Real Estate Fundamentals

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course introduces the basic concepts, principles, and analytic methods applied in the field of real estate. The course covers topics including legal, financial, economic and marketing concepts related to real estate. The goal of the course is to expose students to the field of real estate and prepare them for more advanced real estate courses.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Recognize and understand what real property is.
2.  Describe titles to the conveyance of real estate.
3.  Review land economic principles as they relate to real estate values.
4.  Identify all quantitative issues required to complete effective real estate transactions.
5.  Discuss the aspects of city planning and zoning on real estate values.
6.  Understand basic methods for real estate valuation.
7.  Understand basic issues in real estate development.
