MGMT 41350 - Venture Capital And Entrepreneurial Finance

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course includes descriptions of markets for start-up businesses, venture capital financial structures, valuation of start-ups, role of venture capital in economic development and business growth strategies. Typically offered Fall Spring.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Evaluate start-up ventures and understand the markets for early stage businesses.
2.  Value these companies using Discounted Cash Flow models, markets multipliers and other industry-specific valuation methods.
