GREK 10100 - Ancient Greek Level I

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Introduction to the grammar of Attic Greek of the Classical period, and first forays into the reading of connected prose. Emphasis on accidence, syntax, and vocabulary building. Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Explain foundational Ancient Greek grammar and demonstrate familiarity with core vocabulary.
2.  Bring together their knowledge of Ancient Greek grammar and vocabulary, creating clear English translations by analyzing basic texts adapted from Ancient Greek literature.
3.  Apply this knowledge of grammar and vocabulary to writing simple sentences in Ancient Greek, and translating from English to Ancient Greek.
