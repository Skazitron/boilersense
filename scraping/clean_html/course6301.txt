GREK 20200 - Ancient Greek Level IV

Credit Hours: 3.00. Upper intermediate reading course in ancient Greek, designed to consolidate students' knowledge of grammar and syntax, broaden vocabulary, and develop precision and confidence in reading and translation.  Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Reinforce and extend skills as readers and translators by studying archaic, classical or post-classical Greek literature.
2.  Improve understanding of English through increased sensitivity to grammatical structures and stylistic choices and by extending vocabulary through etymological connections.
