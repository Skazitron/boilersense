GS 40400 - Horizons Advanced Career Preparation

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Horizons Advanced Career Preparation is designed to assist students in beginning their transition from college to the work force or graduate school. The emphasis of the course is placed on self-exploration, career search skills, graduate school preparation, professional conduct, and financial planning. Typically offered Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Understand self-authorship through evaluation of strengths and talents.
2   Navigate a process of personal exploration focused on future career objectives.
3.  Develop and refine resume or curriculum vitae.
4.  Enhance skills as they relate to ongoing job search or graduate school preparation.
5.  Educate navigating a professional setting.
6.  Develop financial literacy as it relates to budgeting, loans, and savings.
