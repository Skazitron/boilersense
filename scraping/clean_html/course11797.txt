CE 20300 - Principles And Practice Of Geomatics

Credit Hours: 4.00.  Basic surveying measurements and computations for engineering project control, mapping, and construction layout; theory of observational errors and error propagation; fundamental concepts of horizontal and vertical control systems; use of topographic maps and plan-profile sheets; computation of horizontal and vertical curves; introduction to computer tools used in Civil Engineering.
