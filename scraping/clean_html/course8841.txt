ECON 61500 - Mathematical Analysis For Economists

Credit Hours: 2.00 or 3.00.  Topics include constrained optimization, comparative statics, and elementary topics in mathematical analysis. Students should be familiar with calculus, linear algebra, basic probability, and have a grasp of microeconomic theory (such as the material covered in ECON 51100).
