ANTH 42800 - Field Methods In Archaeology

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 9.00.  Introduces basic field and laboratory methods in contemporary archaeology: methods of site survey, mapping, and excavation through the excavation of archaeological sites; the basics of archaeological data analysis and classification, and the computer-based analysis of archaeological data. Permission of instructor required.
