AGEC 61200 - Agricultural Production Economics I

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Economic theory and empirical research methods for management of natural resource based production systems. Topics include: analysis of response in crops and livestock production, resource allocation, economics of site specific management, commodity supply and input demand. Concurrent Prerequisite:  ECON 51100.
