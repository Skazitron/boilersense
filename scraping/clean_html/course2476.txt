BTNY 53500 - Plant Disease Management

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An introduction to the epidemiological basis of disease management strategies. The course includes concepts in botanical epidemiology, disease assessment and measurement, disease modeling, epidemics comparison, and a revision of the traditional plant disease control principles. This is an upper-level course and intended for students interested in plant pathology, agronomy, or closely related areas.
