EAPS 42100 - Atmospheric Thermodynamics

Credit Hours: 3.00.  (AGRY 43100) Structure and composition of the atmosphere. Thermodynamics of dry and moist air, including adiabatic and pseudo-adiabatic processes, hydrostatic stability, and air mass determination. Prior course work in introductory atmospheric science, second semester physics or thermodynamics is required.
