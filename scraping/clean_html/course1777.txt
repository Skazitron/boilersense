BCM 57200 - Construction Research Fundamentals

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Construction Research Fundamentals focuses on helping students to develop their thesis research through lecture and faculty guided individual or group activities. Using common research methodologies for construction research as a theme for discussion each week, students work together to build a better understanding of how to approach thesis research for their topic of interest and research topics chosen by other students in the class.
