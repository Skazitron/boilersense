AD 34800 - History Of Islamic Art

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Islamic art, one of the world's richest artistic traditions, is surveyed from its origins in the 7th century AD to the modern day.  All types of artistic media, from large to small-scale, are included and seen against the background of the Islamic religion and history. Offered on a two-year rotation.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Students will acquire a deep appreciation of the varied forms of Islamic art.
2.  Students will understand their historical development over several centuries, and will view them within the larger context of Islamic culture as a whole.
