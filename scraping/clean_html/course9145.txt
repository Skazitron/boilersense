VM 36500 - Laboratory Animal Management

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course will cover the principles of laboratory animal science for the technologist (veterinary and LATG). Concepts of facility management, animal health and diseases, regulatory issues, compliance, and instrumentation will be presented. Typically offered Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Gain a broader knowledge of facility design, facility management, regulatory compliance, disaster planning and fiscal management.
2.  Gain exposure to research protocol development, animal use alternatives, and the use of non-rodent models in research.
3.  Gain an in-depth look at rodent breeding colony management and animal health.
