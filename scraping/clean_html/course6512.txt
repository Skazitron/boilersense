CSR 35500 - Apparel Showcase

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Planning of annual apparel showcase of apparel original designs and artwork of Apparel Design majors. All juniors and seniors in the Apparel Design major work with co-leaders to plan the annual event. This course will be an avenue to work with special projects as requested by outside interest groups.

    1.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Work cooperatively as an organized group to plan fall and/or spring events for showcase of Apparel Design and Technology Program.
2.  Develop strategies for planning and producing a "special event/project".
3.  Prepare at least one "original design" outfit or garment to be modeled in the "special event" fall fashion show or provide service time to complete a special event/project.
4.  Develop strategies for planning and producing annual spring fashion show through organized committees.
5.  Develop and implement strategies to welcome and mentor freshman and sophomore Apparel Design and Technology students.
6.  Develop weekly plans for conducting committee work.
7.  Review and revise policies and procedures.
