ECE 62500 - Analysis Of Electromechanical Systems II

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Extension of ECE 525. Electric propulsion systems including presentation of cycloconverter and rectifier-inverter drive systems. Dynamic and steady-state analysis of machine performance with series controlled rectifiers in the stator or rotor phases. MMF space harmonic analysis. Prerequisite: ECE 62500.
