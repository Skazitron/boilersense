AD 43000 - Interior Design V

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Design of multi-use complex environments, emphasizing program and problem solving, facility planning, building systems, and materials technology. Application of alternative presentation technologies will be explored.

    0.000 OR     3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Understand and apply IBC code 2009 and ADAAG to an interior design project.
2.  Develop an advanced level of space planning strategies in the design of large corporate interior spaces.
3.  Develop an understanding of the need for an open and flexible approach to problem-solving skills in relation to programming and design processes relative to today's varied and constantly changing modes of life.
