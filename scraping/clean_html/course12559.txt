IET 11100 - Introduction To Manufacturing And Supply Chain Systems

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A survey of organizational units and their function within an enterprise, including a brief history of organizations. The course emphasizes a systems perspective and how decisions in one area impact the entire system. Topics include operations, financial, marketing, planning, quality control, process strategy, logistics, and safety. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Explain the purpose and function of various organizational units within an enterprise.
2.  Describe how the enterprise is a system of interrelated parts.
3.  Explain the impact of risk on the enterprise system.
4.  Critically analyze current processes to identify areas of improvement.
5.  Predict how a suggested change impacts the system.
