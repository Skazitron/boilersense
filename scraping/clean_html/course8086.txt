CPB 85700 - Veterinary Systemic Pathobiology

Credit Hours: 5.00.  Provides coverage of system specific pathologic terminology and characteristic pathologic responses of the various body systems to injury. This is followed by presentation of representative examples of diseases of various etiologies, including, but not limited to, infectious and noninfectious diseases.
