ENGT 40500 - Entrepreneurial Capstone I

Credit Hours: 3.00.  The skills needed to define, design and develop innovative commercial products appropriate in engineering technology are introduced and developed. Planning and design alternatives that meet cost, performance, and user-interface goals are emphasized. Project planning, scheduling, and management techniques are studies. Different design approaches are compared. Teamwork, global and societal concerns, and professional ethics are integrated into course projects. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
