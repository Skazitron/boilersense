ASAM 34000 - Contemporary Issues In Asian American Studies

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Overviews various issues in contemporary Asian American Studies through theoretical and literary readings combined with examinations of popular media. Topics examine the multiple intersections of race, ethnicity, class, gender, and sexuality.
