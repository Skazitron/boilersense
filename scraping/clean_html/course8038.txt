CPB 35200 - Parasitology For Veterinary Technicians

Credit Hours: 2.00.  Biology and life cycles of selected protozoan, helminth, and arthropod parasites of veterinary importance. Emphasis will be placed on laboratory procedures for the identification of common parasites of domestic and laboratory animals.
