ANTH 35800 - African Cultures

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An introduction to the diversity of African cultures emphasizing detailed studies of selected cultural groups. Ethnographic writings about African cultures are assessed in relation to general information about the continent, its people and the colonial experience.
