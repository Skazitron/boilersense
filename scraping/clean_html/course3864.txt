AT 40900 - Unmanned Aerial Systems Project Planning And Management

Credit Hours: 3.00.  UAS capstone course. Application of UAS technologies across multiple commercial type scenarios.  A demonstration of the cumulative knowledge and skills learned in previous UAS courses. Requires current FAA Part 107 Remote Pilot Certificate.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Demonstrate the application of safety and risk management principles to UAS operations.
2.  Examine the UAS entrepreneurial ecosystem and formulate a business prospectus based on current industry trends and regulatory policy.
3.  Assess technical data sources to evaluate system health, to include ground and flight testing, troubleshooting, maintenance and repair, system configuration and payload integration.
4.  Demonstrate the ability to plan, manage, and execute various UAS missions safely, legally, and efficiently.
