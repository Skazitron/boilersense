THTR 55000 - Advanced Scenery Technology

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A theoretical and practical study of theatrical scenery planning, construction, and stage use.  Topics will vary by semester and will include project management techniques, mechanical design, automation systems, show control systems, rigging, and safety issues. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring.
