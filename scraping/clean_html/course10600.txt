GER 40200 - German Level VIII

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Further advanced work on speaking, listening, reading, and writing abilities in German. Course materials will cover a variety of topics illustrated by film and other media, both print and nonprint. Conducted in German. Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Compose essays in German after reading authentic German articles and books.
2.  Discuss current events in German based on print and media sources from German-speaking countries.
3.  Formulate arguments during debate in German through group discussion.
4.  State their opinions in German on recordings, television shows, and texts.
5.  Describe information in German from graphs, charts, diagrams, and pictures.
