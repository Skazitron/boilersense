CHE 49500 - Chemical Engineering Study Abroad

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  Short term study abroad experience to expose chemical engineering undergraduate students to the academic and industrial environments across the globe. Each experience location play an important economic role and offer good examples of industrial and engineering development, as well as allow for interaction with Purdue alumni, and cultural aspects through immersion during their time abroad.
