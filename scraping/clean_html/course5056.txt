ECE 27000 - Introduction To Digital System Design

Credit Hours: 4.00.  An introduction to digital system design and hardware engineering, with an emphasis on practical design techniques and circuit implementation.

    0.000 OR     4.000 Credit hours

      West Lafayette


Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Analyze and design CMOS logic gates. 
2.  Analyze and design combinational logic circuits. 
3.  Analyze and design sequential logic circuits. 
4.  Analyze and design computer logic circuits. 
5.  Realize, test, and debug practical digital circuits.
