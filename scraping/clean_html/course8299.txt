ANTH 37800 - Archaeology And Cultural Anthropology Of Mesoamerica (Mexico, Belize And Guatemala)

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Overview of Mesoamerican peoples, cultures, and languages from the earliest periods to the rise of civilizations including Aztec, Maya, and Zapotec, and from the Spanish Conquest to the modern Indian communities of Mexico, Guatemala, and Belize.
