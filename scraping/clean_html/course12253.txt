EDCI 64800 - Curriculum In Career And Technical Education

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Overview of curriculum in career and technical education programs; business and industry training programs; curriculum planning, development, implementation, and evaluation; review and analysis of curriculum models. Typically offered Fall Spring.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Develop an understanding of curriculum and its relationship to other instructional phases in career and technical education and training.
2.  Provide an overview of curriculum processes and procedures in career and technical education and business and industry training programs.
3.  Review and analyze curriculum development research in career and technical education.
4.  Acquire skills in curriculum needs assessment, planning, implementation, and evaluation.
5.  Explore the theoretical, ethical, political, social, and economic factors related to
curriculum development in career and technical education and training.
6.  Gain experience in designing a curriculum plan and curriculum development.
