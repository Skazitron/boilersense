CHE 55000 - Optimization In Chemical Engineering

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Survey of the basic computational tools for solving nonlinear constrained and unconstrained optimization problems. Emphasis on methods applicable to problems arising in chemical plant design, process operations and scheduling, parameter estimation, and waste energy reutilization.
