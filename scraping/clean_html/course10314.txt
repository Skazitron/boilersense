BMS 81400 - Basic And Applied Pharmacology I

Credit Hours: 3.00.  BMS 81400 follows BMS 81300 and includes the study of drugs with actions related to the nervous system and the immune system at large, anticancer drugs, antiviral drugs, and drugs targeted to organ specific pathologies - e.g. cardiac, intestinal, pulmonary, and renal pathologies.
