MSE 25000 - Physical Properties In Engineering Systems

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Class connects math, science and engineering practice and applications.  Presents foundational aspects of engineering problem solving, use of computer math tools for engineering problem solving, basic engineering statics, dynamics and mechanics, group problem solving approaches, and introductory aspects of design and materials selection.
