FR 30100 - French Level V

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Continued development of French speaking, listening, reading, and writing abilities, using materials dealing primarily with everyday life and civilization in France and the other French-speaking countries from various sources (e.g., newspapers, magazines, TV, recent literature, etc.). Conducted primarily in French. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
