ANTH 31200 - The Archaeology Of Ancient Egypt And The Near East

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Selected topics on the archaeology of ancient Near Eastern cultures in Mesopotamia, the circum-Mediterranean area, Egypt, and the Nile Valley, emphasizing an anthropological interpretation of the political, social, religious, and economic systems that contributed to their development.
