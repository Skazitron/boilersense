PSY 68800 - Research Methods In Social Psychology

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course provides a broad and rigorous, graduate-level overview of contemporary research methodology in social psychology and personality. The course follows a discussion seminar format. Permission of department required. Typically offered Fall Spring.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Understand the specific rationale for using different methodological techniques to answer different kinds of questions.
2.  Learn to plan, implement, and analyze social psychology research, including traditional laboratory and other experimental procedures and designs.
