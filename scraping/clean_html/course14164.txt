AD 61100 - Advanced Web Design For Visual Communication Design

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Explores experimental interface design issues and surveys emerging and advanced theories of interface design for the Web.  Application of motion graphics design principles and design experimentation is part of the curriculum.  Prerequisite:  Admission to the M.F.A. program in Art and Design. Permission of department required.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Conduct web design research and present it.  
2.  Present and discuss aspects of web design trends in contemporary society.
3.  Design and develop web design prototypes.
4.  Critique web designs based on established criteria such as content design, graphic design, experience design, navigation design, and performance design.
