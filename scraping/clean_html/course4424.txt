REL 45200 - Systematic Theology

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An introduction to the nature, tasks, and methods of what Christians have called "systematic theology", historically conceived through an exploration of the writings of figures such as Augustine, Aquinas, Melancthon, John Calvin, Barth, and Paul Tillich.
