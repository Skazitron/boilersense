COM 10200 - Introduction To Communication Theory

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Overview of approaches treating communication as an individual, functional activity and as a social, structuring activity. Covers definitions and models of communication, core processes (interpretations, sending, receiving), structures (language, nonverbal behavior), and key contexts (personal, organizational, public).
