MGMT 41310 - Financial Data Analysis And Modeling

Credit Hours: 3.00.  In the business world today, finance professionals are going to be given opportunities to analyze and model data. The objective of MGMT 41310 is to give students opportunities to work with financial data sets to ask and answer real questions that businesses have. In this course, students will use several data analysis software packages including R.  Additionally, the course will include extensive application of Excel tools for Financial Modeling.
