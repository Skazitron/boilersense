MGMT 68000 - Introduction To Information Technology

Credit Hours: 2.00 (West Lafayette) 3.00 (Calumet, North Central).  An introduction to the capabilities, limitations, and applications of computers to the business environment. Addresses issues relating to computer hardware and software, data management, problem analysis, and other management information systems (MIS) topics. Students use the computer as programmers, as users of existing software systems, and in the role of managers within business decision-making contexts. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
