SPAN 34200 - Hispanic Literature II: Prose

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Reading of selected novels and short fiction from Spain and Spanish-speaking America in their historical and cultural contexts. Readings, discussion, and papers in Spanish. Typically offered Fall Spring.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Develop intermediate- to advanced-level oral communication skills in Spanish through classroom discussions, presentations, and group work.
2.  Expand and refine their competence to comprehend, analyze, and interpret texts in Spanish through integrated reading and discussion of authentic texts in Spanish.
3.  Expand and refine their written proficiency in Spanish by producing a variety of analytical essays and writing assignments in Spanish.



Prerequisites: 

 Undergraduate level  SPAN 24100 Minimum Grade of D- or Undergraduate level  SPAN S3600 Minimum Grade of D-
