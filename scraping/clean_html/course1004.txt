EAPS 52500 - Boundary Layer Meteorology

Credit Hours: 3.00.  (AGRY 53500) A study of the physical nature of the lowest layers of the atmosphere. The energy balance concept and the turbulent transfer of heat, momentum, and water vapor are discussed in detail. Some specific microclimates are studied in this context.
