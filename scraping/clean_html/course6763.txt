HIST 40500 - The French Revolution And Napoleon

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A study of revolutionary France from the fall of the ancient regime to the Congress of Vienna. Divided in emphasis between the period of the revolution and the era of Napoleon, the course stresses social, political, ideological, and institutional developments.
