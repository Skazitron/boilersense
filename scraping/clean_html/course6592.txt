HIST 21100 - The Global Field: World Soccer And Global History

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This is an introductory course to the history of soccer, on and off of the pitch. It studies the history of the game itself as well as the international economic, political, social, and cultural trends that shaped it.  Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Understand a major global phenomenon and its relationship with nations and societies around the world.
2.  Learn how to look critically at the same problem (soccer) from the perspective of more than one discipline.
3.  Develop communication, writing skills and critical thinking.
