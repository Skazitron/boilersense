THTR 36200 - Light Design

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A project course in utilizing the principles of design as they can convey an environment and its qualities to further dramatic action. Emphasis on the interrelationship among all aspects of production and how light becomes a building block toward total picturization. Offered in alternate years.
