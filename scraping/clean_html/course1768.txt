BCM 45701 - Construction Safety

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course will examine the impact of safety on the construction industry, including in-depth discussions on the application of the Occupational Safety & Health Administration (OSHA) Safety and Health Standards for the construction industry. The emphasis of this course is to provide training for job sited supervisory personnel. This course will also fulfill the requirements for the OSHA 30-Hour Card.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Identify the role of OSHA in the construction industry and examine current issues related to safety.
2.  Acquire knowledge of jobsite safety management and company safety risk management.
3.  Identify and evaluate jobsite hazards, including abatement strategies and procedures.
4.  Complete the requirements for an OSHA 30-hour card in Construction Safety & Health Administration.
