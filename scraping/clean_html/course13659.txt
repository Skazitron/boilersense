SPAN 20100 - Spanish Level III

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An intermediate Spanish course with emphasis on communicative skills (listening and speaking), literacy skills (reading and writing) and culture. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Demonstrate mastery of grammatical structure and syntax, formation of plural nouns, noun-adjective agreement, verb conjugation, through the application of the communicative pedagogy (teaching in context) in the use of the basic learning skills (reading, writing, speaking and listening) by:
2.  Discussing personal relations and academic studies.
3.  Giving and receiving directions to a destination and/or in order to complete a task.
4.  Writing short essays (maximum 250 words) on specified topics.
