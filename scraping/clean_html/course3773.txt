AT 10000 - Introduction To Aviation Technology

Credit Hours: 1.00.  An introduction to the Department of Aviation Technology and its curricula as well as an overview of the aviation industry and associated careers. Aviation safety practices and human factors issues are discussed. An overview of aviation regulations and the regulatory process is included.
