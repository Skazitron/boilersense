CLCS 38300 - The Roman Empire

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Course examines developments from the Augustan Settlement to the end of the Roman Empire (27BCE - 476CE), along with aspects of religious, social, sexual and material culture throughout the Mediterranean at that time.
