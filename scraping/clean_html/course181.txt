ENGL 30600 - Introduction To Professional Writing

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Development of skill in analyzing rhetorical situations in the workplace. Practice in planning, writing, evaluating, and revising a variety of documents typical of those used in the arts and industry. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Understand the basic rhetorical theories that underlie professional and technical writing.
2.  Explore issues and challenges in professional writing in a variety of professional and technical contexts.
3.  Produce critical writing on rhetorical issues in the field.
4.  Solve workplace or organizational problems using writing.
