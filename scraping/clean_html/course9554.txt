Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Learn about the ways that knowledge both shapes and is shaped by the world around it.
2.  Develop their skills in analysis and critical thinking while gaining a deeper knowledge of the history of ideas.  
3.  Work on interdisciplinary problem solving, interactive group work, and collective decision making.
