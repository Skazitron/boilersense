COM 63200 - Seminar: Special Topics In Mass Communication

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Intensive study of selected topics, varying from semester to semester, from the literature of mass communication. Topics may include institutional analysis, mass communication law, information diffusion, uses of mass communication, or other issues. Permission of instructor required.
