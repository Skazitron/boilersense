ECON 60800 - Macroeconomic Theory I

Credit Hours: 2.00 or 3.00.  An introduction to general equilibrium macroeconomic models in both deterministic and stochastic settings. Topics studied include: static neoclassical models, social planner problems, dynamic programming, growth and capital accumulation, stochastic economies, asset pricing, and overlapping generations models.
