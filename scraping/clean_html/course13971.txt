AD 26700 - Digital Imaging

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An introductory course in the creative generation and digital enhancement of photo-related imagery. Emphasis is on the development of technical and critical thinking skills, as well as fostering an awareness of pertinent theoretical issues in digital age.

    3.000 Credit hours

      West Lafayette


Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Gain knowledge of intermediate and advanced Photoshop.
2.  Gain an understanding of constructed image making in photography using Photoshop.
3.  Understand contemporary artists whose photographic works generated through computer manipulation.
4.  Gain knowledge of theoretical and historical concerns regarding post photography.
