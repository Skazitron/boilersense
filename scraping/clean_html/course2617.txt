CHM 62900 - Chromatographic Methods Of Analysis

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Principles underlying chemical separations are discussed, including mechanisms of selective transport and zone broadening. Relations are shown between chromatography and electrophoresis, as well as other separation methods. Current applications and current research activity in the field of chemical separations are included. Prerequisite: CHM 42400. Permission of department required.
