AT 31900 - Unmanned Aerial Systems Applications, Data And Documentation

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Focused on applying unmanned aerial technology to specific data gathering missions. Sensor types, mounting selection, and control will be demonstrated. Emphasis will be placed on sensor payloads, mission planning, and data acquisition. Post-flight data processing and analysis will be conducted using GIS and Remote Sensing software applications. All specifications and procedures will be documented in an industry-accepted format.
