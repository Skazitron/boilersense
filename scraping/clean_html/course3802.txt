AT 25200 - Aviation Projects

Credit Hours: 3.00. This course will introduce the students to group projects in collaboration with industrial partners in aviation. A new project will be conducted each semester. Students will participate in the development of project goals, conducting field research, and developing a final course document and presentation. Travel outside of the normal assigned classroom time might be required. Permission of Department required.
