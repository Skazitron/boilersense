ASL 10100 - American Sign Language I

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A basic introduction to American Sign Language. This course introduces students to the tools for mastering the grammar at a basic expressive and receptive level. Introduction to cultural and historical aspects of ASL and the deaf community.
