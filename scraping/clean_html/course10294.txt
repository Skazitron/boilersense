BMS 52800 - Avian Physiology

Credit Hours: 2.00.  (ANSC 53500) A study of the basic principles of physiology and functional anatomy of birds. Topics include the following systems: muscular, nervous, cardiovascular, respiratory, digestive, lymphoid, endocrine, and reproductive.
