AT 41101 - Aerospace Internship II

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Students will be placed in an external industry-sponsored position that exists either within the department or at a location specified by the sponsor. Students will function as interns as they develop and manage business and/or operational processes within these units.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Solicit and gain a position as an intern in a selected external business or research unit.
2.  Successfully complete the requirements of the externship.
3.  Develop and deliver a presentation on the work accomplished during the externship.
