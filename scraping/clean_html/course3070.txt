ASL 28000 - American Deaf Community: Language, Culture, And Society

Credit Hours: 3.00.  The linguistic, cultural, and societal context of the deaf community in America. Both historical and contemporary aspects of deaf identity will be included, with an emphasis on the central role that ASL plays in the lives of deaf individuals.
