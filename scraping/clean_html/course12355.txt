AGEC 64400 - International Agricultural Trade

Credit Hours: 3.00.  International trade theory relevant to understanding world agricultural trade and the problems of agriculture in an open economy. Models for analyzing the effects of trade policy and market conditions on the agricultural sector. Welfare analysis of restrictions on trade.  Prerequisite:  ECON 51100, 51200.
