PHIL 49000 - Advanced Topics In Philosophy

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  An advanced study of a significant topic in philosophy. Typically offered Fall Spring.

    0.000 TO     3.000 Credit hours

Repeatable for Additional Credit: Yes - May be repeated up to 1 times


Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Demonstrate familiarity with several advanced problems of philosophy.
2.  Develop the ability to present, explain, defend and critically evaluate advanced philosophical ideas, theories, and perspectives, in oral and written communication.
3.  Develop the ability to locate, interpret, understand and evaluate philosophical arguments encountered in complex philosophical texts within the contexts and traditions that inform those arguments and texts.
4.  Develop critical thinking and writing skills.
