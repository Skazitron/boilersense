Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Describe the contributions and impact of manufacturing in global, national, and regional economies and how these are being impacted by transformations in Smart Manufacturing.
2.  Understand the future of manufacturing, SMART Factories, the power of the Cloud, and new ways of impacting business in a networked manufacturing economy through digital transformation.
3.  Understand the manufacturing ecosystem for rendering of digital services for the improvement and innovation with customers, suppliers, partners, distributors and other stakeholders. 
4.  Understand the fundamentals of Cyber Physical Systems in regards to production systems and management, organizational goals, and business benefits.
5.  Understand industrial Internet of Things and analytics to enable real-time management and optimization of inventory in the manufacturing supply chain.
