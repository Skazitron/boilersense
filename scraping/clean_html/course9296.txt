ECET 32100 - Introduction To Nanotechnology

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course explores the downsizing of electronic devices and introduces the field of nanotechnology. The downscaling to nanoelectronics, properties of nanomaterials and the tools of nanotechnology are studied. Interdisciplinary research and commercial applications are reviewed. The course involves participation in research seminars and hands-on laboratory experiences with nanotechnology instrumentation.
