MFET 34400 - Automated Manufacturing Processes

Credit Hours: 3.00. Shop floor components of computer-integrated manufacturing are explored. Emphasis is focused on current applications and programming practices of various computer automated manufacturing processes and technologies. Topics include CAD/CAM integration, computer-assisted numerical control programming for 2-1/2 and 3 axis contouring, and CNC program verification. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
