MGMT 43907 - Advanced Topics In Real Estate

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course covers selected and timely topics in real estate that are not covered in other regular courses. The topics are determined by the instructor offering the course. The instructor may also include advanced analytical techniques for real estate investment decisions, real estate valuation, and other analyses. Cases and research reports may be used in this course.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Recognize and understand issues and their impacts on the real estate industry.
2.  Assess the impacts of certain issues on a company.
3.  Understand and apply advanced analytical methods in real estate decisions.
4.  Develop a life-long learning ability for future issues in real estate.
