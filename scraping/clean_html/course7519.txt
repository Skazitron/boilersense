BIOL 39600 - Premedical Planning Seminar

Credit Hours: 0.00.  This course is designed for sophomore and junior students who are planning to attend medical school. The course offers information and advice on the MCAT, the application process, the personal statement, the interview, and letters of recommendation. Students in the course will also formulate an alternative career plan. The course meets the first 10 weeks of the semester.
