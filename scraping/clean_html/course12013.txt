CE 67600 - Behavior Of Reinforced Concrete Members

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Studies of the behavior and strength of reinforced concrete members, behavior of beam-columns, deflections, shear, bond, and cracking. Review of research and pertinent literature. Emphasis is placed on the background, use, and limitations of present design specifications.  Prerequisite: CE 57600.
