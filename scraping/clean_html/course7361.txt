HORT 11000 - Opportunities In Horticulture

Credit Hours: 1.00.  A survey of the field of horticulture, with emphasis on horticultural information and career opportunities. This course will utilize a lecture format with a combination of presentations by the instructor and guest speakers with expertise and experience in specialized areas of horticulture. Typically offered Spring.

    1.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Identify important issues and opportunities in the horticulture industry.
2.  Identify different career paths in the field of horticulture.
3.  Utilize the department, college and university job placement facilities to find summer jobs, internships, and permanent job opportunities.
4.  Identify individuals who can assist you in career counseling and job placement.
5.  Experience some the many great activities and opportunities that Purdue has to offer.
