EDCI 63700 - The Teaching Of Mathematics: Insights And Issues

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Examines research related to mathematics teaching, including research on characteristics of mathematics teachers, the features of effective mathematics teaching, and the professional preparation and development of mathematics teachers.  Prerequisite: Master's student standing or higher and enrolled in Mathematics Education. Typically offered Spring.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Develop perspectives on and communicate orally and in writing various theories or theoretical frameworks related to teaching mathematics. 
2.  Investigate and communicate orally and in writing theoretical and empirical foundations of the research literature related to mathematics teaching, teacher education, and/or professional development. 
3.  Critically reflect on and synthesize seminal and current research in mathematics teaching, teacher education, and/or professional development.
