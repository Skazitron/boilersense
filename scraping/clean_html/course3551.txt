COM 49000 - Internship In Communication

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  Experiential, supervised training in public relations, journalism, telecommunication, oral interpretation, speech education, organizational communication, or public communication. Usually given in junior or senior year. Usually gives two credits per internship experience. Permission of instructor required.
