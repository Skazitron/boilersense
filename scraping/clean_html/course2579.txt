CHM 44400 - Cosmochemistry

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Stellar evolution, nucleosyntheses, and chemical abundances. Origin, composition, and structure of the solar system and objects in it. Cosmochronology with particular emphasis on meteorites. Emphasis upon recent developments in planetology. Offered in alternate years.
