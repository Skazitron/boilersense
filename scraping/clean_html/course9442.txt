SFT 47100 - National Security, Leadership Responsibilities, And Commissioning Preparation I

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course examines security challenges around the world and identifies how the Space Force and the other services are organized and employed in response to those challenges. Topics include fundamental aspects of command, the military justice system, and effective communication.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Describe the basic process of developing national security policy.
2.  Explain the Space Force warfighting domains and their interrelationships.
3.  Discuss current domestic and international issues affecting the military profession
4.  Explain the purpose and legal requirements of the Uniform Code of Military Justice.
5.  Identify the responsibilities, authorities, and functions of a Space Force commander.
