AT 28500 - ATC Procedures and Weather

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An introduction to the technical procedures involved in the development and operation of the air traffic control system. Instrument procedures and design will be covered. Weather theory as pertaining to ATC will be discussed, along with observation and dissemination systems used by the FAA and NWS.
