AD 33000 - Interior Design III

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Study of advanced space-planning criteria and selection of furnishings, equipment, materials, and finishes. Design considerations for special populations (e.g., disabled, aged, low-income) will be explored. Must pass mandatory portfolio review. Prerequisites:  Passing mandatory portfolio review.
