EDST 51400 - Economics Of Education

Credit Hours: 3.00.   A general course in financing education, including current economic theories, sources of revenues, and trends in fiscal structure and operations in education.

    0.000 OR     3.000 Credit hours

      West Lafayette


Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Develop and understand the goals of public education, why states invest in education, and the legal basis for financing public education.
2.  Develop and understand the sources of revenues for public education and the approaches used to raise and distribute funds for school district operations.
3.  Develop and understand the concepts of equity and adequacy and how these ideas are reflected in current state education policy and educational leadership practices.
4.  Develop and understand budgeting strategies and procedures used by educational leaders to influence student achievement outcomes and realize other school-based goals.
