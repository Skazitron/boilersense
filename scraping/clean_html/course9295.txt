ECET 31800 - Foundations Of Audio Electronics

Credit Hours: 3.00.  The design, fabrication, and testing of the electronics that comprise audio signal transduction, transmission, processing, amplification and delivery to a loudspeaker are studied at an introductory level. Computer-aided analysis of circuits is used.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Quantify and measure sound and signal levels in terms of dB.
2.  Convert  dB, crest factor, THD, and frequency cut-off specifications into circuit design parameters.
3.  Interface circuitry to microphones and loud speakers.
4.  Design, build, and test a small audio system containing differential transmitters, differential amplifiers, simple active filters summers/mixers, class AB and class D 100 W audio amplifiers.
