AAS 27700 - African American Popular Culture

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course focuses on the production of popular culture through African-based and non African-based traditions among African Americans in the United States.  Pop culture forms covered include film, theatre, visual arts, literature, dance, sports, music and religious traditions.
