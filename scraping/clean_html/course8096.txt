CPB 88502 - Microbiology I

Credit Hours: 1.00.  This is one of three concurrent courses (CPB 88501, CPB 88502, CPB 88503) in which senior veterinary students will be enrolled. This course is designed for senior veterinary students to gain additional training in diagnostic microbiology (bacteriology, mycology, virology and molecular diagnostics) in relation to clinical diseases. Students will actively participate in self study and discussion of selected diagnostic microbiology cases as well as hands-on logistically approached laboratory procedures for culture and isolation of microbial agents. The students are expected to be proficient and competent in veterinary diagnostic microbiology at the end of rotation. Total clinic hours 43.
