HK 41100 - Clinical Applications Of Health/Fitness Concepts III

Credit Hours: 2.00.  Under the supervision of the program coordinator, students will demonstrate the ability to apply specific skills, competencies, and applications of basic/advanced exercise principles in a professional setting.  Required to be taken fall and spring.  Permission of department required.  Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Execute professional responsibilities for each clinical site as assigned (clinical, corporate, community, academic/research).
2.  Mentor lower-level undergraduate students who are assisting in evaluating clients and providing clients feedback on various performance measures.
3.  Develop and implement projects associated with each clinical experience as assigned.
4.  Identify his/her own strengths and weaknesses regarding their clinical responsibilities.
