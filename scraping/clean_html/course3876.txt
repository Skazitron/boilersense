AT 43500 - Supervised  Instructional Experience

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 4.00.  This course provides students with experience as instructors in an aviation classroom or laboratory environment under the supervision of faculty. In addition to the supervisory skills gained, students will become knowledgeable in task planning and communication techniques. Permission of department required.
