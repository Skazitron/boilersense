CHNS 24100 - Introduction To The Study Of Chinese Literature

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Class reading and discussion of selected Chinese poetry, prose, and drama; introduction to the methods of literary criticism, applied to the reading of Chinese literary discourse. Texts, discussion, and written assignments primarily in Chinese. Permission of instructor required.
