CPB 63500 - Advanced Veterinary Diagnostic Microbiology

Credit Hours: 0.00 to 8.00.  Veterinary clinical and diagnostic microbiology training. Prerequisite: DVM degree/ or Equivalent. Permission of instructor required.

    0.000 TO     8.000 Credit hours

      West Lafayette

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Able to identify appropriate specimens and tests to diagnose infectious disease, and ability to communicate those to clients.
2.  Able to interpret lab results and communicate results and appropriate recommendations to clients such as further testing, treatment, and management.
