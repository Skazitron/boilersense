DANC 35000 - Dance For The Camera

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An introduction to technical and aesthetic techniques to create screendance or dance film, with an emphasis on camera use, editing techniques, development and production of choreographic projects designed specifically for film format.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Illustrate how to use a camera and capture dance footage by filming two screendance projects.
2.  Perform basic skills in non-linear digital video editing with attention to motion and action by applying this editing technique to the post production process in screendance projects.
3.  Identify screendance and express its value and meaning as both a creator and observer by analyzing professional screendance works through class discussions and written assignments.
4.  Demonstrate personal expression and artistry through choreographing dance film projects.
