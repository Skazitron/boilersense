CPB 22500 - Parasitology for Veterinary Technicians I - DL

Credit Hours: 1.00.  This course is the first of two courses that provide basic instruction in the concepts of parasitology for the veterinary technician student. This course will place an emphasis on the basic information needed to accurately identify parasites commonly encountered in veterinary medicine. Emphasis will be placed on life cycles and the zoonotic aspects of the parasites.
