MGMT 61200 - Financial Management III

Credit Hours: 2.00 to 4.00.  (West Lafayette) 3.00 (Calumet, North Central) Further treatment of topics in the financial management of nonfinancial corporations, from the viewpoint of the internal financial officer. Topics include further coverage of cost of capital and financial planning, as well as cash management, working capital management, short-term financing, advanced capital budgeting, and leasing. Emphasis on applications. Continuation of MGMT 611 with additional depth and topic coverage. Prerequisite: MGMT 61000.
