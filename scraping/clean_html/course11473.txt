PSY 33099 - Cooperative Education Seminar II

Credit Hours: 1.00 or 2.00.  This seminar course is optional for Co-Op students when they are on-campus.  In this course, students share their work experiences, receive feedback from the instructor and seminar participants, and continue their professional development. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

    1.000 OR     2.000 Credit hours

Repeatable for Additional Credit: Yes - May be repeated for a maximum of 6 credits


Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Develop oral and written presentation skills.
2.  Expand their knowledge of professions in Psychological Sciences.
3.  Learn from the experiences of other participants so that they may apply their knowledge to their situation.



Prerequisites: 

 Undergraduate level  PSY 38299 Minimum Grade of S or Undergraduate level  PSY 38299 Minimum Grade of P
