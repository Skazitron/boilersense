AGR 15000 - Woman In Agriculture I

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Intended to expose first-year students to resources and their development of a network of students across campus. The course will support their transition to Purdue through a sense of community and increased engagement, and provide students access to faculty across the College of Agriculture in a small group setting. Activities include seminars to expose students to college and university resources, hands-on learning activity for team building, study halls, and group meals. Must be enrolled in the Women of Ag Learning Community.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Learn about resources and tools that are available to you as a Purdue Student.
2.  Build a rapport with female professors and staff in the College of Agriculture.
3.  Introduce you to other students in the College to build a support network.
4.  Learn a method to strengthen your confidence in engaging faculty and staff around campus.
5.  Connect you with a network of professionals in the agriculture industry.
