CGT 24700 - Visual Effects - Particles And Procedural Effects

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course focuses on simulating and rendering particles. Special emphasis is placed on volumes, sparks, dust, and smoke effects. Techniques for minimizing render times and meeting production requirements are explored. Emphasis is placed on the use of production tools and techniques to create aesthetically pleasing graphics.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Create efficient particle simulations.
2.  Render particle simulations properly.
3.  Apply approved mathematical formulas to create simulations for entertainment graphics.
4.  Optimize simulations based on time and production project parameters.
5.  Implement common visual effects.
