ENE 49800 - Undergraduate Research In Engineering Education

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  Individual research projects for students with the approval of their academic advisors.  Requires prior approval of, and arrangement with, a faculty research advisor in the Department of Engineering Education.  Written and oral final reports are required. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
