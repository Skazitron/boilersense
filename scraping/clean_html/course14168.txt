AD 61500 - Social Issues In Industrial Design

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A study of mass-production materials and processes used by designers. Students will focus on the limitations and benefits of various manufacturing methods. Special attention will be given to injection molding, casting, and stamping.  Prerequisite:  Admission to the M.F.A. program in Art and Design.
