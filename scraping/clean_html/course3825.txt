AT 31000 - Aerospace Enterprise Organization

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course will provide an understanding of ways in which aerospace enterprises can be structured and organized. A thorough examination of existing and theoretical organizational structures will be conducted. Legal, tax and managerial implications of business organizations will be investigated. Students will create an actual business and manage it throughout the course.

Learning Outcomes:

2.  Analyze tax and legal implications of choices related to business organization.
3.  Create a legal business entity and manage it for the duration of the course.
