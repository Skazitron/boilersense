COM 69500 - Curricular Practical Training

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  Internship providing practical field experience under professional supervision in selected situations related to the student's area of specialization. Good standing in the graduate program of the Department of Communication and an approved plan of study including the internship. Permission of instructor required.
