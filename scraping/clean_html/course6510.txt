CSR 34200 - Personal Finance

Credit Hours: 3.00.  The class covers a comprehensive discussion of investments, consumer credit, insurance and retirement issues. The goal is to show how these components are interconnected in order to create a complete picture of financial health of an individual. CTL:IPO 1811 Personal Finance.
