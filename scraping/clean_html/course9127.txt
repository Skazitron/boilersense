VM 22600 - Laboratory Animal Clinical Mentorship -DL

Credit Hours: 1.00.  This clinical mentorship focuses on the motor skills involved with the basic handling, restraint, administration of medication and nursing care of laboratory animals.  Permission of department required.  Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

    1.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Perform the basics of handling and restraint of rabbits and rodents.
2.  Perform the determination of sex of rabbits and rodents.
3.  Perform the basics of drug and medication administration in rabbits and rodents.
4.  Perform the basics of blood collection in rabbits and rodents.
