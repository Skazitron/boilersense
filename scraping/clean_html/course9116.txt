VM 21100 - Food Animal and Equine Anesthesia-Clinical Mentorship-DL

Credit Hours: 1.00.  This mentorship focuses on the principles of large animal anesthesia from induction and maintenance to recovery. Students will be required to complete projects and participate in discussions related to large animal anesthesia cases. Permission of department required.

    1.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Demonstrate knowledge of history and physical exam pertinent to large animal anesthesia.
2.  Discuss large animal patient care before, during and after anesthesia.
3.  Demonstrate proper record keeping for patients under anesthesia.
4.  Discuss large animal anesthesia cases related to aspects of induction, maintenance and patient monitoring, and recovery from anesthesia.
