ECE 36200 - Microprocessor Systems And Interfacing

Credit Hours: 4.00.  An introduction to basic computer organization, microprocessor instruction sets, assembly language programming, and microcontroller peripherals.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Show ability to program a microcontroller to perform various tasks. 
2.  Show an ability to interface a microcontroller to various devices. 
3.  Show an ability to effectively utilize microcontroller peripheral. 
4.  Show an ability to design and implement a microcontroller-based embedded system.
