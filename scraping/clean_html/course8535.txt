CGT 44600 - Post-Production And Special Effects For Computer Animation

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A variety of commercial applications of technical animation and spatial graphics are analyzed and produced, with special emphasis upon client development, design, organization, scripting, storyboarding, technical production, management, and evaluation.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Describe the business model of film and animation production.
2.  Understand script development and analysis as they relate to production.
3.  Demonstrate image acquisition in the production environment.
4.  Demonstrate an understanding of final finishing and distribution processes.
