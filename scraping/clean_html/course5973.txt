ENGR 37399 - Professional Practice Flex Co-Op III

Credit Hours: 0.00.  Co-Op is an academic program with oversight provided by assigned Faculty Coordinators who serve as the Instructor for participating students.  In the Flex Co-Op program, students must work for their first employer for a minimum of two work sessions, and then have the option to continue for one-to-three additional work sessions; or, after completing two work sessions with the first employer, the student may elect to start with a second employer.  If the student elects to change employers, they must notify their first employer and their Faculty Coordinator in writing at the conclusion of the second work session. The student must commit to work for the second Co-Op employer for a minimum of two work sessions. Permission of Instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
