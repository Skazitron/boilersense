BAND 44100 - Renaissance Ensemble

Credit Hours: 2.00.  A survey of renaissance and baroque ensemble literature to be played on appropriate instruments. Reproductions of renaissance and baroque wind, stringed, and brass instruments are available for use in the Department of Bands.
