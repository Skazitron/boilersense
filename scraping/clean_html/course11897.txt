CE 44200 - Introduction To Hydrology

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Description, measurement, and analysis of hydrologic processes: precipitation, evapotranspiration, infiltration, and runoff. Hydrograph analysis: unit and synthetic unit hydrographs and flood routing. Statistical analysis of hydrologic data. Fundamentals, budget, and yield analysis of groundwater flows; well hydraulics. Case studies illustrating the application of principles in both surface and groundwater flows.
