AGEC 61700 - Applied Welfare And Environmental Economics

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Theory of welfare economics relevant to understanding foundations of benefit-cost analysis and concepts for policy design in areas such as environment, resources, and trade. Applied projects to be selected and analyzed by students. Prerequisite: AGEC 60400 and ECON 60700, or Consent of Instructor.
