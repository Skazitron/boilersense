MUS 35100 - Studio Piano

Credit Hours: 2.00.  Students will study and perform selected piano or digital keyboard literature with the focus on improving their technical and interpretive performance skills. Students will also learn about particular historical, analytical or technical issues related to work(s) studied.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Demonstrate improved music making skills in the areas of piano or electronic keyboard performance that will include an understanding of the following: music style and interpretation, scales, arpeggios and other musical patterns, selected musical repertoire, improved finger dexterity and technical facility.
