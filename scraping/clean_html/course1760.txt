BCM 36000 - Residential Construction

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A study of the best building practices in residential construction, based upon green building standards, energy star, and other national programs. Course content includes partial fulfillment of the requirements for the certified green professional designation (CGP) from the National Association of Home Builders.
