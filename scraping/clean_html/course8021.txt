SLHS 67900 - Advanced Clinical Practice In Audiology

Credit Hours: 4.00.  An advanced-level clinical practicum in audiology. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

    4.000 Credit hours


Syllabus Available

Levels: Graduate, Professional, Undergraduate 

Schedule Types: Clinic, Lecture 


Offered By: College of Health & Human Sci 

Department: Speech, Lang and Hearing Sci 


Course Attributes: Full-Time Privileges, Practicum 


May be offered at any of the following campuses:      

      West Lafayette


Repeatable for Additional Credit: Yes - May be repeated an unlimited number of times


Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Documented clinical experiences that cover the depth and breadth of the scope of practice of audiology.
2.  Meet the knowledge and skills associated with certification standards of the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association.
