ENGL 38700 - History Of Film Since 1950

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A survey of narrative, experimental, and documentary styles of feature films from the mid-twentieth century to the present. Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Demonstrate knowledge of a range of topics and conventions related to films from the mid-twentieth century. 
2.  Identify and articulate connections among these topics and conventions as they relate to their diverse historical, cultural, and aesthetic contexts.
3.  Produce well-reasoned written arguments about post-1950 films based on appropriate use of evidence.
