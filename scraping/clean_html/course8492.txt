CGT 33300 - Modeling For Entertainment Graphics

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course offers students a chance to comprehensively explore Polygon Modeling, with consideration of how choices made in modeling can affect all other aspects of the animation pipeline.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Evaluate polygon models based on topology and polygon budget.
2.  Build polygon models appropriate for level of detail sculpting and detailing.
3.  Unwrap polygon models with minimal UV shells, maximizing texture space.
4.  Texture and detail polygon models with appropriate shaders, achieving a desired result.
5.  Render polygon models in a format appropriate for demo reel review.
6.  Correct topological errors in conversion from NURBS to Polygons.
7.  Identify challenges in conversion of two-dimensional drawings to three-dimensional models.
