BME 58100 - Fundamentals Of MEMS And Micro-Integrated Systems

Credit Hours: 3.00.  (ECE 52600)  Key topics in micro-electro-mechanical systems (MEMS) and biological micro-integrated systems; properties of materials for MEMS; microelectronic process modules for design and fabrication. Students will prepare a project report on the design of a biomedical MEMS-based micro-integrated system.
