CSR 42100 - Apparel Design III

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Creation of apparel product line or collection from concept to consumer. Application of principles of apparel design from rendering, technical sketches, pattern design, pattern grading, marker making, and construction of several apparel garments from the line or collection.
