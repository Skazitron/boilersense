AT 26502 - Aircraft Electrical Systems

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An in-depth study of aircraft electrical components, both individually and as systems in the aircraft. DC electrical circuits, series and parallel circuits, magnetism, and electromagnetism are covered, as are DC power systems, schematics, and aircraft wiring practices. Aircraft charging systems, main power distribution, solid state electronics, batteries, and motors are also discussed.

Learning Outcomes: 
1. Develop an understanding of electrical principles as they relate to aircraft system operation.
2. Construct, analyze, and isolate faults in circuits.
3. Demonstrate troubleshooting procedures for direct currently electrical aircraft components and power distribution circuits.
