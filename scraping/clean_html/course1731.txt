ILS 59000 - Independent Study In Information Sciences

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  Intensive study on specific topics in information or data science that are not otherwise covered by courses currently offered at Purdue University. Plan of study and assessment is agreed upon by faculty and student before registration. Permission of department required. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
