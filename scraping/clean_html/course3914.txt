AT 50700 - Quantitative Research Methodologies In Transportation

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course provides an introduction to business statistics with a specific focus on data analyses and managerial decision making in the transportation industry. Topics include descriptive statistics, probability models, estimation, hypothesis testing, and regression analysis. Students use software to perform their own analyses. Permission of instructor required.
