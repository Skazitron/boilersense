AAE 53900 - Advanced Rocket Propulsion

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Generalized internal compressible and incompressible flows. Thermochemistry and heat transfer in rocket applications. Advanced topics in solid rocket motor performance and internal ballistics. Liquid rocket engine cycle analysis and turbopump design. Combustion of liquid and solid propellants. Hybrid rockets and thermal-nuclear engines.
