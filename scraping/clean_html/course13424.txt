AAS 35900 - Black Women Writers

Credit Hours: 3.00.  (ENGL 35900)  This course introduces students to the rich and varied literary texts produced by black women writers.  Literary analysis, along with a consideration of historical, cultural, gender, and racial contexts will be emphasized.
