AFT 23100 - Air Force ROTC Leadership Laboratory III

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Cadets experiment with and develop leadership/management skills.  Study of Air Force customs and courtesies, drill and ceremonies, and giving military commands; instructing, correcting, and evaluating the preceding skills, and study the Air Force officer environment.
