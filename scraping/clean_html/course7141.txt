HTM 54100 - Advanced Hospitality Accounting And Finance Systems

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Specialized accounting and finance systems as related to the hospitality industries. Applications of budgeting and pricing models, performance evaluations and incentives systems, and short-term asset management are discussed, as well as other current subjects. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Interpret and analyze financial statements.
2.  Calculate present value and future value of multiple cash flows.
3.  Value assets based on the discounted cash flow method.
4.  Utilize capital budgeting techniques.
5.  Understand the concepts of risk, return, and cost of capital.
6.  Describe capital structure theories.
7.  Describe the relationship between economy and hospitality businesses.
8.  Explain the concept of diversification.
9.  Calculate the present value of a franchise and a leasing contract.
