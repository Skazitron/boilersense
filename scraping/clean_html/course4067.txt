NRES 12100 - Stepping Stones To Environmental Science

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Intended to expose first-year students to emerging environmental problems and innovative solutions, to support their transition to Purdue through a sense of community and increased engagement, and to provide students access to faculty in the environmental sciences in a small group setting. Activities include field trips, hands-on learning, study halls, and meals together. Enrollment is restricted to members of the environmental science learning community. Department permission required. Typically offered Fall.
