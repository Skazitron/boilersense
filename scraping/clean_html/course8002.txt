SLHS 56300 - Pediatric Audiology

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Examination of protocols for the identification and evaluation of infants and children with hearing loss, using behavioral, physiologic, and electro-physiologic assessment methods. Course content places an emphasis on clinical decision-making and problem-solving skills.
