PSY 43200 - Social Psychology In Film

Credit Hours: 3.00. This course examines theory and research in experimental social psychology and uses popular films to assist in provoking thought and analysis of the theory and research. Typically offered Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Correctly interpret research findings from experimental social psychology.
2.  Apply these findings to various films.
3.  Formulate written ideas about films that are grounded in social psychological theory.
