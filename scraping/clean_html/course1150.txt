AAE 55500 - Mechanics Of Composite Materials

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Current and potential applications of composite materials. Review of elasticity of anisotropic solids. Methods for determining mechanical properties of heterogeneous materials. Static and dynamic analyses of laminated composites. Fracture and fatigue of laminates. Reliability, testing, and design of composites.
