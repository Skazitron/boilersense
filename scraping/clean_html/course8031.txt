CPB 22501 - Parasitology For Veterinary Technicians I - DL

Credit Hours: 2.00.  This course provides basic instruction in the concepts of parasitology to the veterinary technician student. Emphasis will be on the basic information needed to accurately identify parasites commonly encountered in veterinary medicine. Emphasis will also be placed on life cycles and zoonotic aspects of the parasites covered.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Identify parasite diagnostic stages.
2.  Describe the life cycles, transmission, basic pathogenesis, zoonotic aspects, adult parasite predilection site, clinical manifestations and parasitic control methods for parasites.
3.  Apply appropriate public health information to clients regarding the common parasites of domestic animals.
