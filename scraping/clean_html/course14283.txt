CMPL 33000 - International Cinema

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course is designed to develop students seeing, reading, speaking writing and critical thinking skills, and especially to improve visual competency, awareness of visual culture, and knowledge of selected international cinema. All films will be show with English subtitles.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Explain and assess different ways of seeing as an introduction to understanding visual theory.
2.  Demonstrate understanding of the concepts and techniques of cinematography, processing, and reading images and film.
3.  Recognize, select, and justify choices for seeing and interpreting the visual.
4.  Identify and analyze culturally-conditioned references.
