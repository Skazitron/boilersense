HK 13500 - Introduction To Health And Kinesiology

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Overviews the disciplines of health and kinesiology. Students explore skills and academic interests in theses areas. Describes career opportunities and options for graduate study. Provides strategies for professional and portfolio development. Students complete five hours of community service per semester. Permission of instructor required.
