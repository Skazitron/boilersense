AGEC 42100 - Advanced Commodity Marketing

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Application of commodity marketing principles to grain, livestock, and other commodity sectors. Applications include hedging, speculation, risk management, and fundamental and technical price analysis. Examination and testing of pricing strategies and the development of commodity marketing plans.
