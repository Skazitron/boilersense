COM 65500 - Health Advocacy

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course considers how federal, state, and local policy influence health status and health improvement. Through this course students gain an understanding of the role of health communication campaigns in health advocacy efforts. To engage their understanding, students will apply an advocacy campaign model to address a relevant health issue.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Understand of the role of health communication campaigns in health advocacy efforts.
2.  Apply an advocacy campaign model to address relevant health issues.
3.  Plan for a needs assessment.
4.  Understand how health policy is enacted at different levels of government.
5.  Conduct formative research.
