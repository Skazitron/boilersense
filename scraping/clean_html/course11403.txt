ASEC 59500 - Internship In Agricultural And Extension Education

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 10.00.  A special course designed to provide practical field experience under professional supervision in selected situation related to the student's area of specialization. Amount of credit to be determined by the nature and extent of the assignment. To be arranged with the instructor of departmental representative prior to registering. Permission of instructor required.

    1.000 TO    10.000 Credit hours


Syllabus Available

Levels: Graduate, Professional, Undergraduate 

Schedule Types: Experiential 


Offered By: College of Agriculture 

Department: Agric Sciences Educ & Comm 


Course Attributes: Internship, Upper Division 


May be offered at any of the following campuses:      

      West Lafayette


Repeatable for Additional Credit: Yes - May be repeated an unlimited number of times


Learning Outcomes: 
1. Explore components of Purdue Extension or other agricultural and extension entities.
2. Participate in activities, projects, and programs of Purdue Extension or other agricultural and extension entities.
3. Reflect on organizational philosophy, structure, and outcomes.
