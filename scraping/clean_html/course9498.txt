FNR 41910 - Furniture Product Development And Strength Design

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Qualitative and quantitative principles of furniture construction, product development methodology and strength design principles, furniture performance testing, product sustainability and end of life options (LCA, computer-based applications and solutions).  Course features laboratory evaluating, furniture joints and furniture structures.  Typically offered Spring.
