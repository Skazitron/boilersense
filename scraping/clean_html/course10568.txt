GER 20500 - Accelerated Intermediate German

Credit Hours: 4.00.  An accelerated intermediate level course in German designed to achieve second year language proficiency in German. The course is appropriate for students who would like to accelerate the process of learning German. Typically offered Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Identify appropriate use of German language in specific contexts by reading a variety of German texts.
2.  Identify German cultural aspects by watching videos in spoken German language integrated in the book.
3.  Identify appropriate use of German language in specific contexts by listening to audio recordings of spoken German.
4.  Compose grammatically correct German sentences by observing the norms and standards of the language.
5.  Produce sentences in spoken German in the form of dialogues and presentations and respond appropriately to questions and statements related to everyday life.



Prerequisites: 

 Undergraduate level  GER 10500 Minimum Grade of C- or Undergraduate level  GER 10200 Minimum Grade of C-
