CLCS 38000 - Alexander The Great and Hellenistic World

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Course examines the career of Alexander the Great and the rise of Macedonia in the Hellenistic Era. Topics include the emergence of Macedonia under Philip II; the achievements of Alexander the Great; and the wars of succession following his demise.
