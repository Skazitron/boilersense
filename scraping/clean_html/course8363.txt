ANTH 67300 - Seminar In The Anthropology Of Religion

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Explores anthropological approaches to studying the place of religion in human experience. Considers different theories that anthropologists have developed for understanding religion - symbolic, materialist, evolutionary, psychological, and others - and how they fit with ethnographic data on religion in different cultures.
