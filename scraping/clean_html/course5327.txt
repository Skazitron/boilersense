ECE 64100 - Digital Image Processing II

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An advanced treatment of selected topics in digital image processing. Image models, color, digital video, synthetic aperture radar, magnetic resonance imaging, stack filters, morphological filters, in-verse problems in computational vision, multiscale techniques. Offered every third semester. Prerequisite: ECE 60000, 63700.
