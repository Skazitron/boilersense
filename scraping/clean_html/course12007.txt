CE 63100 - Advanced Concrete And Aggregates

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Microstructure of concrete. Physicochemical properties of cements and their hydration. Nature of hardened cement paste. Properties of aggregates. Workability, strength, shrinkage, creep, and fracture of concrete. Durability, freezing and thawing, air-entertainment, reactions of aggregates, chemical attack. Influence of microstructure on engineering properties of concrete. Prerequisite:  CE 53000.
