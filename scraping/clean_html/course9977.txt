HSCI 13000 - Introduction To Medical Laboratory Science

Credit Hours: 1.00.  An introduction to career opportunities in medical technology. Preprofessional and professional requirements. A survey of the major clinical laboratory departments and their applications to patient care and management. Discussion and interpretation of some of the major diagnostic tests, i.e., for cardiac enzymes, cholesterol, blood cells, diabetes, and drug monitoring. Typically offered Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Have a clearer and more in depth understanding of the field of medical laboratory science.
2.  Have an appreciation for the diverse disciplines and thus career opportunities within this field.
3.  Have an opportunity to engage in and contribute to your own knowledge gain.
4.  Have the ability to translate a simple job description into an exciting professional career.
