GSLA 48001 - Senior Interdisciplinary Capstone Project In Global Studies II

Credit Hours: 3.00.  The senior capstone is a year-long course for which Global Studies majors enroll in consecutive semesters. Its primary goal is for students to define and complete a research project of personal, academic, and/or professional interest, as they integrate the interdisciplinary aspects of their entire curricula into one project. Students must complete GSLA 48000 and GSLA 48001 in order to complete the Global Studies major. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
