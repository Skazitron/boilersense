HK 40100 - Clinical Practice In Athletic Training - Lead

Credit Hours: 2.00.  Under the supervision of the certified athletic trainer, the student will be provided the opportunity to practice those skills acquired. The student is expected to take a leadership role in the clinical setting. The student must obtain a minimum of 240 hours, but no more than 480 hours of practicum experience. Typically offered Fall Spring.
