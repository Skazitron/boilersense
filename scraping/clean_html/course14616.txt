AGR 12100 - Introduction To Agricultural Sciences Education And Communication Academic Programs

Credit Hours: 0.50.  An introduction to the academic programs offered in the Department of Agricultural Sciences Education and Communication which includes Agricultural Communication and Agricultural Education. Topics include, but are not limited to undergraduate plans of study, courses, experiential programs, internships, student organizations, career opportunities, academic policies, scholarships, and student services. Course meets during weeks 1-8.
