EAPS 47400 - Sedimentation And Stratigraphy

Credit Hours: 4.00.  Systematic study of continental and marine depositional environments. Interpretation of sedimentary facies, structures, and microfacies. Principles and applications of stratigraphy, including applications of paleontology, field relations, paleomagnetism, well logs, seismic reflections, and chemistry. Field investigations may be required.  Prior course work in earth materials, mineralogy and petrology is required.
