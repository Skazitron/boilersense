CPB 86200 - Clinical Epidemiology For Companion Animals

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Fundamentals of experimental epidemiology and clinical decision making are explored, particularly as they relate to the practice of companion animal medicine. Methods for disease detection and prevention are emphasized in catteries and kennels. The computer laboratories illustrate the software available for problem solving in population medicine.
