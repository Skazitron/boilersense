BCM 32000 - Introduction To Disaster Restoration And Reconstruction Management

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Introductory topics in the DRR concentration are discussed; causes of disasters and dimensions of their effects, protocols, equipment, and techniques of restoration and reconstruction, including project and business management practices and imperatives of DRR contractors.
