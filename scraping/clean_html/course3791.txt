AT 21000 - Ground Trainer I

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Included in this course are maneuvers to develop basic attitude instrument flying skills. The student then proceeds into tracking, holding, and approach procedures. Intersection holding and arc flying will also be covered. Permission of department required.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Demonstrate proficiency in attitude instrument flying skills
2.  Demonstrate proficiency in the use of enroute and approach navigation methods
3.  Demonstrate proficiency in holding and arc maneuvers




Prerequisites: 

 Undergraduate level  AT 24300 Minimum Grade of D- [may be taken concurrently] or Undergraduate level  AT 24302 Minimum Grade of D- [may be taken concurrently]
