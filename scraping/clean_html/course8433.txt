CGT 16400 - Graphics For Civil Engineering And Construction

Credit Hours: 2.00.  An introductory course in the area of computer graphics documentation for civil engineering- and construction-related professions. Experiences focus on accepted industry graphic standards and their technical visual applications. The course emphasizes creation and distribution of graphics to enable communication.
