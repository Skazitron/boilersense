CM 55500 - Construction Leadership And Marketing

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Analysis of organizational leadership at the executive level. The goals of this course are to introduce and stimulate thought and discussion of leadership theories, characteristics and behaviors of successful leaders, contextual factors affecting leader effectiveness, dynamics of leader-follower relationships, communication, decision-making and contemporary challenges confronting organizational leaders as well as marketing theory, marketing vs. business development, the marketing process, and an introduction to a construction marketing dashboard.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Understand the essential principles of leadership theories and apply the theories in construction management.
2.  Comprehend the characteristics and behaviors of successful leaders in the field of construction.
3.  Grasp the main contextual factors affecting leader effectiveness.
4.  Apply the dynamics of leader-follower relationships in daily operations.
5.  Acquire communication, decision-making and other key leadership skills.
6.  Recognize the contemporary challenges confronting organizational leaders.
7.  Understand the marketing theory, the market process, and marketing vs. business development.
8.  Understand and utilize construction marketing dashboard.
