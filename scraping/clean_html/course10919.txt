CHE 44200 - Chemistry And Engineering Of High Polymers

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Introduction to basic principles of polymer engineering, including the chemical structure and use of a variety of industrial polymers, polymerization mechanisms and kinetics, techniques for molecular and morphological characterization, polymer processing, and a variety of engineering properties.
