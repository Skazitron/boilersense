CHNS 28100 - Introduction To Chinese Food Culture

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Selected topics on Chinese food history, production, preparation, festivals, as well as food in literature, film, business and folk beliefs. Discussion will also include civilization and food as cultural diplomacy. Lectures in English.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Knowledge of evolution of Chinese food history.
2.  Learn main concepts of Chineses cooking and gastronomy.
3.  Acquire appreciation of the important food-related works in Chinese literature.
4.  Learn current trend of food culture in China.
5.  Acquire appreciation and understand cultural and historical aspects on major Chinese teas, wines, and snacks in association with their respective geographic features; folk beliefs on medicinal usages of Chinese teas and wines; and the etiquette regarding tea art, wine drinking, drinker's wager game, and snack serving.
