AFT 24000 - Team And Leadership Fundamentals II

Credit Hours: 1.00. Provides a fundamental understanding of both leadership and team building. The lessons and course flow are designed to prepare students for field training and leadership positions in the detachment.

    1.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Explain Air Force core values.
2.  Discuss examples of how Air Force core values were used throughout Air and Space Force history.
3.  Describe the employment of Air and Space power from institutional, doctrinal, and historical perspectives.
4.  Demonstrate basic Air Force verbal and written communication skills.
