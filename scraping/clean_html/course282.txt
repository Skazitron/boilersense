ENGL 41400 - Studies In Literature And Culture

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A study of literature or film from the perspective of the cultural norms and values it expresses, celebrates, and challenges. A project-based capstone seminar containing a significant research component. May be repeated for credit only with a different topic. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

    3.000 Credit hours

Repeatable for Additional Credit: Yes - May be repeated up to 1 times


Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Demonstrate knowledge of the relationship between literary texts and their culture.
2.  Discuss those relationships in light of texts' diverse historical and aesthetic contexts.
3.  Produce a substantial work or project: a research essay; a digital humanities project; a scholarly edition; or a comparable service learning project.
