AGEC 69200 - Workshop In Applied Economics

Credit Hours: 1.00.  A workshop on how to communicate research results in a seminar setting. Students present at least once during the course and will be present for other students' presentations. Students may present their own research, or a paper from a recent journal also is acceptable. The course is open to master's students, and one credit of this course is required of doctoral students.
