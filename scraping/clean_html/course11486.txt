PSY 35200 - Introduction To Neuropsychology

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course is an examination of the effects of various types of brain damage on human behavior, the methods used to assess brain damage, and evaluation of research studies on human brain damage and behavior. Typically offered Fall Spring.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Describe effects of damage to different human brain areas on behavior.
2.  Discuss what effects of brain damage teach us about brain function and organization.
3.  Describe approaches used to study human brain damage.
4.  Critically evaluate published research studies on human brain damage and behavior.
