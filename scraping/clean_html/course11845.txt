CE 34100 - Hydraulics, Hydrology, And Drainage

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Basic hydraulic theory, including properties, kinematics, and dynamics of liquid flow in open channels and closed conduits. Principles of hydrology with emphasis on rainfall and runoff. Application of the above principles to the analysis and design of storm sewers, sanitary sewers, drainage courses, and culverts.
