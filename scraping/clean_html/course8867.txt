ECON 67300 - Time Series Econometrics

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  This advanced course gives an up-to-date and comprehensive treatment of modern time series analysis and empirical macroeconomics. Topics include: ARIMA processes, spectral analysis, forecasting, covariance-stationary vector processes, vector auto regressions, state space representations in the Kalman filter, non-stationary time series and fractional integrated processes, and time series models of heteroscedasticity.  Prerequisite: ECON 67200.
