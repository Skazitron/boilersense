Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Analyze physical circumstances according to Newton's Laws of motion properly.
2.  Apply basic conservation laws and and to recognize the circumstances in which they apply.
3.  Use physical terms such as energy, acceleration, momentum, impulse, velocity, etc., properly.
