EAPS 59000 - Field Geology North America

Credit Hours: 2.00.  Advanced study of tectonic evolution and stratigraphic history of specific geologic provinces. Particular emphasis on the relationship between tectonics, basin development, and sedimentation. Field component deals with application of field techniques used in basin analysis and structural studies. A 10-day field trip is required. Permission of instructor required.
