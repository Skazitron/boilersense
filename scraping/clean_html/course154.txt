ENGL 24000 - British Literature Before 1789

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A historically oriented survey of genres, authors, and texts from the medieval to the Romantic period. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Demonstrate knowledge of a range of genres, topics, and literary conventions from the pre-Romantic period. 
2.  Discuss those genres, topics, and conventions in relation to their diverse historical, cultural, and aesthetic contexts.
3.  Produce well-reasoned written arguments about pre-Romantic literature based on appropriate use of textual evidence.
