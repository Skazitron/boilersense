Credit Hours: 2.00.  An introduction to the techniques and principles of modern dance.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Develop the ability to perform beginning locomotor movements. 
2.  Increase body awareness as well as awareness of space and spatial design. 
3.  Learn anatomical, physiological and mechanical principles of the human body, especially as they apply to the movement for the dancer. 
4.  Develop kinesthetic and muscular response to tempo and rhythm. 
5.  Develop an appreciation of modern dance as an art form.
