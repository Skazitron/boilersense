CPB 69400 - Special Topics In Immunology

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Understanding of principles of immunology is required. Journal club style class geared to provide graduate students with in-depth knowledge in current immunology. Faculty will participate in class to facilitate the students' understanding and discussion. Students present one-two times depending on the number of participating students. They select a research paper, recently published in the area of immunology, assigned for the semester and have it approved by the course organizer.
