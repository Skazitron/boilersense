IDE 49500 - Special Topics In Interdisciplinary Engineering

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 4.00.  Primarily designed for interdisciplinary engineering subject areas for which there is no specific course offered, but for which there is sufficient student interest to justify teaching a specialized course on a trial basis.  May be repeated for credit as long as the topic being taught is not repeated. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
