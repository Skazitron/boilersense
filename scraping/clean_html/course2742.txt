TLI 48800 - Technology Leadership And Innovation Capstone

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This is an integrative course that focuses on using cross-functional teams to identify, scope, design, and propose solutions for problems that span the areas of industrial engineering technology, organizational leadership, and supply chain management. Field trips may be required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
