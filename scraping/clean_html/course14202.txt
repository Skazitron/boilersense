ENTM 22841 - Forensic Entomology Principles Lab

Credit Hours: 1.00.  An optional laboratory component to Principles of Forensic Entomology. Insects of importance in the fields of stored products, urban, medical/veterinary, and medico-legal will be covered, including morphological identifications and an assessment of their biology and damage. Typically offered Fall.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Experience the basic techniques and skills employed by each of the four main disciplines reviewed.
2.  Classify and identify insects within each discipline using morphological characteristics.
3.  Develop appropriate collecting and rearing techniques for each type of insect.
4.  Evaluate an entomological scenario and create guidelines on how to best handle the situation.
