AGEC 20400 - Introduction To Resource Economics And Environmental Policy

Credit Hours: 3.00.  The course provides an overview of microeconomic theory and its application to issues related to evaluating resource economic issues and environmental policy.  Topics discussed include efficiency, sustainability, valuation, externalities, governmental policies, and benefit cost analysis.
