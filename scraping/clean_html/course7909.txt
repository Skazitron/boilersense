ANSC 37200 - Horse Evaluation

Credit Hours: 2.00.  A student-centered laboratory course designed to familiarize students with functional horse conformation and type that maximizes athletic ability, applies selection criteria established by national breed associations for evaluating performance events, and prepares students to select halter and performance horses of many breeds and disciplines. The combination of ANSC 37000, 37100, 37200, 47000, 47100, and 47200 cannot exceed three credits toward ANSC electives. Requires class trips. Students will pay individual lodging or meal expenses when necessary.
