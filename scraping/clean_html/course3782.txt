AT 14400 - Private Pilot Lectures

Credit Hours: 4.00.  A study of the subject areas and information needed to operate as a private pilot in the aviation environment. The FAA private pilot written exam will be offered to those who qualify after taking the lecture course offering.

    0.000 OR     4.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Recall the aeronautical subject areas required by the FAA as a private pilot.
2.  Analyze aspects of professionalism and decision making within the aviation environment.
3.  Compare and contrast the reference information available covering a given aviation concept or best practice.
4.  Explain to team members about issues requiring resolution or input.
5.  Demonstrate the knowledge, skills, and abilities to manage, lead, and empower others to efficiently address organizational and group needs and objectives.
