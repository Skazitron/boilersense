LC 65900 - Seminar In Literature And Culture Of The African Diaspora

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An examination of the African Diaspora through the study of literatures and cultures of Latin America and the Caribbean. Prerequisites: Experience working with literatures of the African Diaspora and critical theory. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.


    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Acquire knowledge of the historical shifts that affected the formation of the literary movements.
2.  Use theory in their interpretation of the texts.
3.  Demonstrate ability to apply literary theories in order to analyze the literature read.
