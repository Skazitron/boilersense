SOC 48900 - Research Internship In Sociology And Law And Society

Credit Hours: 3.00 to 6.00.  Course organized around internship experience in organizations that collect or analyze data from surveys, social media, focus groups, interviews, or experiments. Completion of 60 credits with a minimum GPA of 3.0, including 15 credits of Sociology with a minimum grade of B in SOC 10000, SOC 38200, and SOC 38300. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Summer.
