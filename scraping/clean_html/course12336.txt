AGEC 60400 - Fundamentals Of Applied Welfare Economics

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Basic concepts and principles of applied welfare economics including Pareto conditions for welfare maximization; public goods, externalities, and property rights; consumer and producer surplus; theory of the second best; and neoclassical and contemporary theories of income distribution.  Concurrent Prerequisite:  ECON 51100.
