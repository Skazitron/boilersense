AT 35100 - Flight Instructor Flight

Credit Hours: 2.00.  Flight training in preparation for flight instructor certification. Instructional techniques are demonstrated and practiced by the student. Heavy emphasis is placed on student practice teaching of all maneuvers.  FAA Commercial Pilot Certificate with Instrument Airplane Rating required. Permission of department required.

    2.000 Credit hours


Syllabus Available

Levels: Graduate, Professional, Undergraduate 

Schedule Types: Distance Learning, Laboratory 


Offered By: Polytechnic Institute 

Department: Schl of Aviation & Transp Tech 


Course Attributes: Dept Credit, Upper Division 


May be offered at any of the following campuses:      

      West Lafayette


Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Able to perform, critique and analyze all Private Pilot maneuvers.
2.  Able to perform, critique and analyze all Commercial Pilot maneuvers.
3.  Able to teach all knowledge areas for the Private Pilot Certificate.
4.  Able to teach all knowledge areas for the Commercial Pilot Certificate.
5.  Able to teach aircraft systems.
6.  Accomplish the Flight Instructor Airplane Practical Test.





Prerequisites: 

 (Undergraduate level  AT 25300 Minimum Grade of D- or Undergraduate level  AT 25302 Minimum Grade of D-) and Undergraduate level  AT 35200 Minimum Grade of C- [may be taken concurrently]
