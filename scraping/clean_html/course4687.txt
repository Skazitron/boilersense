FR 32400 - Professional French II

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Practical reading, writing, speaking, and listening comprehension skills at an intermediate level will be developed toward the use of French for business purposes.  Course materials cover daily business dealing as well as national and international trade.  Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Discuss specific aspects of the French professional world, such as the role of the French government in the economy.
2.  Accomplish administrative tasks in French (write a professional letter, reports, etc.).
3.  Behave appropriately in a French professional context.




Prerequisites: 

 Undergraduate level  FR 30200 Minimum Grade of C and Undergraduate level  FR 22400 Minimum Grade of C
