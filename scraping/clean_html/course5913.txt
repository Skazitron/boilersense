ENGR 10300 - Introduction To Engineering In Practice

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Weekly small group seminars led by faculty designed to introduce a specific topic, problem, or discipline of engineering to First-Year Engineering students. Seminars are designed to help students explore a single area of engineering and/or to assist in their decision of an engineering degree program. Typically offered Fall Spring.
