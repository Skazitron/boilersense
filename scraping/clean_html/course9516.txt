FNR 49000 - Sustainable Product/Process Design Guided Research

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  Students will be involved in efforts to solve many complex natural resource utilization issues on the local and global scale.  Projects engaging with important industrial forest products stakeholders will be encouraged to allow students to demonstrate proficiency in a sustainable product development or application of sustainable manufacturing practices and communication of their ideas.  Permission of Instructor required.  Typically offered Fall Spring.
