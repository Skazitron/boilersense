ASTR 56700 - Observational Techniques In Astronomy

Credit Hours: 3.00.  (PHYS 56700)  Fundamental concepts in observational astronomy, including coordinate and time systems, telescopes and detectors, radiation and optics, and methods of statistical data analysis.

    3.000 Credit hours


Syllabus Available

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Gain knowledge and skills needed to carry out the major steps involved in astrophysical research, including literature research and formulation of a project.
2.  Identification of suitable telescope and detector.
3.  Planning of observations.
4.  Reduction of data and analysis/hypothesis testing.
5.  Publication in a professional journal.
