POL 49505 - Undergraduate Research Experience Capstone

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Students are able to earn credit by working on a research project with a faculty member to count as capstone credit on plan of study. Students will become familiar with all phases of the research process. Credit and course requirements are arranged with the instructor. Permission of department required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Learn the systematic collection of data for quantitative and qualitative analysis.
2.  Apply research methods and discuss how to utilize effectively in a research project related to the discipline.
3.  Develop writing and oral communication proficiencies through completion of semester long paper and/or presentation.
4.  Collaborate on research work with faculty mentor or peers.
