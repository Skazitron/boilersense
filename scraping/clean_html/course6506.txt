CSR 32700 - History Of Fashion

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Exploration of the historical development of western world fashion from the ancient world through the 21st century. Students learn to understand the impact and influence of society, culture, art, work, economics, politics, and technology on the style of fashion - past, present, and future.
