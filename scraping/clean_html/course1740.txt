BCM 21200 - Construction Layout

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Application of surveying skills relevant to the field of construction. Projects include layout of buildings, route centerlines, indirect determination of elevation and distance, referencing, establishment of grade, topographic mapping, and earthwork computations. Instruments used will include transit, theodolite, automatic level, laser, and EDM.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Determine the accuracy required for specific construction layout activities.
2.  Utilize EDMs to measure and lay out distances in construction settings.
3.  Calculate and lay out horizontal and vertical curves. 
4.  Calculate field layout data from coordinates.
