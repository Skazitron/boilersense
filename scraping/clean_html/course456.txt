ENGL 58500 - Creative Nonfiction Writing

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Study and practice of advanced methods of composing creative nonfiction, with primary emphasis on the student's own work. Workshop criticism and discussion of published writing.  Typically offered Fall Spring.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Write and revise sophisticated creative nonfiction through the study and practice of techniques unique to the genre.
2.  Produce polished creative nonfiction revised according to suggestions received in workshop and feedback from the instructor.
3.  Acquire a broader knowledge of the context of contemporary creative nonfiction by reading and discussing work by several contemporary fiction writers.
