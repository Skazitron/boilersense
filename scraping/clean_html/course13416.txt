CLCS 48000 - Potters And Society In Antiquity

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Course covers the range of eastern Mediterranean ceramics encountered in Rough Cilicia Archaeological Survey Project from the Bronze Age to the Later Roman Empire. Course also explores strategies employed by archaeologists and historians to exploit ceramics as research materials.
