REL 23000 - Religions Of The East

Credit Hours: 3.00.  (PHIL 33000) A study of the history, teaching, and present institutions of the religions of India, Southeast Asia, China, and Japan.  This will include Hinduism, Jainism, Sikhism, Buddhism, Confucianism, Taoism, Shintoism, and Zoroastrianism. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
