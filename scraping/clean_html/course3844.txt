AT 36600 - Multi-Engine Flight Instructor Flight

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Flight and ground training in preparation for the Flight Instructor Multi-engine Flight Test is given. Instructional techniques are demonstrated and practiced by the student. Analysis of maneuvers and common student errors also included. FAA Flight Instructor Airplane, Instrument Airplane, Commercial Pilot Certificate with Airplane Multi-engine Land Rating, 15 hours pilot in-command in multi-engine airplanes. Permission of department required.
