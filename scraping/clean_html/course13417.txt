CLCS 48100 - Culture And Society In The Age Of Pericles

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Course explores interrelationships between the emergence of Greek democracy and the cultural, political, social, and economic rise of Athens in the fifth century BCE. More broadly, course surveys history of the Greek world from the Late Bronze Age to 362 BCE.
