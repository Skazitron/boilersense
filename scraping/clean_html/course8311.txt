ANTH 40400 - Comparative Social Organization

Credit Hours: 3.00.  The course uses a broad cross-cultural comparative perspective to identify and analyze the major forms of human social organization. Emphasis is on kinship terminology, descent, marriage, residence units, economic exchange, political structure, and social inequality.
