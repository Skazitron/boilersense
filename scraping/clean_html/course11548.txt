PSY 49200 - Internship In Psychology

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Experiential, supervised training in various areas of psychology-related fields including human factors, human resources, mental health-related organizations, research labs, and supervised living institutions. Usually taken in junior or senior year. Permission of instructor required.
