SOC 27500 - Sociology Of Aging And The Life Course

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An examination of research on the social context of human aging, including demographic and developmental changes.  A life course perspective is emphasized for studying health, life events, and quality of life.  Considers exceptional longevity in comparative perspective. Typically offered Fall.
