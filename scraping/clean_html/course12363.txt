AGEC 68400 - Applied Quantitative Methods For Decision Making

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Explores the application of contemporary concepts and quantitative techniques for decision making in the face of uncertainty. Focus is on application of statistical tools to decisions facing the food and agricultural business manager. Permission of department required.
