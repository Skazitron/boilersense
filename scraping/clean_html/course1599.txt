MGMT 58800 - Business Insights With Spreadsheets And Macro Programming

Credit Hours: 2.00 or 3.00.  This course takes a variety of business topics and implements with state-of-the-art spreadsheet models. Students will gain hands-on experience with a wide range of business problems and databases in finance, marketing, operations, and so on. The focus of this course is to learn Visual Basic for Applications (VBA) to design functions and macros that will enhance spreadsheet models. Students will also have exposure to basics and other advanced topics of spreadsheet modeling. In addition to in-classroom time, this course will also meet in computer-based labs for hands-on instructions and implementation. Typically offered Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Learn the basic concepts, components, and elements of the Visual Basic language.
2.  Learn the macro programming environment in Excel.
3.  Apply the basics of macro programming in organizing, summarizing, and visualizing data.
4.  Develop the ability to use the techniques to analyze real-world data and draw scientific or managerial implications.
