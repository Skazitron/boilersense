Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Demonstrate knowledge of a range of topics and conventions related to classic and contemporary films.
2.  Discuss those topics and conventions in relation to their diverse historical, cultural, and aesthetic contexts.
3.  Produce well-reasoned written arguments about classic and contemporary films based on appropriate use of evidence.
