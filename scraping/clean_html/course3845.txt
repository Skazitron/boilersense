AT 36900 - Air Traffic Control

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An introduction to the principles of air traffic control, including tower and enroute separation. Navigation theory as it relates to the ATC system will be explained, as will the theory of operation of ATC radar and computer systems.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Operate ATC simulation equipment.
2.  Understand the basic information required to perform the duties of an air traffic controller.
3.  Understand operations in the National Airspace System.
