CGT 17207 - User Experience Design Experience Studio I

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course allows students to practice as a UX designer in a company-like environment. Students learn about the roles they can pursue and build foundational collaboration and project management skills. Projects utilize a human-centered design approach to provide a rich user experience.
