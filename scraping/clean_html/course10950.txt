CHE 62300 - Separation Processes

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Design of binary and multicomponent separation processes. Analysis and synthesis of adsorption, ion exchange, and chromatography in packed beds, moving beds, simulated moving beds, and in cyclic operation. Design and operation of membrane separation techniques including dialysis, reverse osmosis, ultrafiltration, and dynamic membranes. Offered in alternate years.
