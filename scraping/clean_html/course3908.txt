AT 49600 - Applied Research Proposal

Credit Hours: 2.00.  Students will study all aspects of performing a product or process design review and life-cycle management analysis. Topics include reliability, supportability, life cycle management, and performance analysis. Each student will select their capstone research area and development a proposal for conducting a product or process analysis for their applied research project.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Integrate knowledge and skills to plan and propose a process/product/service improvement or process/product/service design for design, aircraft operations, or maintenance. 
2.  Prepare and present a project plan and proposal.
3.  Demonstrate knowledge of design and improvement processes. 
4.  Demonstrate the application of problem-solving and technical knowledge learned throughout the curriculum. 
5.  Apply life-long learning skills to acquire the knowledge, skills and resources required to complete the project.
