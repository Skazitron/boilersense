AD 11900 - Color Photography

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Introductory course in creative use of color photographic processes and studio practices.  Emphasis is on the acquisition of digital cameras, studio, and digital printing skills which enable students to successfully perceive and structure images reflecting color's formal, symbolic, and emotional impact.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Gain an understanding of capturing and processing color digital imagery.
2.  Basic color correction and image enhancement using Photoshop.
3.  Gain an understanding of conceptual dialogue using photography and color, and compiling a portfolio of images displaying the understanding.
