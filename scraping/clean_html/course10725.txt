ENTR 49000 - Senior Level Special Topics In Entrepreneurship And Innovation

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  The requested course will provide the opportunity to develop capstone courses for the Certificate in Entrepreneurship and Innovation Program.  Capstone courses are designed to provide students with the opportunity to apply the knowledge acquired in Certificate Program "core" and "option" courses to hands-on, real world activities related to entrepreneurship and innovation. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
