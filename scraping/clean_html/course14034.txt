AD 33800 - Advanced Interior Design Communication

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Study of graphic design fundamentals and application of interior design visual presentation.  To provide a foundation for developing proficiency in the use visual communication design in interior design presentation. Offered 3rd year in the Interior Design BFA program of study.

    0.000 OR     3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Learn graphic design fundamentals and techniques as a tool for communicating interior design.
2.  Develop the skills, ability, and judgement to visualize, develop, and communicate interior design ideas through graphic representation.
3.  Strengthen a student's interior design portfolio through review and organization of contents.
4.  Learn to graphically represent design ideas, design processes, and design outcomes.
