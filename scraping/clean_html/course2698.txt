LING 69000 - Individual Seminar

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  Under the individual guidance and supervision of a faculty member, students will select and investigate a topic and will produce a paper of professional caliber on the subject. Prerequisite: Doctoral student standing and Linguistics majors only. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
