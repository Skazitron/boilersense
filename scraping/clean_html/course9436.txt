SFT 11000 - Foundations Of The Space Force I

Credit Hours: 1.00.  A survey of officership, professionalism, communications skills, the Space Force organization and its mission. Emphasis is placed on helping students understand what they will experience as a Space Force Officer.

    1.000 Credit hours


Syllabus Available

Levels: Graduate, Professional, Undergraduate 

Schedule Types: Distance Learning, Lecture 


Offered By: Polytechnic Institute 

Department: Division of Military Science 


Course Attributes: Lower Division 


May be offered at any of the following campuses:      

      West Lafayette


Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Explain the military role of the US Space Force.
2.  Describe leadership fundamentals, service benefits, and opportunities for officers.
3.  Explain how the Space Force has evolved from a historical perspective.
4.  Explain the Space Force's Guardian Commitment as an ethical framework.
5.  Demonstrate the fundamentals of communicating effectively.
