AT 38300 - Instrument And Multiengine Instructor Flight

Credit Hours: 1.00.  This course prepares the student for the FAA Flight Instructor Instrument and Multiengine Instructor ratings. All flight instruction is conducted in a multi-engine aircraft.

    1.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Explain and demonstrate the definitions of objectives, elements, and errors common during the performance of instrument and multi-engine maneuvers.
2.  Perform, critique and analyze all instrument and multi-engine maneuvers.
3.  Demonstrate the ability to teach all required knowledge areas for the instrument and multi-engine ratings in both the aircraft and the classroom.
4.  Demonstrate proficiency in flight planning, instrument flight procedures, and simulated aircraft operations including preflight takeoff, departure, holding patterns, approach/missed approach, and normal landing procedures.
5.  Demonstrate proficiency in operating all aircraft systems in both normal and abnormal situations.



Prerequisites:
