ANTH 56300 - Historical Linguistics

Credit Hours: 3.00.  ( ENGL 56300, LC 56300) A survey of mechanisms and motivations of linguistic change. Topics include: phonological, morphological, semantic and syntactic change, comparative and internal reconstruction, linguistic variation, language contact, and linguistic typology.
