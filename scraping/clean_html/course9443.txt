SFT 48100 - National Security, Leadership Responsibilities, And Commissioning Preparation II

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course addresses how the Space Force and the other services are organized and employed to respond to national security challenges. It also examines the military justice system and the responsibilities, authorities and functions of military command. Emphasizes the importance of effective communication.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Describe the basic process of developing national security policy.
2.  Explain the Space Force warfighting domains and their interrelationships.
3.  Discuss current domestic and international issues affective the military profession.
4.  Explain the purpose and legal requirements of the Uniform Code of Military Justice.
5.  Identify the responsibilities, authorities, and functions of a Space Force commander.
