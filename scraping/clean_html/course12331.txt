AGEC 57300 - International Business Analysis

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This is the third course in a required three-course sequence designed for students in the Professional Masters in International Agribusiness concentration in the Department of Agricultural Economics. The course objective is to assist students in conducting a professional business analysis focusing on either a market opportunity, financial management, or other management issue in international agribusiness. The course is project-based and includes group activities and written and oral presentations.
