HTM 25500 - Advanced Spreadsheet Techniques For Hospitality And Tourism Management

Credit Hours: 2.00.  This course builds upon information and skills obtained in an entry level-computing course.  It will take students to the next level by introducing them to more advanced techniques used in Hospitality, Tourism, Retail, and Sales Management. Typically offered Fall Spring.

    2.000 Credit hours


Syllabus Available

      West Lafayette


Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Demonstrate proper utilization of spreadsheet techniques to effectively organize and analyze data for management presentations.
2.  Explain the purpose of selected spreadsheet techniques and describe how they are used in Hospitality, Tourism, Retail, and Sales Management.
3.  Demonstrate advanced spreadsheet techniques, such as: Advanced Spreadsheet Formulas; Statistical Analysis; Validating Data Entry; Conditional Formatting; Advanced Charting Techniques; Pivot Tables; Spreadsheet Dashboards; Recording and Using Spreadsheet Macros; Power Query; Advanced Cell/Column/Row/Table Formatting; Creating Templates.
