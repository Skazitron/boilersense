EDCI 56600 - Educational Applications Of Multimedia

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Examination of educational applications of hypermedia tools and related research. Creation of hypermedia instructional materials. Incorporation of digitized media (sound, photographs, and motion clips) in hypermedia is explored. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Explain the effectiveness of a specific instructional design principle in educational multimedia design. 
2.  Demonstrate when and how to use specific instructional design principles within a given educational multimedia learning environment. 
3.  Address the key principles and methods for evaluating student outcomes in a multimedia learning environment.  
4.  Develop educational computer-based multimedia instructional materials with various software and tools. 
5.  Be familiar with the history of multimedia research and design and underlying assumptions from relevant theorists. 
6.  Explain variations in usability testing and its role in multimedia development.
