RUSS 34100 - Russian Literature In The Nineteenth Century

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Selected Russian poetry, prose, and drama from the Kievan period to 1900 in historical and cultural context. Students will write critical essays on literary topics. Conducted largely in Russian. Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Understand the role of literature in 19th century Russian culture and society.
2.  Situate the works they read in their historical and political content.
3.  Improve understanding of the way literary works function through various artistic devices.
4.  Improve ability to perform close readings of literary texts.
5.  Increase ability to write analytical papers supporting a thesis.
6.  Increase ability to conduct and present independent research.
7.  Confront the philosophical and ethical questions raised by these works.
