PSY 35400 - Close Relationships

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course introduces undergraduate college students to major topics in psychological research on intimate relationships. There are many types of close relationships. This primarily focuses on relationships that currently are, will be soon, or at one point were, romantic involvements. A goal is to educate students on current research findings concerning intimate relationships. Typically offered Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Navigate relevant research and demonstrate understanding and of relationship science.
2.  Judge the accuracy of portrayals of relationships in the general media.
3.  Describe the mechanics underlying close relationships.
4.  Demonstrate the ability to read and understand published studies concerning close relationships.
