AGR 12500 - Introduction To Plant Science

Credit Hours: 1.00.  An introduction to the academic programs offered in plant science. Topics include, but are not limited to, undergraduate plans of study, courses, and experiential programs including undergraduate research opportunities, internships, student organizations, career opportunities, academic policies, scholarships, and student services.
