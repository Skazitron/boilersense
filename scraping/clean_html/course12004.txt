CE 61200 - Physical Geodesy

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Gravity field modeling. Spherical harmonics. Gravimetry and gravity reduction methods. Discrete/continuous estimation. Gravity prediction, collocation. Local and world height datums. Earth rotation, differential equations of Euler and Liouville. Basics of inertial surveying.  Prerequisite:  CE 61100.
