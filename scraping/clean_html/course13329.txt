DANC 35100 - Intermedia Dance Technology

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An introduction to dance and intermedia within the choreographic process.  Integration of movement and various forms of technology which might involve digital images, film, animation, programming, sensors, sound, lighting and/or robotics.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Perform new approaches to choreography by integrating intermedia technology into the dance-making process through intermedia dance projects.
2.  Illustrate basic skills for creating film, digital media, sound editing, and programming by choreographing and directing intermedia dance projects.
3.  Identify dance intermedia technology art and express its value and meaning as both a creator and observer by critiquing professional intermedia dance technology works through class discussions and written assignments.
4.  Demonstrate personal expression and artistry through composing intermedia dance technology projects.
