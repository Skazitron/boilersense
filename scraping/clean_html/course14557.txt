AMST 61000 - Transnational American Studies Abroad

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Provides students an annual opportunity to study abroad and to familiarize themselves with Transnational scholarship in the field of American Studies. The seminar reflects efforts of the program to internationalize its curriculum and to establish ties with international universities. The course also complements the requirement of second language study for the Ph.D. in American Studies. Prerequisite:  AMST 60100.
