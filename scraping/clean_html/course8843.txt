ECON 62000 - Industrial Organization

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  A survey of some of the major theoretical issues in the field of industrial organization. Topics include: the welfare economics of competition and monopoly, static and dynamic oligopoly theory, differentiated products models, and entry deterrence.
