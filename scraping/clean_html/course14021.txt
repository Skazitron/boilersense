AD 33100 - Digital Video Production And Aesthetics

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course is a basic studio introduction to video production and how video can be used as an artistic medium in conjunction with the student's photography. Topics include video/film formats, lighting, audio, editing, distribution, experimental, commercial and music video, production paperwork and extensive time spent on mastering the camera. Classes will consist of hands-on demonstrations, and critiques.
