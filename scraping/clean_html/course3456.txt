COM 35300 - Problems In Public Relations

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Approaches to problems in public relations as they occur in industry, government, education, social agencies, and other institutions.

    3.000 Credit hours


Syllabus Available

Levels: Graduate, Professional, Undergraduate 

Schedule Types: Distance Learning, Lecture

      Northwest- Hammond

      West Lafayette


Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Develop an attitude of professionalism and tact.
2.  Expand existing knowledge about theories in the practice of public relations.
3.  Gain a greater understanding of the use of the tools of public relations practice.
4.  Recognize types of public relations challenges and how to address them.
5. Work effectively and efficiently as a strong team member.
6.  Write and edit well, and know how to improve at both.
7.  Feel an increase in self-confidence and professionalism that will help with career advancement.
