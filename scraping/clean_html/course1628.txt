MGMT 62500 - Marketing Research

Credit Hours: 2.00 to 4.00.  (West Lafayette) 3.00 (Calumet) The purpose of this course is to develop skills in the planning and execution of market research studies designed to acquire useful information for marketing decisions. It aims to familiarize students with techniques of research design, data collection, and analysis. Emphasis is placed on evaluating the results obtained from such investigations. Prerequisite: MGMT 67000.
