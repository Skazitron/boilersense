CNIT 39000 - Supervised Practicum

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  An instructor-directed practicum designed to combine University study with work experience directly related to the student's plan of study.  Designed to be scheduled during a regular semester. Credit awarded upon the completion of department-approved project.  Maximum of three credit hours may be applied to the CIT bachelor degree.
