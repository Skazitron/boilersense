AD 52000 - Student Visual Design Service

Credit Hours: 3.00.  The course simulates the operation of a professional design studio affording the opportunity for hands-on experience in the development of a graphic product from concept to finish and include contact with clients, and printers.

    3.000 Credit hours

      West Lafayette

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Produce design proposals to solve real-world problems.
2.  Produce briefs, schedule, research strategy, and budgets.
3.  Understand approaches related to client interaction and communication.
4.  Develop an understanding of the structure of a professional design studio.
5.  Understand aspects of the design industry related to vendor, clients, licenses and suppliers. 
6.  Develop collaborative design skills.
