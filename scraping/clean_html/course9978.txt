HSCI 13100 - Introduction To Medical Terminology

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This self-paced learning course is designed to familiarize the student with terms used in medicine. Each body system will be studied individually. Word roots, prefixes, and suffixes will be used to build words relating to the system. Diagnostic and symptomatic terms will be introduced. CTL:IPO 1820 Medical Terminology
