CPB 62200 - Microbial Pathogenesis

Credit Hours: 2.00.  Provides advanced study of the concepts of microbial pathogenesis and host-microbe interaction. The virulence factors and molecular mechanisms of infection used by bacteria, viruses, and protozoa to cause disease will be examined. Specific diseases will be studied as examples of how microbes affect animals as well as humans. Permission of instructor required.
