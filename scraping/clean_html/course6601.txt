HIST 23005 - Hitler's Europe

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course will examine the rise, seizure, and consolidation of power by the Nazi Party and will trace the development of Adolf Hitler from a provincial Austrian to dictator of Nazi Germany. Typically offered Fall Spring.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Explain how and why Adolf Hitler and the Nazis seized and exercised power, and the consequences for Germany and Europe.
2.  Distinguish between secondary and primary sources and critically read both types of sources; synthesize information and arguments from both types of sources.
3.  Evaluate biography as a historical genre; engage in independent historical interpretation with the use of primary and secondary sources.
4.  Compose analytical essays in fluid English prose with thesis statements, clear organization, and appeals to concrete evidence.
