EDCI 42200 - The Teaching Of English In Secondary Schools

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Explores a wide variety of instructional practices suitable for teaching middle and high school English including language, literature, and writing - addressing specific pedagogical issues such as teaching diverse learners and developing cohesive units of instruction. Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Explore different philosophical and instructional applications for secondary English teaching.
2.  Examine diverse issues affecting teaching and learning in the secondary English classrooms.
3.  Develop the reflective and critical thinking skills necessary for secondary English teachers.
4.  Increase pedagogical competency in diverse aspects of teaching and learning, including classroom instruction, student interaction, and teacher development.
