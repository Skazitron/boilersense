EDCI 62700 - Current Topics In Learning Design And Technology

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A critical analysis of problems, trends, issues, and programs related to the area of learning design and technology.  Prerequisite:  EDCI 51300. Typically offered Summer Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Critically analyze current issues and trends in the field related to a given topic.
2.  Identify and discuss advantages and limitations to implementing specific emerging tools, strategies, and /or approaches.
3.  Identify strategies for examining the effectiveness of specific emerging tools and strategies.
