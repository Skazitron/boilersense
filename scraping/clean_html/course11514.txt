PSY 40400 - Honors Research Seminar I

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Part one of a research series aimed at providing a structured learning experience to help students develop the basic research skills needed in order to conduct psychological research.  Students will be expected to develop a research plan to be completed in concert with PSY 40500. Permission of department required. Typically offered Spring.
