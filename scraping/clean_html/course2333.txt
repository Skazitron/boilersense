ME 61400 - Computational Fluid Dynamics

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Application of finite difference methods, finite element methods, and the method of characteristics for the numerical solution of fluid dynamics problems. Incompressible viscous flows: vorticity transport equation, stream function equation, and boundary conditions. Compressible flows: treatment of shocks, implicit and explicit artificial viscosity techniques, and boundary conditions. Computational grids.  Prerequisite: ME 50900 or 58100.
