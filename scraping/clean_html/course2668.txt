LING 51100 - Phonology I: Descriptive Analysis

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Foundations of phonological analysis. Development of concepts and methods for the analysis of phonological data and the phonological structures of natural languages within the framework of generative phonology. Focuses on problem solving and linguistic argumentation. Offered in conjunction with ENGL 51300. Typically offered Fall Spring.
