ECON 69300 - Bayesian Econometrics I

Credit Hours: 2.00 or 3.00.  This is a first course in Bayesian Econometrics. The overarching goal of the course is to expose students to the Bayesian perspective and get them up-to-speed with modern Bayesian simulation methods. Prerequisite: ECON 67100 or equivalent.
