VCS 23900 - Dentistry For Veterinary Technicians - DL

Credit Hours: 0.50.  This course is an introduction to veterinary dentistry for the technician, with emphasis on basic dental anatomy, pathologies and treatments, dental charting, intraoral radiography, dental cleaning and periodontal disease management. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

    0.500 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Identify tooth anatomy and perform dental charting.
2.  Recognize common pathologies and understand treatment approaches.
3.  Perform oral exams and dental cleaning.
4.  Produce diagnostic intraoral radiographs
5.  Develop and provide client education.
