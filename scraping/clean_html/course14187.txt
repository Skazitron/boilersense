ENTM 10100 - Insect Biology And Societal Grand Challenges

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Appreciate the scope of societal grand challenges.
2.  Understand and demonstrate where Insect Biology and grand challenges intersect.
3.  Evaluate and critique popular literature and media surrounding these issues.
4.  Identify gaps in our current knowledge and understand future needs.
