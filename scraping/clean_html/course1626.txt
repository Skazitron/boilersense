MGMT 62200 - Marketing Strategy

Credit Hours: 2.00 to 4.00.  (West Lafayette) 3.00 or 4.00 (Calumet) 3.00 (North Central) Key aspects of marketing strategy formulation and implementation are covered, including customer needs assessment, targeting, and positioning strategies in a competitive market. A managerial perspective allows development of decision-making skills necessary for successful marketing strategies. Prerequisite: MGMT 62000, 62100.
