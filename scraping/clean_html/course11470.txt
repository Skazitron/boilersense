PSY 32700 - Psychology Of Helping

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course is designed to provide a broad but rigorous, undergraduate-level comprehensive overview of contemporary scientific research on prosocial processes from the perspective of personality and social psychology. Typically offered Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Demonstrate a comprehensive mastery of the mechanisms and processes associated with prosocial behavior.
2.  Demonstrate an ability to think creatively about research on prosocial processes.
3.  Demonstrate an understanding of how research on prosocial behavior is planned and conducted.
4.  Demonstrate how prosocial processes are integrated with more general interpersonal processes and social relations.
