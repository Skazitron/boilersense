IET 59800 - Directed MS Project

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  A formal investigation of a particular problem under the guidance of the advisory committee.  Not applicable to a thesis option plan of study.  Enrollment during at least two consecutive terms for a total of three credits is required.  Permission of Instructor required.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Identify a business or industry relevant solution to a technology related problem
2.  Define and/or validate a business or industry relevant problem.
3.  Address a technological problem in a systematic and replicable manner.
4.  Effectively use technical/professional research and/or development procedures.
5.  Identify criteria for success/solution of the problem.
6.  Gather information appropriate to the problem by employing business research procedures.
7.  Document the research and development activity in a manner that permits replication and assessment of key decisions and alternatives.
8.  Write effectively in a form customary to business and industry, using APA format.
9.  Prepare and deliver a presentation in a form customary to business and industry.
