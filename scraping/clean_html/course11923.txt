CE 47900 - Design Of Building Components And Systems

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Design of simple floor and roof systems and load bearing walls; uses of building materials; fundamentals of design of metal form decking, steel joists, masonry (beams, columns and load bearing walls), and timber (beams, trusses, and mechanical connections).
