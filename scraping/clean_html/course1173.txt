AAE 65400 - Fracture Mechanics

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Methods of stress analysis in elastic media containing cracks. Fracture criteria. The strain energy release rate and J-integral. Crack tip plasticity. Interface cracks. Cracks in anisotropic solids. Bending of cracked plates. Dynamic crack propagation.  Prerequisite:  AAE 55300.
