ECE 41400 - Elements Of Electro And Fiber Optics

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Introduction to the use of lasers, fiber and integrated optical components and devices in communication and sensory applications. Topics include generation, transformation, modulation, deflection, and detection of laser beams, and their applications in fiber communication sensory systems.
