PTGS 33000 - Brazilian, Portuguese, And African Cinema

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Screening and analysis of selected films from Portuguese- speaking countries: Brazil, Portugal, Angola, Mozambique, Cape Verde, Guinea-Bissau, S. Tome and Principe, with discussion of their cultural contexts (colonial/post-colonial societies; cross-Atlantic relationships; racial diversity; minority/majority issues).  Knowledge of Portuguese not required.. Typically offered Summer Fall Spring.
