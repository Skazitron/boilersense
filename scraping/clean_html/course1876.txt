THTR 13300 - Survey Of Acting

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This is an introductory acting course for non-theatre majors. It is an exploration of the fundamentals of stage acting designed to enhance communication, creativity and collaboration. Class work includes acting exercises, theatre games and scene work. Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Become familiar with the techniques and practices of acting: vocal and physical techniques, analysis and interpretation.
2.  Understand the concepts of given circumstances, action, objective, obstacle, character and conflict, and utilize these in basic scene work.
3.  Communicate the play's idea through the medium of character in a scene from a published play.
