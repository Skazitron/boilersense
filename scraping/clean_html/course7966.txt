SLHS 34500 - Research Methods In Infancy And Childhood

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This is a laboratory-based course that focuses on methods to test childhood cognitive and language development.  As part of the course, students will be working in the Purdue University Infant Labs. Permission of instructor required.
