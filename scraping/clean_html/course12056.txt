EDCI 31101 - Integrating Media In The K-2 Classroom

Credit Hours: 2.00.  The goal of the course is for teacher candidates to understand how to choose and evaluate children's literature and media to use in K-2 classroom, develop ways to integrate literature and understanding of literature and media using a variety of methods, select appropriate materials for individual student's needs, and assess student's learning. Admission to Teacher Education is required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Evaluate both fiction and nonfiction resources for the use in classroom including identifying media which is broadly representative of the world in which we live using a variety of rubrics and evaluation forms.
2.  Determine cross-curricular connections, teaching ideas, and assessment methods of individual titles for use in the K-2 classroom.
3.  Develop materials (i.e., graphic organizers, vocabulary activities, comprehension supports) to use with literature which may be differentiated for individual learner's needs.
