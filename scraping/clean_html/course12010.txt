CE 67200 - Advanced Topics In Structural Engineering

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  A series of mini courses on special topics offered as CE 67000, CE 67200, etc. These special topic mini courses provide an opportunity for introducing students to topics of contemporary importance or special interest which fall outside the scope of the regular structural courses. Information about current offerings may be obtained from the schedule of classes or the structural engineering.
