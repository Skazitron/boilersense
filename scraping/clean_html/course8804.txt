ECON 51200 - Intermediate Economics II

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Course content includes money and banking, national income and aggregative economics; the analysis of the determination of national income, employment, the price level, and the balance of payments. Consideration of both theory and economic policy. Not open to students with credit in ECON 35200.
