SPAN 10500 - Accelerated Basic Spanish

Credit Hours: 4.00.  Accelerated Basic Spanish course that substitutes for SPAN 10100 and 10200.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Identify appropriate use of Spanish language in specific contexts by reading Spanish texts.
2.  Identify appropriate use of Spanish language in specific contexts by listening to audio recordings and watching videos of spoken Spanish.
3.  Compose Spanish sentences by focusing on a set of grammatical rules and standards in the language.
4.  Produce sentences in spoken Spanish in the form of dialogues and presentations.
