ENGL 60400 - Introduction To Inquiry In Second Language Studies

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Survey of a variety of approaches to inquiry (incl. hermeneutic, conceptual, historical, qualitative, and quantitative) available for second language studies to provide participants copies for design of their own research and scholarship.
