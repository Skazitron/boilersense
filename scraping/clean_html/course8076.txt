CPB 84100 - Use And Care Of Laboratory Animals II

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Priority will be given to students that have completed CPT 84000. This course teaches skills useful for the role of a laboratory animal veterinarian. Course is project-based and includes hands-on techniques in laboratory rodents.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Understand basics of rodent health monitoring, facility design and protocol review.
2.  Gain hands-on experience in handling rodents and performing techniques.
3.  Understand common research procedures in non-rodent species.
4.  Use critical thinking to work through clinical cases.
