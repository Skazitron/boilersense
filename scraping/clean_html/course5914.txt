ENGR 10301 - Introduction To Engineering In Practice

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Weekly small seminars led by faculty designed to introduce a specific topic, problem or discipline of engineering to First-Year Engineering students. Seminars are designed to help students explore a single area of engineering and/or assist in their decision of an engineering degree. Permission of department required. Typically offered Fall Spring.
