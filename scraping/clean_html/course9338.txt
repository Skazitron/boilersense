ECET 39000 - Lab Assistant In Electrical Engineering Technology

Credit Hours: 0.00 to 3.00.  This course allows a student to serve like an employee as an undergraduate teaching assistant in the laboratory, lab exercise/apparatus developer, or undergraduate researcher. Hours, subject matter, and credit to be arranged by faculty. Course content applies toward the EET Professional Experience requirement. Permission of instructor required. .
