ECE 64200 - Information Theory And Source Coding

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A treatment of the basic concepts of information theory. Determination of channel capacity and its relation to actual communication systems. Rate distortion theory is introduced, and the performance of various source codes is presented. Offered in alternate years.  Prerequisite: ECE 60000.
