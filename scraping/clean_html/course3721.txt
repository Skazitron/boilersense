CM 21000 - Construction Management Portfolio I

Credit Hours: 0.50.  Creation of a portfolio demonstrating developing and emerging levels of competence within the construction management discipline. Students will meet individually and in small groups with faculty mentors to review and critique progress.

    0.500 Credit hours
