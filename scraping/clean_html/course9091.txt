TECH 62800 - Technology Research And Use Of Data Analytics

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Examines concepts, models and methods useful for applying data analytics in business environments. Focusing on Hypothesis generation, the capturing, storage and expression of data, analysis for research and visualization. Permission of department required.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Enhance critical thinking abilities and analytical thought.
2.  Identify a variety of data types and storage strategies.
3.  Source, cleanse, join, and analyze a set of data yielding actionable insights that can be applied to research or business.
4.  Discuss aspects of the analytical thinking process and formulate individualized opinions surrounding the approach to data analytics.
5.  Gain a wide perspective on data analytics utilizing article reviews and up to the week content.
