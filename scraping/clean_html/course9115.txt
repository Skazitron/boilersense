VM 21000 - Small Animal Anesthesia-Clinical Mentorship-DL

Credit Hours: 2.00.  This mentorship will require the student to work with a small animal veterinary care facility to practice several tasks under the supervision of a clinical mentor.  This mentorship focuses on the principles of anesthesia from induction, maintenance to recovery.  Permission of department required.  Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Perform the basics of history and physical exam pertinent to anesthesia.
2.  Perform the basics of patient care before, during and after anesthesia.
3.  Perform the basics of proper record keeping for patients under anesthesia.
4.  Perform the basics of induction of general anesthesia in the small animal.
5.  Perform the basics of patient monitoring and recovery from anesthesia.
