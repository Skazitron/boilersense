AAE 55100 - Design Theory And Methods For Aerospace Systems

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Introduction to design theory, aerospace design process, design specification and requirements, concept generation and selection, design decomposition, improving designs, process design, and concurrent engineering. Design for assembly/manufacture. Projects allow for application and critical analysis of design methods.
