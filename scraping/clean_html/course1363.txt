CHNS 10700 - Chinese For Heritage Students

Credit Hours: 4.00.  A Chinese language course designed for students who have acquired some oral Chinese from home or other sources.  It capitalizes on students' heritage background to develop communication and comprehension skills, maintaining and vitalizing the linguistic and cultural proficiency already acquired.
