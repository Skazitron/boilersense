AT 38100 - Aviation Security

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course introduces the challenges and complexity of aviation security faced by aviation professionals across the industry.  It traces the evolution of current security approaches and explores technologies and processes targeting threat mitigation and improved operational efficiency.
