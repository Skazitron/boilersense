COM 61200 - Seminar: Special Topics In Interpersonal Communication

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Intensive study of selected topics, varying from semester to semester, from the theoretical and research literature of interpersonal communication. Topics may include communication models, information theory, systems theory, general semantics, sociolinguistics, etc., as they relate to the study of interpersonal communication.  Prerequisite: COM 51200.
