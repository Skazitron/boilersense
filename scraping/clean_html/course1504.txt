MGMT 44710 - Competitive Strategy

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course focuses on the dynamics between a firm and the marketplace including interaction with competition. Strategy implementation occurs over multiple periods and is highly dependent on choices and behavior of competition. The course material covers how these dynamics and expectations of competitor response will shape the decisions of managers. This is inclusive of signaling intent to competitors and mutual forbearance to avoid negative sum gain competition.
