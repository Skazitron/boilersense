ECE 60400 - Electromagnetic Field Theory

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Review of general concepts (Maxwell's equations, materials interaction, boundary conditions, energy flow); statics (Laplace's equation, Poisson's equation); distributed parameter systems (classification of solutions, transmission lines, and wave-guides); radiation and antennas (arrays, reciprocity, Huygen's principle); a selected special topic (e.g., magnetostatics, waves in anisotropic media and optical fibers).  Offered in alternate years.  Prerequisite: Master's student standing or higher.
