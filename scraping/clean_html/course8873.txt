ECON 68600 - Experimental Economics II

Credit Hours: 2.00 or 3.00.  Covers topics different from ECON 685, and they vary from year to year. May be taken prior to ECON 685. Course readings are primarily from journal articles.  Prerequisite: ECON 60700. 61000.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Identify research questions that are suitable for answering using economics experiments.
2.  Develop you own experimental designs to address specific research questions and hypotheses.
3.  Criticize and evaluate experimental research published in leading economics journals.
