Credit Hours: 2.00.  A continuation and refinement of the modern dance techniques and principles addressed in DANC 10100.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Learn basic anatomical, physiological and mechanical principles of the human body as they apply to movement for the intermediate level dancer. 
2.  Learn intermediate lever locomotor movement.
3.  Develop kinesthetic and muscular response to tempo and rhythm.
4.  Develop performance awareness of space and spatial design at the intermediate level.
5.  Develop an appreciation for modem dance.
