COM 49100 - Special Topics In Communication

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  Intensive study of selected topics, varying from semester to semester, from the literature or practice of communication. Course content will be drawn from areas not dealt with in the regular curriculum and may include such topics as photojournalism, economic reporting, and campaign communication. Permission of instructor required.
