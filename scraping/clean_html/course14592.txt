AFT 47200 - Air Force ROTC Leadership Laboratory VII

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Advanced leadership experience for introductory military training cadets. Cadets lead the planning, coordinating, and directing of military activities for the cadet corps; provide formal training and guidance to increase the understanding, motivation, and performance of other cadets. Prepare for active duty.
