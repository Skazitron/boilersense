TLI 30000 - Organizational Leadership Coaching

Credit Hours: 1.00.  This course is designed to help students to become results oriented through coaching. The course will discuss strategies on how to unlock potential, and maximize performance through results orientation and through facilitation. Attention is directed to facilitating coaching skills, emotional intelligence, and empowerment. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
