BMS 80600 - Clinical Anatomy Of Exotic Pets

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Consists of seven one-hour lectures and/or discussion periods and seven three-hour laboratory sessions. Course material is based upon clinical conditions of rats, mice, hamsters, guinea pigs, fish, etc., that have an anatomical basis or emphasis. Laboratory sessions include dissection, anatomical models, radiographs, and live animal examination.
