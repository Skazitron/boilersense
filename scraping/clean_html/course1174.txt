AAE 66600 - Nonlinear Dynamics, Systems, And Control

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Fundamental concepts and tools that are useful in the analysis of nonlinear systems and in the design of controllers and estimators for such systems. Results are illustrated by, and applied to, aerospace/mechanical systems.  Prerequisite:  AAE 56400.
