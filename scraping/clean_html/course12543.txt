FVS 33202 - Sports Video Production Winter/Spring

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 4.00.  This experiential course furthers the students' understanding of live sports video production. Lecture combined with various production experiences increase the students' proficiency with professional technology and equipment as well as the art and storytelling of the craft. Potential Men's and Women's Winter/Spring sports include Swimming & Diving, Wrestling, Basketball, Baseball, Softball, Tennis, Golf, or Track & Field. Typically offered Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Identify career options and job types within live sports video.
2.  Demonstrate an understanding of the technique and technology of the live camera.
3.  Demonstrate an understanding of the technique and technology of live audio.
4.  Demonstrate an understanding of the technique and technology of simple broadcast graphics.
5.  Demonstrate an understanding of storytelling in a live multi-camera production.
