ECE 66200 - Pattern Recognition And Decision-Making Processes

Credit Hours: 3.00.  (C S 662)  Introduction to the basic concepts and various approaches of pattern recognition and decision-making processes. The topics include various classifier designs, evaluation of classifiability, learning machines, feature extraction and modeling. Prerequisite: ECE 30200.
