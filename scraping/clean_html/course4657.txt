FR 10100 - French Level I

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A beginning French course with emphasis on communicative skills (listening and speaking), literacy skills (reading and writing) and culture. Permission of department required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Explain and produce biographical information about themselves and others in the French language.
2.  Identify necessary information by asking questions about people and things in the French language.
3.  Solve a simple survival situation such as ordering food and beverages in the French language.
4.  Compose with language by combining learned elements in the French language.
