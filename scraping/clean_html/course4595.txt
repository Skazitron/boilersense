PUBH 29500 - Internship & Career Preparation

Credit Hours: 1.00.  This course provides students with professional competencies in identification and development of professional resources that aid in a greater understanding and preparation for careers in public health. Upon completion of the course, students will be prepared to seek an internship and apply concepts learned to their professional development experiences. Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Define and conceptualize individual areas of study as a professional discipline, including its purpose, goals, trends, professional expectations, types of settings, etc. 2.  Identify procedures for internship preparation. 3.  Demonstrate the ability to develop a professional cover letter and resume.
