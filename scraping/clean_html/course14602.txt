SYS 59000 - Projects In Systems

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 6.00.  This is a Graduate Level variable title course for topics related to Systems (Systems Thinking, Systems Engineering, Systems Dynamics, etc). Graduate students and seniors can enroll in this course to do independent study or individual project with the instructor. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
