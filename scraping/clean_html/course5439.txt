NUTR 49600 - Evaluation Of Nutrition Science Research

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Evaluation of nutrition science research as it relates to health and disease. Typically offered Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Distinguish between data and interpretation through the practice of reading, analyzing, critiquing, and discussing nutrition science research articles.
2.  Access and integrate the contribution of multiple research studies to an area of nutrition science rresearch and its impact on health and disease.
3.  Explain, orally and in writing, the evaluation of nutrition science research.
4.  Identify and assess the validity of sensational nutrition science research presented in media.



Prerequisites:
