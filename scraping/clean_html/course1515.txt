MGMT 46600 - Project Management

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course seeks to introduce different phases of managing projects from conception to termination with particular emphasis on quantitative tools for planning, scheduling, resource allocation, monitoring, and control. In addition, topics such as risk management, quality management and procurement management will be covered. Students will also gain a working knowledge of MSProject software. Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Provide in-depth exposure to the use of project management tools and philosophies.
2.  Demonstrate a realistic experience using these tools to plan and control a project.
3.  Know basic steps in project planning: work breakdown, scheduling, resource allocation, information systems, and control.
