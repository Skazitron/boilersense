SPAN 48000 - Spanish Civilization

Credit Hours: 3.00.  The study of modern Spanish life, with special emphasis on social institutions and customs. Lectures in Spanish. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Locate and name the autonomous communities of Spain on the map.
2.  Distinguish the different languages spoken there from written samples and audio recordings.
3.  Explain in Spanish key differences and similarities between the regions through succinct summaries of historical, geographical, gastronomical, and political influences.



Prerequisites:
