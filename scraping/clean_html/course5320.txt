ECE 62900 - Introduction To Neural Networks

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Information processing with neural networks, biological and engineering implications, learning algorithms, current neural network models and architectures, implementational topics, applications in areas such as signal/image processing, pattern recognition, optimization, simulation, system identification, nonlinear prediction, communications, and control.
