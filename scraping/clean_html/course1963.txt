THTR 54500 - Directional Process In Production

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A study of models the director uses when dealing with the other creative personnel involved in production. Primary emphasis will be placed on the directorial and actoral processes as they relate to the work of the designers and technicians. Offered in alternate years. Permission of instructor required.
