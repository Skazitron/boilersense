EAPS 53300 - Atmospheric Physics II

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A continuation of EAPS 53200, covering the following selected advanced topics in atmospheric physics: gaseous absorption, Mie scattering, the equation of transfer, and radiative transfer in cloudy and hazy atmospheres. The application of radiative transfer in active and passive remote sensing. Advanced topics in atmospheric thermodynamics, such as heterogeneous systems, kinetic theory of gases, and planetary atmospheres. Basic ozone photochemistry applied to stratospheric and tropospheric pollution.
