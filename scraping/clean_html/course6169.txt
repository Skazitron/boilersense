VCS 63000 - Current Topics In Veterinary Clinical Sciences

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 6.00.  One-two hour sessions organized and conducted weekly by VCS residents, with input/discussion from the attending VCS faculty. Alternating weekly topic sessions may include journal club and morbidity and mortality patient rounds or other topics appropriate to the specialty. Permission of instructor required.
