COM 33700 - Advanced Digital Video Production

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Provides experience in writing program proposals and scripts, taping with small-format television equipment, and audio and video editing for various program formats. Special attention to editing, theory and technique, aesthetic considerations, and institutional and community cable outlets. Permission of department required.
