AT 38700 - Turbine Aircraft Simulation Lab

Credit Hours: 2.00.  This course is a laboratory flight experience in a turbine aircraft flight simulator emphasizing normal and abnormal aircraft operations.  Emphasis is on FAA-required flight procedures for pilot certification and safe operation of turbine powered aircraft.  Permission of department required.

Learning Outcomes: 
1. Demonstrate proficiency in flight planning, instrument flight procedures, and simulated aircraft operations.
2. Demonstrate proficiency in preflight, takeoff, departure, holding patterns, approach/missed approach, and normal landing procedures.
3. Demonstrate proficiency in operating all aircraft systems in both normal and abnormal situations.
