VM 20900 - Equine Medical Nursing Clinical Mentorship - DL

Credit Hours: 1.00.  This clinical mentorship focuses on the principles of handling, restraint, and equine nursing care.  Tasks to be documented include restraint and handling techniques, data collection, basic nursing care, and administration of medication and treatments.  Permission of department required.  Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Perform equine restraint and handling techniques.
2.  Determine age of horses.
3.  Perform placement and maintenance of indwelling catheters in horses.
4.  Perform data collection techniques in horses (TPR, etc.)
5.  Perform hoof care and grooming techniques in horses.
6.  Perform sample collection techniques in horses.
7.  Perform proper placement of a mouth speculum in a horse.
8.  Perform equine injection techniques.
9.  Perform application of leg and tail wraps in a horse.
