ENGL 40900 - Intermediate Fiction Writing

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Study and practice of methods of composing fiction, with a primary emphasis on the student's own work. Workshop criticism and discussion of published fiction. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1. Write and revise fiction by using effective character development, plot and scene.
2. Produce a polished fiction by revising according to suggestions received in workshop.
3. Be cognizant of the range of the genre by reading and discussing key works of modern and contemporary fiction.



Prerequisites: 

 Undergraduate level  ENGL 20500 Minimum Grade of C- or Undergraduate level  ENGL 30500 Minimum Grade of C-
