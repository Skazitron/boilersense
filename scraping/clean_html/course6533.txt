CSR 48600 - Retirement Planning And Employee Benefits

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Analysis of employee benefit plans, retirement needs analysis, and types of retirement plans including employer sponsored plans and participant directed plans.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Evaluate a client's retirement income and savings need.
2.  Explain important provisions of Social Security, The Affordable Care Act and Medicare.
3.  Differentiate between individual Retirement Arrangements including those used by individuals and small businesses.
4.  Know the rules relating to qualified retirement plans.
5.  Conduct an in-depth analysis of qualified plans (e.g. defined benefit, profit sharing, 401(k) plans, stock bonus plans, and ESOPs).
6.  Employ rules and strategies for appropriate retirement decumulation.
7.  Understand deferred compensation plans, stock option plans, and planning for executive compensation.
