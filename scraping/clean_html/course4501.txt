ARAB 30100 - Standard Arabic Level V

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Continued development of Arabic speaking, listening, reading, and writing abilities, using materials dealing primarily with the everyday life and civilization of Arabic-speaking countries, from various sources (newspapers, poetry, chapters from the Qu'ran). Conducted primarily in Arabic.
