VM 24500 - Management Topics for Vet Techs I -DL

Credit Hours: 1.50.  This course is the first of two courses that provide basic instruction in the concepts of a veterinary technician's role in the management of day-to-day operations of a veterinary clinic. This course will emphasize problems related to ethical and liability issues, medical records, inventory, marketing and fees, writing cover letters and resumes, and job interviews. Students must be currently working/volunteering at least 10-15 hours a week at a veterinary facility. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
