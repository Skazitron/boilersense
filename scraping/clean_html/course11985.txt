CE 57300 - Structural Dynamics

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Analysis of structural members and systems subject to dynamic loads such as wind and earthquake loads; basic theory for single-degree-of-freedom and multi-degree-of-freedom analytical models of civil engineering structures; free vibration, harmonic and transient excitation, foundation motion, resonance spectrum, Lagrange's equation, modal analysis, lumped parameter methods, computer methods.
