NUTR 34000 - Field Experience In Nutrition, Fitness And Health

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  Field experience in NFH-related setting that provides an opportunity for the application of the classroom experience.  For NFH majors only.  The student secures his/her own field experience, which must be approved by the NFH program coordinator in order to receive academic credit. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
