AD 61000 - Advanced Poster Design

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Specific principles and methods pertaining to poster design.  Explores the historical and social implications of poster design.  Practical and theoretical exercise will aid students in creative thinking and concept generation.  Prerequisite:  Admission to the M.F.A. program in Art and Design.  Permission of department required.
