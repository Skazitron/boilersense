AGEC 33000 - Management Methods For Agricultural Business

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Management of nonfarm, agriculturally related businesses. Topics include tools for management decision making, legal forms of business organization, basics of accounting, and important financial management techniques. Case studies and computer simulation game.
