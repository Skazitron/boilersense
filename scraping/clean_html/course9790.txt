POL 14100 - Governments Of The World

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An introduction to the politics and government in selected foreign countries. The course presents the tools and background needed to understand contemporary events in the world beyond the United States. Readings and discussions pay special attention to democratization and development. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
