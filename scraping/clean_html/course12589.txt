IET 31600 - Statistical Quality Control

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course introduces the application of statistical and probability tools to develop, implement, and maintain effective quality assurance in technology and service systems.  A systems approach to product or service quality from inception to disposal is employed.  Factors affecting variation in quality are studied.  The concepts and implications of quality from a global business environment are examined.  Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Describe the role and importance of quality planning, implementation, and control in manufacturing and service industries.
2.  Apply quality improvement tools to solve problems.
3.  Apply basic statistics to solve quality control problems.
4.  Construct and analyze various control charts.
5.  Calculate and apply fundamental probability concepts.
6.  Examine the role of "code of ethics" and ethical practices in business and industry.
