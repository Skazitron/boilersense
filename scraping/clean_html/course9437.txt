SFT 12000 - Foundations Of The Space Force II

Credit Hours: 1.00.  A survey of Space Force installation design and location, Space Force professions, principles of spaceflight and the space environment, geopolitics, defense policy and strategy and the Space Force's partner services in the Department of Defense. Emphasis is placed on helping students understand what they will experience as a Space Force officer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Explain the evolution of the Space Force from its beginning in the Air Force to the present-day.
2.  Describe the structure of the Space Force and its overarching mission.
3.  Apply basic orbital mechanics, properties of the space environment, and how they affect spacecraft.
4.  Communicate the capabilities of space operations and applications to joint functions.
5.  Demonstrate the fundamentals of communicating effectively.
