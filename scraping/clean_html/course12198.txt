EDCI 56400 - Integration And Management Of Technology For Learning

Credit Hours: 3.00. This course focuses on techniques and issues related to integrating technology within the educational process. Topics covered include integrating technology for: planning, instruction, record-keeping, and assessment for student learning. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
