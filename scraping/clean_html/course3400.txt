COM 27100 - Podcasting

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This directed study is designed as an introduction to podcasting. Students in the course will learn about the structure and development of podcasting campaigns, then create their own weekly recording. Recording begins in the third week of classes, giving students 14 opportunities to develop their program.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Develop an idea for a podcast.
2.  Write a treatment for a podcast.
3.   Pitch the idea for a podcast to an audience.
4.   Create a production outline.
5.  Produce a budget for the work produced and identify potential funding sources for the project.
6.  Execute an advertising campaign for a podcast.
7.  Coordinate a schedule for a podcast recording.
8.  Evaluate the finished production and the experience of creating that work.
