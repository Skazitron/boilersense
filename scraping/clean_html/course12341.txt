AGEC 61300 - Introduction To Economics Of Risk

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A background in expected utility theory and applications. EUH axioms, risk aversion, utility elicitation, expected utility and moments, stochastic dominance, mean-variance, risk free asset, diversification, single index model, increasing risk, production under risk, programming models and applications.  Prerequisite:  AGEC 55200, ECON 51100, STAT 51100.
