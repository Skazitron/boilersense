ENGR 33099 - Cooperative Education Seminar II

Credit Hours: 1.00 or 2.00.  This seminar is optional for Co-Op students when they are on-campus. In this course, students share their work experiences, receive feedback from the instructor and seminar participants, and continue their professional development. Prerequisite: Student must have completed at least two Co-Op work terms. May be repeated once after the fourth work term. Can be taken for two credits subsequent to completing two back-to-back work terms. Instructor permission required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
