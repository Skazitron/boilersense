AD 43400 - Professional Practice For Visual Communication Designers

Credit Hours: 2.00.  Specific professional practice topics for the visual communication designer. Topics include contracts, professional associations, ethics, portfolio and resume preparation, self-promotion and exhibition of creative work, the job market and the future of the profession. Field trips may be required.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Develop proficiency in the use of career search media and tools.
2.  Develop a practical understanding of the visual communications job market.
3.  Seek functional solutions that meet specific visual communication career goals
4.  Develop an expanded ability to generate contacts and leads for employment or graduate school.
5.  Strengthen student's design portfolio through review and organization of contents.
