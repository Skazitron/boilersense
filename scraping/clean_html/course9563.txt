HONR 29901 - Interdisciplinary Honors - Honors Mentors

Credit Hours: 2.00.  This hands-on course provides leadership training to the continuing Honors College students selected to work as mentors for the required, first-year honors course. Students work closely with a faculty instructor. Restricted to Honors College students. Department permission required. Typically offered Fall.
