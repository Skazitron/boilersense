AD 34300 - Northern Renaissance Art

Credit Hours: 3.00.  The development of a mature North European art originating in the International Gothic tradition and culminating in the works of Albrecht Durer, Jerome Bosch, and Pieter Bruegel, the Elder.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Be familiar with the history of the period in general and with major artists in particular.
2.  Show the ability to read and think critically about a work of art, to look carefully at a work and to discuss what he or she is seeing.
3.  Be able to describe clearly a work of art and to compare it to other works and artists of the period.
4.  Be able to write an explanation of how a work of art illustrates a particular theme or idea in clear and understandable prose with correct spelling and punctuation in paragraphs that lead logically from one to another.
5.  Show the ability to follow instructions and pay attention to detail in the preparation and presentation of research citing sources accurately.
