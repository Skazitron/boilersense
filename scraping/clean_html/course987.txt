EAPS 43300 - Synoptic Lab III

Credit Hours: 1.00.  (AGRY 44300) Diagnoses of mid tropospheric wave propagation and growth. Analysis of surface pressure fields and fronts and their relationships to upper air features. Extensive use is made of teletype and facsimile weather information. Prior course work in atmospheric dynamics and synoptic lab II is required.
