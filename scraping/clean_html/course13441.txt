EDPS 10500 - Academic And Career Planning

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Builds understanding of career interests and personality characteristics related to academic and career decisions. Expands knowledge of Purdue academic programs and of the world of work. Introduces decision-making strategies. Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Describe and explain multiple facets of your identity.
2.  Identify and analyze potential academic majors.
3.  Identify and analyze potential careers.
4.  Evaluate potential majors and careers in relation to yourself.
