EDPS 50002 - Foundations Of Licensure

Credit Hours: 1.00.  This course is designed to guide candidates to complete required documents needed to determine appropriate clinical practice placements(s) for candidates within unique employment situations. This course includes two process applications required of all employed candidates: 1) Clinical Practice Pre-Application Process and 2) Clinical Practice Application Process. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
