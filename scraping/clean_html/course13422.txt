AAS 27100 - Introduction To African American Studies

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Introduction to the philosophical and methodological principles underlying Afro-American studies.  Dimensions of the black experience, including history, education, politics, psychology, economics, religion, social organization, and art, will be covered, and the requisite academic tools and sources will be examined.
