ENGT 40600 - Entrepreneurial Capstone II

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This is the second of two courses in the entrepreneurial capstone project sequence. Project management and system engineering methods are applied to solving innovation and commercialization problems associated with the development of potentially successful products. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
