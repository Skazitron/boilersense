BME 55300 - Biomedical Optics

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Optical methods applied to address biological and biomedical problems. The course includes two parts: fundamentals of optical microscopy and advanced techniques that are currently used to probe and/or manipulate cell and tissue samples. Permission of instructor required.
