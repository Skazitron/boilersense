SPAN 48100 - Spanish Culture

Credit Hours: 3.00.  The development of the cultural life of the Spanish people as reflected in the geography, history, music, art, and architecture of Spain. Lectures in Spanish. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

    3.000 Credit hours


Syllabus Available

      Northwest- Hammond

      West Lafayette


Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Catalogue and describe in Spanish the major historical epochs of Spain.
2.  Correctly identify major works of painting and music, and locate them temporally, geographically, and by cultural movement or period.
3.  Debate accurately Spain's current regional conflicts through knowledgeable assessment of the historical evolution of the language, literature, commerce, and agriculture.



Prerequisites: 

 Undergraduate level  SPAN 30200 Minimum Grade of D- or Undergraduate level  SPAN 30800 Minimum Grade of D-
