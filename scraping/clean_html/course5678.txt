LALS 40200 - Directed Reading In Latin American/Latino Studies

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 6.00.  Directs the reading of students with special interests in Latin American or U.S. Latina/o studies. Guides students in profitable reading in subjects of their own choice. Individual conferences; no class meetings. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
