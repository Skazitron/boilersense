ENGL 38100 - The British Novel

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Survey of British novels ranging from the eighteenth- to the twenty-first centuries. Typically offered Fall Spring.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1. Demonstrate knowledge of a range of topics and literary conventions related to the British novel.
2. Discuss those topics and conventions in relation to their diverse historical, cultural, and aesthetic contexts.
3. Produce well-reasoned written arguments about British novels based on appropriate use of textual evidence.
