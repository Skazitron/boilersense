ECE 49401 - Professional Communication Capstone

Credit Hours: 1.00.  This course provides ECE students an opportunity to refine their professional communication skills before launching into the workplace or graduate school. Topics include: ethics in ECE, workplace communication (written and verbal), and plans for professional growth.

    1.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Communicate effectively with a range of audiences. 
2.  Function effectively on a team whose members together create a collaborative and inclusive environment, plan tasks, and meet objectives. 
3.  Ability to make informed judgments based ethical and professional responsibilities in engineering situations, giving consideration to global, economic, environmental, and/or societal contexts.
