TLI 48595 - Organizational Leadership Capstone II

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course builds upon the pre-requisite capstone course in which students engage in theory-based applied learning as well as team project-based learning and learning in context. Students will work with organizations to address various organizational challenges in which they will integrate their organizational skills and demonstrate their leadership competencies. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
