MGMT 41250 - Fixed Income Securities

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course includes examination of fixed income securities institutions that invest, roles in institution, portfolios and math associated with assessing characteristics of fixed income securities. Typically offered Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Examine management of fixed income securities.
2.  Examine portfolios and risk management strategies.
3.  Demonstrate use of simulation models for risk assessment.
