MGMT 66100 - Management Of Operating Systems

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 4.00.  A project course in which students are exposed to a variety of operations problems as they arise in a real world setting. The focus of the course is problem formulation, analysis, and proposal(s) of an implementable scheme for their resolution.  Prerequisite:  MGMT 66000. Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Gain an increased ability to identify and analyze issues in operations management.
2.  Develop implementable solutions.
3.  Enhance understanding of how specific projects relate to larger business goals.
4.  Improve professional skills, including teamwork, client communication, and project management.
