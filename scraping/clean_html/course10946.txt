CHE 61000 - Advanced Chemical Engineering Thermodynamics

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Properties of pure fluids and mixtures are described in the context of classical and statistical thermodynamics. Equations of state and solution theories are developed for this description of fluid phase equilibrium and chemical equilibrium.
