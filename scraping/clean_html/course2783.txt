RUSS 49800 - Topics In Russian Culture

Credit Hours: 3.00 to 6.00.  This course provides a sustained examination of some aspect of Russian culture or a topic relevant to Russian cultural studies. Topics may include Russia Confronts the East; The Caucasus: An Introduction; Popular Culture in Russia. Typically offered Fall, Spring.

    0.000 TO     6.000 Credit hours

Repeatable for Additional Credit: Yes - May be repeated for a maximum of 9 credits


Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Attain an in-depth understanding of a particular movement, feature, or aspect of Russian culture.
2.  Improve knowledge of Russian history and society as it pertains to cultural development.
3.  Increase ability to read critically and write persuasively.
4.  Become acquainted with the resources available for further pursuit of topics in Russian cultural history.
