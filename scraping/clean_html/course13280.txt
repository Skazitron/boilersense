LA 11700 - Graphic Communication In Design II

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A course in graphic communication theory focusing on digital skills and processes. Project based instruction will be used to simulate design office procedures through 2D and 3D exercises using raster and vector software.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Prepare students for internship and professional practice through office-ready digital workflows.
2.  Develop and refine a personal graphic style along with a critical "designer's eye" through formal presentation and discussion.
3.  Analyze a problem and determine the best tool(s) for the task while pursuing graphic innovation by knowing the "why" and "how" of digital tools.



Prerequisites:
