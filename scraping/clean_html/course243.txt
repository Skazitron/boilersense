ENGL 38200 - The American Novel

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A survey of American novels ranging from the eighteenth century to the twenty-first-centuries. Typically offered Fall Spring.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Demonstrate knowledge of a range of topics and literary conventions related to the American novel.
2.  Discuss those topics and conventions in relation to their diverse historical, cultural, and aesthetic contexts.
3.  Produce well-reasoned written arguments about American novels based on appropriate use of textual evidence.
