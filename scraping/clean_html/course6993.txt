BCHM 64000 - Metabolic Plant Physiology

Credit Hours: 3.00.  (AGRY 64000, BIOL 64000, BTNY 64000, HORT 64000)  Topics include photosynthesis, respiration, carbohydrate and lipid metabolism, and nitrogen, sulfur, and secondary metabolism. This course is the third in a series of core courses in the Purdue Plant Biology Program graduate curriculum. Prerequisite:  BCHM 56100, BTNY 55300 or HORT 55300, Prerequisite:  BIOL 55100 or HORT 55100.
