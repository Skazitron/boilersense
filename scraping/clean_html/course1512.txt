MGMT 46300 - Supply Chain Analytics

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Supply Chain Analytics focuses on data-driven and rigorous decision making in supply chain management. It is a complete problem solving and decision making process, and integrates a broad set of analytical methodologies that enable the creation of business value. MGMT 30600 is highly recommended but not required. Basic Calculus, Statistics, and Excel literacy are required. Typically offered Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Gain analytical insights on major supply chain tradeoffs.
2.  Know supply chain functions: demand forecasting, procurement, inventory, transportation, pricing, contract, revenue management, reverse supply chain and integration of multiple functions.
3.  Know a systematic set of analytical methodologies to use effectively in their future careers.
