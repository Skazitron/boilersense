AGR 11600 - Introduction To Botany And Plant Pathology Academic Programs

Credit Hours: 0.50.  An introduction to the academic programs offered in the Department of Botany and Plant Pathology. Topics include, but are not limited to undergraduate plans of study, courses, experiential programs, internships, student organizations, career opportunities, academic policies, scholarships, and student services. Course meets during weeks 1-8.
