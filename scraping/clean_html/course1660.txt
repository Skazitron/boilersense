MGMT 65420 - Leadership

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  Examines organizational leadership responsibilities, forms of leadership, the acquisition, use, and consequences of power, critical leader skills, and how corporate environments shape leadership. Emphasis on self-discovery and development of managerial leadership knowledge and skills.  Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Increase conceptual understanding of key leadership principles and strategies utilized within formal organizations.
2.  Have deepening knowledge regarding important interpersonal relationship skills and abilities.
3.  Help students discover important insights into oneself as a leader (including strengths and weaknesses/opportunities for personal growth).
