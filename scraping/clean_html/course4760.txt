CNIT 14100 - Internet Foundations, Technologies, and Development

Credit Hours: 3.00.  (CGT 14100) This course explores the history, architecture and development of the World Wide Web. Current tagging and scripting languages are covered in a tool independent environment. Topics also include authoring tools, design, graphic and multimedia formats, and commerce, implementation and security issues. PC literacy required.
