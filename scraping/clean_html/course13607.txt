EDPS 62700 - Career Assessment Practicum

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Students acquire training and experience with the administration and interpretation of various inventories related to career decision-making, including career interest inventories, personality tests, and academic achievement tests. Students will also gain experience in report writing and providing clients with feedback on their assessment results. Prerequisites: EDPS 50500. Permission of instructor required. Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Develop advanced knowledge of the administration, scoring, and interpretation of career-related assessments.
2.  Demonstrate an enhanced ability to synthesize findings across different types of assessments.
3.  Understand cultural and individual differences in the career assessment process.
4.  Understand the unique developmental issues associated with exploration of career interests during adolescence.
