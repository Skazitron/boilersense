ENGL 50900 - Advanced Fiction Writing

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Study and practice of advanced methods of composing fiction, with the primary emphasis on the student's own work. Workshop criticism and discussion of published writing. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

    3.000 Credit hours


Syllabus Available

Levels: Graduate, Professional, Undergraduate 

Schedule Types: Lecture

Department: English

May be offered at any of the following campuses:      

      West Lafayette


Repeatable for Additional Credit: Yes - May be repeated an unlimited number of times


Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Write and revise fiction, using all the skills from English 40900 but at a more proficient level.
2.  Produce polished fiction revised according to suggestions received in workshop and feedback from the instructor.
3.  Acquire a broader knowledge of the context of contemporary fiction by reading and discussing work by several contemporary fiction writers.
