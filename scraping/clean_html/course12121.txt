EDCI 42700 - Teaching Of World Languages

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This is one of the two methods courses required for World Language Teaching major. While LC 47500 is a theoretical course, this course is a practical course on world language teaching in secondary school. Typically offered Fall.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Design lesson plans that draw on knowledge of content areas.
2.  Write language learning assessment documents (diagnostic formative, and summative).
3.  Design language assessment rubrics.
4.  Evaluate their own and others' teaching effectiveness.
5.  Plan lessons that incorporate strategies to monitor student learning.
6.  Build collaborative relationships with other language teaching professionals.
7.  Identify resources for the developing foreign language teaching, including textbooks, scholarly journals, pedagogy associations and conferences.
