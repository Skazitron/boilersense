CPB 63100 - Avian Immunology

Credit Hours: 2.00.  The avian immune system is significantly different than the mammalian immune system. This course compares and contrasts mammalian and avian immune systems. Prerequisites: At least one graduate or undergraduate course in biology or poultry science. Permission of instructor required.
