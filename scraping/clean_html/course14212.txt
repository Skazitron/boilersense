ENTM 30100 - Experimentation And Analysis

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Introduction to experimentation and quantitative data analysis in the life sciences with a focus on examples from insect biology. Typically offered Fall.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Start with an observation and design appropriate experiment that recognizes and avoids common pitfalls.
2.  Apply the general linear model and its variants in data analysis.
3.  Appropriately use common basic statistical packages.
