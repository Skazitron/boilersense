DANC 24000 - Dance Composition

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An introduction to the theory and practice of the principles and utilization of choreographic tools; movement exploration, manipulation of basic dance elements, development of movement themes, and application of compositional dance forms.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Learn the systematic application of choreographic tools which will allow them to create a coherent dance composition.
2.  Increase awareness for dance composition and understand its value and meaning as both a creator and observer.
3.  Learn to discuss and critique dance choreography.
