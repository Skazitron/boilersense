SPAN 30200 - Spanish Level VI

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Further work to develop speaking, listening, reading, and writing abilities in Spanish on the basis of materials dealing with the ideas and events that have shaped the present-day Spanish-speaking countries. Conducted primarily in Spanish. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.   Develop and expand intermediate-advanced proficiency in Spanish oral communication skills through discovery in various media, presentations, group work, and interpersonal communication.
2.   Expand and refine their written proficiency in Spanish by producing a variety of essays, project, and other written assignments.
3.   Expand and enhance their knowledge of the diverse Spanish-speaking world and their cross-cultural proficiency through the use of aural, visual, and written texts in Spanish.
