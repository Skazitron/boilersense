DANC 23000 - Biomechanics Of Dance, Movement and Strength

Credit Hours: 2.00.  This course will provide conceptual and experiential understanding of biomechanical approaches to dance technique. Through studio, lecture, movement exercises and assignments, the students will learn to perform all types of dance and movement with ease, strength and economy of motion.
