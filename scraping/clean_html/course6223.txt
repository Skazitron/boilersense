VCS 82700 - Small Animal Imaging

Credit Hours: 2.00.  A continuation of VCS 81300. Specific diseases of small animals where imaging is part of the diagnosis are discussed. Emphasis is on radiography and ultrasonography. The use of alternate imaging modalities is discussed where appropriate. Typically offered Spring Summer.
