CGT 59700 - Directed MS Capstone

Credit Hours: 3.00 to 6.00.  An original investigation of a particular problem under the guidance of the instructor(s). The resulting product is a substantial project report with an evaluation or research component. Not applicable to a thesis option plan of study. Enrollment for a total of six credits is required. Permission of department required.

Learning Outcomes: 
1. Independently identify a problem or opportunity and conduct a substantial project relevant to the student's focus area.
2. Iteratively evaluate project outcomes using appropriate research methods.
3. Communicate project process, outcomes, and relevant forms of evaluation through written and oral outputs.
