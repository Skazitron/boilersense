Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Identify fundamental functions of a sound system, explain their operations and demonstrate a basic ability to manipulate their characteristics in simple audio productions.
2.  Produce music and audio recordings that demonstrate an ability to meet basic audio technical and production standards.
