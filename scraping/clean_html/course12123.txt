EDCI 42900 - Methods Of Teaching Physical Education In Secondary Schools

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Application of instructional concepts and strategies in physical education field experience setting. No undergraduate students may be enrolled in any of these undergraduate courses until they have been admitted to teacher education. Typically offered Fall.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Select an appropriate instructional model for a teaching situation.
2.  Correctly implement an instructional model for a unit of instruction.
3.  Create a unit of instruction using an instructional model and considering all relevant contextual factors.
4.  Determine the impact of one's teaching on student learning.
5.  Write developmentally appropriate lesson plans that utilize an instructional model and focus on student learning.
6.  Reflect on one's teaching in order to enhance student learning.
7.  Become knowledgeable about professional organizations and websites related to physical education.
8.  Demonstrate professional behaviors related to physical education and teaching.
