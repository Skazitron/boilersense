THTR 61400 - Classical Text II

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Continuation of Classical Text I, with emphasis on studio scene work beginning with Shakespeare and progressing through the works of other major classical playwrights. Permission of instructor required.  Prerequisite: THTR 61300. Typically offered Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Develop and demonstrate the application of metrical analysis. 
2.  Identify the poetic devices in classical text and demonstrate these in performance.
3.  Demonstrate critical thinking skills of analysis, interpretation and application. 
4.  Analyze Shakespeare's characters in reference to their historical period. 
5.  Develop a systematic approach for performing classical text. 
6.  Identify classical and poetic language through a variety of authors.
7.  Develop the ability to enhance the performance of dialogue using vocal orchestration.
