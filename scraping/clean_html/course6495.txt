CSR 22200 - CAD For Apparel Pattern Design

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Study of computer-aided-design for apparel pattern making, pattern grading, and marker making for the manufacture of apparel and other sewn products. Operation of CAD systems from Gerber Technology AccuMark PDS & GMS, and PDM (Product Data Management) are incorporated in this course.
