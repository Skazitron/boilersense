THTR 27100 - Interpreting The Play Script

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course introduces several approaches to the creative interpretation of a play script, giving students experience in applying both analytical methods and sensory-intuitive lenses of interpretation. Typically offered Fall.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Interpret your sensory-intuitive responses to a script.
2.  Demonstrate an understanding of the world of the play.
3.  Analyze key structural components of a script.
4.  Communicate your interpretation through multiple means (written, oral, visual, etc.).
