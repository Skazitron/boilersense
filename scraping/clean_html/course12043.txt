EDCI 27000 - Introduction To Educational Technology And Computing

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  Addresses fundamentals of educational technology, including the integration of instructional design, media, computers and related technologies within the classroom setting.

    0.000 TO     3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Demonstrate the ability to use various forms of technology to access, generate, and manipulate information; as well as to present and communicate results.
2.  Given the background information and resources for a potential lesson, identify the instructional activities, methods, and media that should optimally be selected, implemented and evaluated within the lesson.
3.  Apply educational technology to plan and deliver instructional lessons that integrate a variety of software, applications, and learning tools.
4.  Identify issues (e.g., equity, privacy, security, plagiarism, isolationism) that have been produced or enhanced because of the integration of technology and describe how these issues can be effectively addressed.
