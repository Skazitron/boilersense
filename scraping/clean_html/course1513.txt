MGMT 46400 - Logistics: Concepts And Models

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Studies the role of transportation and distribution channel, and the relations with other supply chain supply activity such as inventory control. Topics include forecasting, logistics system, facility location, distribution strategy, inventory control, supply chain contract, and so on. Typically offered Fall Spring.
