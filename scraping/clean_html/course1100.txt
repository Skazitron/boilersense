AAE 36100 - Introduction To Random Variables In Engineering

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Events, sample space, probability, conditional probability, independence, random variables. Probability mass functions, density functions, and cumulative distribution functions. Derived probability density functions. Bernoulli, Poisson, and normal processes. Applications to engineering problems. Some fundamental limit theorems.
