LC 51900 - Teaching Of Foreign Languages

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A basic course designed to provide a forum for ideas connecting theory and research to teaching practice.  Explores issues related to how learning and teaching can be enhanced and presents ideas that can be implemented in the classroom. Permission of instructor required.
