ECE 67600 - Academic Oral Communication For International Research Students

Credit Hours: 0.00.  Discussions of and activities related to such topics as appropriate nonverbal communication; learning styles; strategies for effective speech and listening; and speech acts for the classroom, office, and laboratory. Individual weekly tutoring sessions to improve speech comprehension. Prerequisite: Master's student standing or higher and Electrical & Computer Engineering majors only. Permission of department required.
