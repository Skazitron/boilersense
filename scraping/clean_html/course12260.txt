EDCI 67000 - Learning Design And Technology Integrated Project

Credit Hours: 1.00 or 2.00.  Current issues and problems in educational technology. Projects in current practice and research selected and pursued. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring.

    1.000 OR     2.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Develop an electronic version of your competency portfolio based on artifacts as evidence.
2.  Compose narratives that explain how your artifacts demonstrate each competency.
3.  Develop an updated resume or vita suitable for job searching.
4.  Understand the differences between competency and professional portfolios.
