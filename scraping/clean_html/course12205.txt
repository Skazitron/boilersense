EDCI 57300 - Instructional Development Practicum

Credit Hours: 2.00 or 3.00.  Provides supervised field experience in programs involving instructional design development activities. Students participate in ongoing projects in the design, development, and evaluation of instructional materials and training programs in business and industry, medical facilities, or other settings deemed appropriate. Recommended prerequisites:  EDCI 56900, EDCI 57200, and EDCI 67200. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Design, develop, and evaluate a major instructional design project within a professional work setting/environment.
2.  Create and manage an instructional design project within a professional setting following a specific timeline. Management of the process may require collaboration with supervisors, subject matter experts, and others to complete the project in the most effective manner.
