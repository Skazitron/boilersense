NUPH 49200 - Basics Of Nuclear Pharmacy Practice

Credit Hours: 1.00.  This course emphasizes some of the daily tasks performed by nuclear pharmacists when working in the nuclear pharmacy.  Typically offered Fall.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Understand the fundamentals of radiation and radiation safety.
2.  Know the principles of instrumentation used in nuclear pharmacy and understand the use of these instruments as related to daily practice.
3.  Understand the theoretical foundations of the profession of nuclear pharmacy.
4.  Meld theory and abilities in the practice setting.
5.  Develop a basic knowledge base to enable a student to practice the profession ( ex. summer internships).
