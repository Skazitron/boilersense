IET 44275 - Global Transportation And Logistics Management

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A study of the various aspects of logistics. The development, implementation, and control of physical transportation systems, product distribution, warehousing, and inventory policy models will be emphasized. A working knowledge of third and fourth party logistics and transportation strategies will be analyzed. The impact of logistics and transportation in the global environment will be discussed. Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Describe the role of logistics and its relationship to other functional areas of business within a single firm and within the supply chain.
2.  Explain various management and control techniques in logistics.
3.  Apply statistical principles to evaluate the relative worth of cost-savings initiatives in the logistics industry.
4.  Describe how enterprise resource planning (ERP) systems are used to manage logistics and transportation functions.
