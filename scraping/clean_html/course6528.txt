CSR 42300 - Apparel Portfolio/Professional Development

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Exploration of employment-seeking skills including cover letters and resume writing, networking, job-seeking organization and interview practice. Creation and development of various forms of computer generated art for the portfolio including fashion illustration, textile design, story boards, and line brochures as well as stationary and business card design.
