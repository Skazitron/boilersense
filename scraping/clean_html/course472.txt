ENGL 60111 - Introduction To Scientific, Technical, Medical, And Healthcare Writing

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Introduction to Scientific, Technical, Medical, and Healthcare Writing offers an overview of the genres, conventions, and strategies that these professional fields have in common. This course is the gateway course into the online Professional Certificate and Masters in Scientific, Technical, and Medical Communication. Permission of department required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Identify the written, spoken, and media genres that are common to scientific, technical, medical, and healthcare industries. 
2.  Explain how genres and conventions are used in professional workplaces within a variety of industries. 
3.  Demonstrate how common communication strategies can allow a writer, speaker, or media user to explain scientific, technical, medical, and healthcare concepts and to persuade others. 
4.  Develop a common genre set that is applicable to the needs of a particular workplace within a broader scientific, technical, medical, and healthcare industry.
5.  Evaluate existing communication practices at a workplace or within an industry and make recommendations for improvements.
