ENGL 25700 - Literature Of Black America

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A survey of literature written by black American authors. Close attention is paid to the history of black literature and to the historical context in which it was written, as well as to the texts of major works by black writers. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
