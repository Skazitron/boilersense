HTM 24100 - Managerial Accounting And Financial Management In Hospitality Operations

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course teaches decision making using managerial accounting, financial analyses, and time value of money. Consideration will be given to systems, techniques, information types, and Excel as used by hospitality management. Emphasis on situations oriented to the hospitality industry.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Analyze financial statements to evaluate company performance and make recommendations.
2.  Utilize cost behavior to make pricing decision.
3.  Prepare financial budgets and conduct variance analysis.
4.  Prepare the statement of cash flows and develop strategies for cash management.
5.  Use time value of money to make investment decisions.
