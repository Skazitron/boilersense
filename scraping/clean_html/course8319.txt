ANTH 41800 - Field Methods In Cultural Anthropology

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 9.00.  Introduces basic field methods in Cultural Anthropology. Topics may include ethnographic or other interviewing techniques and methods of inquiry into any of the broad topics covered by Cultural  Anthropology. Permission of department required.
