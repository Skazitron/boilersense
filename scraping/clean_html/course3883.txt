AT 45000 - Airline Transport Flight

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Course provides flight and academic preparation for the airline transport pilot certificate flight check. Applicant must have successfully completed the Airline Transport Pilot written examination meet federal aviation regulation requirements for the flight check. Permission of department required.
