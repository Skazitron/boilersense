CE 51200 - The Comprehensive Urban Planning Process

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An introductory course that provides a framework for better understanding of the current urban planning process. Concepts and emerging trends are covered as well as an elementary description of planning methods and techniques. For planning majors and those in related design, development, and socioeconomic disciplines.
