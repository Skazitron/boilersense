ENGL 49000 - Worksite Internship Practicum

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  Course facilitates the transition between an English undergraduate degree and the workplace or professional life. The course has two components:  a professor-guided component and a practicum component in a chosen area. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
