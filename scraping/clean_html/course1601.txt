MGMT 59100 - Launching Global Leaders

Credit Hours: 0.00 to 2.00.  Capitalizing on the unique personal and professional backgrounds of each student in our program, the Launching Global Leaders course provides a comprehensive and customizable leadership development curriculum. In this year long course, students will engage in a 360 assessment of strategically identified professional competencies critical to success in the contemporary workforce. Using these target areas as a guide, students will then customize their leadership development experience by selectively engaging in a variety of program offerings, including: interactive industry simulations, personal and team performance enhancement tools, professional coaching, interactive learning sessions with industry leaders, and workshops in developing intercultural competency, personal branding and career management. Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Comprehensively assess their individual leadership strengths and areas of development along the Launching Global Leaders competency model.
2.  Gain exposure to a variety of leadership styles and strategies from successful industry leaders.
3.  Gain an awareness of contemporary issues facing leaders of global organizations, and strategies for future.
4.  Gain exposure to tools and concepts currently utilized in industry that inspire continuous self-improvement.
