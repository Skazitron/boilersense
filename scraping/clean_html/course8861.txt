ECON 65600 - Labor Economics II

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course is the third in the PhD Labor Economics sequence. This course is designed to be primarily a "topics" course. Class will focus on some of the most popular open questions in labor economics, however, we will take a long view to these questions in order to understand the progress that has been made over the last half-century.
