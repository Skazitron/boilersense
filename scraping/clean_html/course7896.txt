ANSC 31100 - Animal Breeding And Genetics

Credit Hours: 4.00.  Genetic principles and their applications in improvement of production efficiency in livestock.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Explain how decisions relative to selection and mating systems to result in animal improvement.
2.  Discuss mating systems and impact of systems on inbreeding and heterosis.
3.  Understand the impact of heritability, accuracy of selection, and intensity of selection.
