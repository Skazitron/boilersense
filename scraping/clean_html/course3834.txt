AT 34500 - Aviation Marketing

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course is an introduction to the principles of marketing with emphasis on aviation-related industries. It demonstrates the practical and managerial applications of balancing an organization's objectives and resources against needs and opportunities in the marketplace.
