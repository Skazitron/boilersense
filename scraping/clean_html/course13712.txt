SPAN 40100 - Spanish Level VII

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Advanced work on development of Spanish speaking, listening, reading, and writing abilities, focusing on materials dealing primarily with culture and the arts in the Spanish-speaking countries. Conducted primarily in Spanish. Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Respond critically in Spanish to a movie from a Spanish speaking country, paraphrase its plot, and discuss the major themes by writing an essay on a specific topic.
2.  Appraise, assess and compare different sources, by researching a culture topic in Latin America and making a 10-15 minute presentation in Spanish to the class.
