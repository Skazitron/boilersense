MGMT 53500 - International Law For Managers

Credit Hours: 2.00.  Today's managers work in a highly international business environment. That creates an atmosphere where US laws interact and collide with other countries and international law. In this class we will explore contract, marketing, intellectual property, employment law and more in the international context. We will use case studies and headlines to see how these issues effect real businesses. When you leave, you will be prepared to help lead your employer in the international environment while staying on the right side of the law. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Know about the law of the international contracting environment.
2.  Know the global principles that apply to managing employees in an international legal context.
3.  Know about marketing legal issues in the international context, specific human resources management techniques (or methodologies), and apply some of the techniques.
4.  Know important intellectual property legal principles in the international context.
