ENGT 18100 - Engineering Technology Applications

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Basic electrical, electronics, mechanical, and process laboratory skills are introduced, including simple troubleshooting techniques and safety practice. Relevant engineering technology projects are emphasized. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Operate safely in a lab environment.
2.  Use standard lab instrumentation to take measurements accurately.
3.  Communicate relevant experimental results.
4.  Complete an engineering technology team project.
