MFET 41000 - Introduction To Additive Manufacturing

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course covers topics in additive manufacturing (AM) technologies. Topics include the AM benefits and drawbacks of additive as compared to subtractive manufacturing, additive manufacturing process and material selection, design for additive manufacturing, support structure generation, printing process planning, and quality control techniques, such as monitoring, inspection, and surface modification. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Describe the concepts, benefits, and application of additive manufacturing.
2.  Select appropriate additive manufacturing processes and material for producing parts.
3.  Design 3D objects for additive manufacturing considering the process requirements.
4.  Develop strategies and practices incorporating appropriate quality control techniques.
