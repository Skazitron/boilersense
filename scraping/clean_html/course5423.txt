NUTR 43600 - Nutritional Assessment

Credit Hours: 2.00.  Nutrition assessment in humans, including anthropometric, biochemical, clinical, and dietary assessment. Typically offered Spring.

    0.000 OR     2.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Explain, compare and contrast techniques used to assess the nutrition status of individuals and groups in clinical, community and research settings.
2.  Utilize the nutrition care process to obtain, verify and interpret data, and ultimately, to make decisions about the nature and cause of nutrition-related problems.
