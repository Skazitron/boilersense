ECON 67200 - Econometrics II

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  Topics include generalized method of moments, estimation and inference with weak instruments, panel data models and bootstrap methods. Prerequisite: ECON 67100.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Identify important econometric issues of interest in practical scenarios. 
2.  Demonstrate the application of state of the art methods to relevant economic questions. 
3.  Outline a broad picture of the subject by logically connecting the various topics. 
4.  Criticize the use of inadequate methods through discussion of published work.
