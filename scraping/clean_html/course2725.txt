TLI 36100 - Engineering And Technology Education Instructional Planning And Evaluation

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course addresses selecting content, writing instructional objectives, planning lessons, preparing a unit of study, and evaluating student progress. Emphasis is placed on evaluating
instruction and student achievement to include developing valid, reliable testing instruments as they relate to measuring student achievement in the cognitive, affective, and psychomotor domains.  Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Demonstrate instructional planning skills related to the delivery of engineering/technology education to both middle-level and high school students.
2.  Prepare lesson plans, a unit of study, and evaluation instruments.
3.  Appraise instructional material effectiveness and propose revisions for improvement.
4.  Prepare appropriate assessment instruments that align with identified learning objectives.
