FR 20500 - Accelerated Intermediate French

Credit Hours: 4.00.  French 205 is an accelerated intermediate-level course designed to develop language proficiency in French.  The course will help students progress toward competence in speaking, listening, reading and writing, as well as toward knowledge of French and Francophone cultures.  The course focuses on active language use.  Students will interact meaningfully with classmates and develop communicative strategies.  Course will be taught in French. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Identify appropriate use of French language in specific contexts by reading French texts.
2.  Identify appropriate use of French language in specific contexts by listening to audio recordings and watching videos of spoken French.
3.  Compose French sentences by focusing on a set of grammatical rules and standards in the language.
4.  Produce sentences in spoken French in the form of dialogues and presentations.
