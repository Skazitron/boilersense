AT 34001 - Aerospace Business Statistics

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course provides an introduction to business statistics with a specific focus on managerial decision making in the aerospace industry. The course will focus on aircraft leasing and value determination. Topics include descriptive statistics, probability models, estimation, hypothesis testing, and regression analysis. Students use software to perform their own analyses.
