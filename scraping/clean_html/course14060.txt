Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Build expertise in problem-solving and expanding a brand visually based on market research. 
2.  Practice synthesizing research, critical dialogue (feedback), and Critiques within an iterative design process.
3.  Develop skills to construct/combine graphic form using various digital tools, software, and screen-based media as well as print and 3D objects.  
4.  Create system designs and brand identities. 
5.  Strengthen attention to detail and craft knowledge. 
6.  Strengthen ability to pitch ideas and create viable solutions for real-world clients.
