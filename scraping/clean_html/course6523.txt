CSR 40600 - E-Retailing

Credit Hours: 3.00.  To provide understanding of the technological and retail infrastructure that underlines doing retailing over the internet. Build an understanding of business strategies over the net. To identify management issues and consumer issues in e-retailing.

    0.000 OR     3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Understand the technological and retail infrastructure that underlies doing retailing over the Internet.
2.  Learn the vocabulary to discuss e-retailing.
3.  Build and understand business strategies over the Internet.
4.  Acquire power to exploit any business opportunities that jump out your way.
5.  Provide the ability to get your hands dirty and use some of the most important net marketing tools.
6.  Understand the "business case" for Internet commerce in retailing (or other business).
7.  Identify management issues in e-retailing.
8.  Understand the consumer issues in e-retailing.
9.  Acquire skill sets that allow a competitive market advantage in interviews, jobs and careers.
