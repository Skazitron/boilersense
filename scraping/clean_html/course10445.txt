MET 45200 - Advanced GD&T Concepts Applied To Product Quality

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course explores the theory and application of Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing (GD&T) to measurement systems and the programming necessary to insure product quality. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Develop a comprehensive understanding of GD&T standards and callouts and how they apply to product quality.
2.  Develop and demonstrate a comprehensive understanding of the critical components, capabilities, and software of CMMs and similar product measurement systems.
3.  Apply uncertainty principles to measurement data to determine critical parameters associated with CD&T controls of product quality.
