CHNS 22400 - Chinese Level IV Business Chinese

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Practical reading, writing, speaking, and listening comprehension skills directed towards use of Chinese for business purposes. Course materials cover daily business dealings as well as national and international trade, living conditions, environmental, cultural, and social problems.
