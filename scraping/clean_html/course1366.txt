CHNS 20700 - Intermediate Chinese For Heritage Students

Credit Hours: 4.00.  An intermediate level Chinese language course which capitalizes on students' heritage background for further development of communication and comprehension skills.  It maintains and further vitalizes the linguistic and cultural proficiency that has already been acquired.
