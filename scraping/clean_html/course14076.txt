AD 39600 - Art Museum Practices

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course examines art museums as cultural institutions with varied collections, serving an important social role. Museum administration and operation are discussed, as are exhibition preparation and museum architecture; a few field trips are required. Museum careers also are explored.
