AFT 11000 - Heritage And Values I

Credit Hours: 1.00. A survey course designed to introduce students to the United States Air and Space Forces and provides an overview of the basic characteristics, missions, and organization of the Air and Space Forces.

    1.000 Credit hours


Syllabus Available

Levels: Graduate, Professional, Undergraduate 

Schedule Types: Distance Learning, Lecture 


Offered By: Polytechnic Institute 

Department: Aerospace Studies 


Course Attributes: Credit By Exam, Dept Credit, Lower Division, Military 


May be offered at any of the following campuses:      

      West Lafayette


Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Describe general aspects of the Department of the Air Force.
2.  Describe leadership fundamentals for Air Force Officers.
3.  Identify service benefits and opportunities for officers serving in the Air Force.
4.  Explain the history and evolution of the United States Air Force and Space Force.
5.  Demonstrate fundamental written and verbal communication skills.
