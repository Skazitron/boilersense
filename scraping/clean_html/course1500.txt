MGMT 44433 - Leading And Working In Teams

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course will explore the forces that affect behavior in teams, and the factors that lead to team success. We will use a variety of readings, cases, activities, and class discussions to build concrete skills related to leading and working in teams. The course will explore the specific challenges associated with leading teams, some of which include: building and designing teams, managing information exchange within and across teams, structuring effective group decision processes, igniting creative thinking, enabling complex problem solving, managing team diversity and conflict, and managing systems of teams.
