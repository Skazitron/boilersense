POL 40300 - Field Experience In Political Science

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 9.00.  Students are able to earn credit for internships in federal, state, and local government offices as well as with political parties, campaign organizations, nonprofit organizations, legislative bodies, and interest groups. Credit and course requirements to be arranged with the instructor. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
