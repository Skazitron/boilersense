GER 42400 - Business German

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A study of the terminology and techniques used in commercial transactions, including the interpretation and writing of business materials. Development of the four language skills, with emphasis on writing and speaking. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Learn and practice vocabulary and cultural knowledge that is applicable to business situations in German-speaking countries.
2.  Compare business practices in German-speaking countries with those in the United States, and will be able to discuss the similarities and differences in German.
