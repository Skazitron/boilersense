GRAD 61000 - Grant Training Program I

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  This variable topic course is designed to be used by any department at WL needing to offer a training program associated with research grants such as those required by certain NIH, NSF or other grant agencies or programs. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
