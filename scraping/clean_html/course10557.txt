GER 10100 - German Level I

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A beginning course in German reserved exclusively for students who have had less than two years of German at the ninth-grade level or above. Students with two years or more of previous German study may not take this course for credit. Permission of department required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Identify appropriate use of German language in specific contexts by reading German texts.
2.  Identify appropriate use of German language in specific contexts by listening to audio recordings of spoken German.
3.  Identify appropriate use of German language in specific contexts by watching videos of spoken German.
4.  Compose German sentences by focusing on the set of grammatical rules and standards in the language.
5.  Produce sentences in spoken German in the form of dialogues and presentations.
