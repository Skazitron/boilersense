BIOL 44207 - Exploration Of Protein Structure

Credit Hours: 1.00.  This module is a hands-on exploration of the principles of enzyme structure. Students will use computers to retrieve and inspect protein structures from publicly available databases. Through the performance and analysis of sequence structural alignments, students will explore the relationship between primary, secondary and tertiary structures and the final active form of a protein.
