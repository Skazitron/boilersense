VCS 60500 - Supervised Surgical Laboratory Training In Small Animal Surgical Techniques And Approaches

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Small animal neurosurgical, orthopedic and soft tissues approaches, techniques, and procedures practiced in cadaver labs. Only graduate students enrolled in an American College of Veterinary Surgeons or American College of Veterinary Internal Medicine-Neurology/Neurosurgery residency will be eligible. Permission of Instructor required.

    1.000 Credit hours

Repeatable for Additional Credit: Yes - May be repeated an unlimited number of times


Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Funnel different existing surgical techniques from literature and textbooks into a patient detailed approach.
2.  Assess recent literature reports of surgical techniques.
3.  Gain proficiency in surgical techniques and skills.
4.  Become comfortable handling specific surgical equipment.
5.  Provide exposure to infrequently performed techniques.
6.  Learn methods/techniques to troubleshot certain intraoperative complications.
