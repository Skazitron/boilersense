PUBH 57100 - The Business Of Healthcare

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course is designed to help you keep pace with the rapidly changing world health and healthcare. We will (1) explore the drivers of value creation, valuation approaches, and the differences in capital structures between nonprofit and for-profit healthcare providers; (2) examine horizontal consolidation and integrations among nonprofit and for-profit providers mergers and acquisitions, join ventures, and vertical consolidation and integration between providers and payers; and (3) discuss the ethos of healthcare, disruptive innovation, and emotional intelligence.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Understand the drivers of value creation, valuation approaches, and differences in capital structures between nonprofit and for-profit healthcare providers.
2.  Evaluate horizontal consolidation and integrations among nonprofit and for-profit providers mergers and acquisitions, join ventures and vertical consolidation and integrations between providers and payers.
3.  Understand the ethos of healthcare and role of disruptive innovation and emotional intelligence in the advancement of healthcare delivery.
4.  Analyze the role of healthcare value creation and its connection with hospital ownership and the disruptive innovation in healthcare.
5.  Evaluate the intersection of public health, population health, and the business of healthcare delivery.
