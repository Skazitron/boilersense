ECE 38200 - Feedback System Analysis And Design

Credit Hours: 3.00.  In this course, classical concepts of feedback system analysis and associated compensation techniques are presented. In particular, the root locus, Bode diagram, and Nyquist criterion are used as determinants of stability.
