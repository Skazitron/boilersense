AGEC 68500 - Advanced Quantitative Methods For Decision Making Under Uncertainty

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Explores key areas of risk management of the food and agricultural firm, including price, production, strategic, regulatory, technology, market/competitor, political, financial, and exchange rate risk. Contemporary tools, such as score carding, decision trees, and real options are introduced for quantifying and managing uncertain decisions. Permission of department required.
