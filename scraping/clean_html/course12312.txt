AGEC 45100 - Applied Econometrics

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Application of strategies to economic problems. Simple and multiple regression, dummy variables, logit analysis, time series, and forecasting.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Learn how to estimate and interpret linear regression models.
2.  Learn how to test hypotheses based on regression coefficients.  
3.  Learn how to develop forecasts abased on regression models.
