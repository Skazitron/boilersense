ECET 49700 - Project Design And Development, Phase II

Credit Hours: 1.00 or 2.00.  This conclusion of the design project begun in ECET 49600 emphasizes system integration and testing. The course concludes with a formal demonstration of and oral presentation on the finished product and a written report on the final design.
