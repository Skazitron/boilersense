BMS 80300 - Applied Anatomy Of The Dog And Cat

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Consists of seven 50-min lectures and/or discussion periods and seven 150-minute laboratory sessions, plus a comprehensive final exam. This course builds on basic anatomical knowledge and explores, in depth, the anatomy relevant to medical and surgical conditions of the dog and cat. Laboratory will utilize anatomical models, cadavers as animal models, electronic resources, and limited use of live animals for clinical examinations.
