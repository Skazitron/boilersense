COM 49101 - Boiler Communication

Credit Hours: 1.00.  This course provides a practical, hands-on approach to public relations by working in student-led teams to produce comprehensive, strategic communication campaigns for actual clients. The course also includes many opportunities for professional development and portfolio building.

Learning Outcomes:

2.  Develop professionally to be able to start a public relations career.
3.  Use creativity and critical thinking to help clients with public relations needs while engaging in social responsibility.
