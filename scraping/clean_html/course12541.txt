FVS 33200 - Live Events Video Production

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 4.00.  This experiential course furthers the students' understanding of live video production. Lecture combined with various production experiences increase the students' proficiency with professional technology and equipment as well as the art and storytelling of the craft. Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Identify career options and job types within live event production.
2.  Demonstrate an understanding of the technique and technology of the live camera.
3.  Demonstrate an understanding of the technique and technology of live audio.
4.  Demonstrate an understanding of the technique and technology of simple broadcast graphics.
5.  Demonstrate an understanding of storytelling in a live multi-camera production.
