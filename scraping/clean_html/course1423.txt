MGMT 29450 - Leadership Development To Bridge Gender Divide

Credit Hours: 1.00.  This seminar offers students the opportunity to hone your ideas about leadership and gender while reflecting on your own leadership goals by engaging the ideas of gender in the workplace. Students in the seminar will attend talks and events with visitors to campus and the course, whose work you will engage on numerous levels, from preparatory research to final reflection. The goal of this seminar is to launch you on your own leadership path by allowing you to analyze and reflect upon the pathways that other leaders in gender and the workplace. You should plan on attending some events outside of class time as part of your effort for the course.
