ENGL 36000 - Gender And Literature

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An introduction to feminist approaches to the study of literature, including poetry, drama, fiction, and/or autobiography. Examines how gender intersects with race, ethnicity, sexual orientation, and class in shaping authorship, reading, and representation. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
