CGT 32600 - Graphics Standards For Product Definition

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Introduction to product data exchange and interoperability standards, ANSI and NIST product documentation standards, product modeling standards, methodologies for technology selection, implementation, and evaluation. This course supports the product archival and communication processes in a PLM environment.
