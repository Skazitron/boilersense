EDCI 64500 - Organization And Administration Of Career And Technical Education

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Organizational structure and delivery systems at the national, regional, state, and local levels; program areas and services; legislative initiatives; federal, state, and local planning; administrative functions and practices; leadership development; trends; and issues.  Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Develop an understanding of the organizational structure and delivery systems at the national, regional, state, and local levels in career and technical education (CTE).
2.  Provide an overview of the CTE program areas, career clusters, career pathways, and services and their relationships to academic and special education.
3.  Review federal and state legislation related to CTE.
4.  Examine federal, state, regional, and local planning and funding activities.
5.  Analyze administrative functions and practices and management systems.
6.  Critique leadership and staff development initiatives in CTE.
7.  Evaluate current trends and issues in the field.
