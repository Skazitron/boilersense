AGR 12000 - Introduction To Horticulture And Landscape Architecture Academic Programs

Credit Hours: 0.50.  An introduction to the academic programs offered in the Department of Horticulture and Landscape Architecture. Topics include, but are not limited to undergraduate plans of study, courses, experiential programs, internships, student organizations, career opportunities, academic policies, scholarships, and student services. Course meets during weeks 1-8.
