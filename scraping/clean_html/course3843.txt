AT 36500 - Instrument Flight Instructor Flight

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Flight and ground training in preparation for the Instrument Flight Instructor Flight Test is given. Instructional techniques are demonstrated and practiced by the student. Analysis of maneuvers and common student errors also included.  FAA Flight Instructor Airplane Pilot Certificate and Flight Instructor Instrument written exam passed. Permission of department required.
