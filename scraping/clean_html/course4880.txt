CNIT 56500 - Information Security Management

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Information Security Management explores the issues surrounding the alignment of business and information security. More precisely, once the context of information security is understood in terms of the business and the problems with the current state of Information Security Management are identified, the course pursues solutions to those problems in terms of people, policy, process, procedures, and technology. The product produced as a result of this pursuit is an Enterprise Security Architecture, mapping business needs to technology implementation. The enterprise security architecture produced is dynamic and maintainable, thereby assuring traceability and accountability. Permission of instructor required.
