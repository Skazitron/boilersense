SPAN 39800 - Special Topics In Spanish

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  Studies of particular aspects of Spanish (e.g., culture, civilization, literature, linguistics, film, art, politics, etc.) by examining a varied selection of works.  Readings, discussion, and papers in Spanish.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Recognize and apply a broad range of hospitality and tourism-related vocabulary.
2.  Read, understand, and discuss specific hospitality and tourism topics in Spanish.
3.  Write texts in different styles using appropriate structures and vocabulary.
4.  Hold a conversation about specific problems in hospitality and tourism in Spanish.
