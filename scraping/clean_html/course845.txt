IE 37000 - Manufacturing Processes I

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Principal manufacturing processes; metal cutting, grinding and metal forming operations, machine tools, and tools and tooling. Nontraditional machining and welding. Introduction to computer-aided manufacturing and computer-aided graphics and design, N/C programming, robots, and flexible manufacturing systems. Classroom and laboratory demonstrations included. Not open to students with credit in ME 36300. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
