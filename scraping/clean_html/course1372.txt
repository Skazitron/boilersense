CHNS 28500 - Chinese Calligraphy

Credit Hours: 1.00.  Principles and techniques of writing Chinese characters. The aesthetics of Chinese calligraphy, its function as an art form, and its role in Chinese culture. Emphasis on training and practice in calligraphic writing. Conducted in English.
