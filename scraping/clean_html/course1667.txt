MGMT 66000 - Introduction To Operations Management

Credit Hours: 2.00 to 4.00.  As goods and services are produced and distributed, they move through a set of inter-related operations or processes in order to match supply with demand. The design of these operations for strategic advantage, investment in improving their efficiency and effectiveness, and controlling these operations to meet performance objectives is the domain of Operations Management. The primary objective of the course is to provide an overview of this important functional area of business. Prerequisite:  MGMT 67000, 67100.
