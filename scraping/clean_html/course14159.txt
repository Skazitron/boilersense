AD 60600 - Problems In Visual Design

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Problems relating to printing and digital technology, motion design, computer graphics, perception, and the development of visual communication prototypes.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Study the roles, impacts, and topics in poster design.
2.  Examine the various techniques for image generation.
3.  Strengthen students' conceptual skills.
4.  Engage students' creative solutions through practical and theoretical exercise in poster design and display.
