BIOL 54100 - Molecular Genetics Of Bacteria

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Advanced bacterial genetics, with emphasis on the use of genetics as a powerful and creative intellectual activity that enables us to discover biological functions and to construct new organisms by the manipulation of DNA. Major topics include: mutations, genetic selections, recombination, regulatory mechanisms, and genomic evolution.
