AAE 51800 - Low-Gravity Fluid Dynamics

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Theory and design for control of liquid propellants in weightlessness. Gravity and orbits review. Satellite and space-probe acceleration environments. Capillary-dominated fluid dynamics: contact angle, surface tension, curvatures, and solution methods. Industry practice and current topics.
