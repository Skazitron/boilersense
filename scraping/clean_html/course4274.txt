MUS 13300 - Music Theory II

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A variety of styles and forms of music serve to exemplify melodic and harmonic processes and voice-leading practices in diatonic tonal music. Activities include analytic reading of musical scores, developing musical listening skills, and acquiring functional piano techniques.
