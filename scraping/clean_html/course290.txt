ENGL 42000 - Business Writing

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Workplace writing in networked environments for management contexts. Emphasizes organizational context, project planning, document management, ethics, research, team writing. Typical genres include management memos, reports, letters, e-mail, resumes (print and online), oral presentations. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Create written documents that use persuasion and emphasize audience and the ethical dimensions of workplace communication.
2.  Manage written projects using appropriate technologies and documentation.
3.  Design documents that meet audience expectations, including clear descriptions, procedures, and visual content.
4.  Collaborate effectively including working online, determining roles, managing conflicts, providing feedback, and responding constructively to obstacles.
5.  Produce written documents that triangulate sources of evidence and use appropriate research methods.
6.  Know and use multiple appropriate literacy technologies.
