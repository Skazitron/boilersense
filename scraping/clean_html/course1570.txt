MGMT 53700 - Persuasive Communications

Credit Hours: 2.00.  The purpose of this course is to work with you to improve your professional communications specifically as those communications relate to client and external communications. We will focus on oral and written communications theory, techniques, and practice. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Acquire skills in writing professional business documents including business memos, business letters, group papers, and PowerPoint slides.
2.  Develop and present elevator pitches for their career development.
3.  Gain skills to research and craft an intensive analysis regarding a marketing plan for Fortune 500 companies.
4.  Enhance personal presentation skills via elevator pitches, impromptus, and group presentations.
