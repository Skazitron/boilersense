AAS 37700 - African American Sexuality And Society

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course focuses on the history of ideas, approaches, and paradigms that have shaped and defined our understanding of Black sexuality through engaging stereotypes, symbols, and images that have influenced the sexual attitudes and behavior of African Americans.
