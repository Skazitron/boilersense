EAPS 38500 - Principles Of Engineering Geology

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Application of geology to evaluation of design and construction problems relating to dams, highways, tunnels, and reservoirs; review of construction material sources and their utilization. A field investigation is required. Prior course work in mineralogy, petrology or geology for engineers is required.
