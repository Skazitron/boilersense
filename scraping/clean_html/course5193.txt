ECE 47000 - Curricular Practical Training

Credit Hours: 0.00.  An electrical and/or computer engineering work experience. This internship experience is intended to complement the student's academic plan-of-study and help prepare him/her for his/her future role as a practicing engineer.  A letter from the prospective employer stating the period of employment, hours per week, job title, job qualifications, and job minimum period of employment is required. This course may not be taken in successive semesters. Permission of department required.
