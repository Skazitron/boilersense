HIST 46700 - The Emergence Of Modern America

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Examines the changes in American society between 1877 and 1932. The course covers such issues as the rise of industry, the growth of consumerism, the shift to a multi-ethnic society, imperialism, Populism, Progressivism, World War I, and the 1920s.
