COM 67400 - Seminar: Special Topics In Organizational Communication

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Intensive study of selected topics, varying from semester to semester, from the theoretical and research literature of organizational (including business and industrial) communication; analysis of recurring communication problems in complex organizations; critique of research findings and methodologies.
