THTR 51400 - Vocal Production For The Stage II

Credit Hours: 2.00.  Continuation of Vocal Production for the Stage I. Emphasis on exercises in vocal exploration through application to performance. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Recognize the anatomy of the voice production mechanism.
2.  Classify and apply to text the International Phonetic Alphabet and the Lessac System.
3.  Identify any unnecessary vocal tensions to develop a clear open vocal instrument.
4.  Develop a flexible vocal instrument, enabling the voice to respond to a wide variety of emotional and vocal stimuli.
5.  Evaluate your personal vocal growth and establish a routine for continued vocal health. 
6.  Apply and practice Lessac's three vocal Energies through performance.
7.  Develop an exercise support system for the actor to use in his/her professional life.
8.  To develop personal awareness of your vocal skill and the ability to analyze others.
