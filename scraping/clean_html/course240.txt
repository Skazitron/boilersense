ENGL 37900 - The Short Story

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A study of the development and features of the short story genre. Typically offered Summer Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1. Demonstrate knowledge of a range of topics and literary conventions related to the short story genre. 
2. Discuss those topics and conventions in relation to their diverse historical, cultural, and aesthetic contexts.
3. Produce well-reasoned written arguments about short stories based on appropriate use of textual evidence.
