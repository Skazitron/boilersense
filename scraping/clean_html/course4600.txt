PUBH 38000 - Public Health Policy

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course will provide the opportunity to examine and analyze the financing, organization and delivery of health care in the U.S. and how these core elements are shaped and influenced by health care policy and decision-making.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Describe the role of policy, law and government relations in health care and public health.
2.  Explain how federal, state, and local health policy is made.
3.  Identify and critique the difference between population health and individual health.
4.  Explain the policy interests between the public good (public health) and individual rights.
5.  Explain the organization of the health care system, including principles and examples of health insurance and health care finance.
6.  Describe health care policy formulation and implementation.
7.  Explain and apply health policy principles to the policy topics discussed in class.
