EDCI 61500 - Qualitative Research Methods In Education

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Focuses on expanding graduate students' research skills to include knowledge of the theories and methods associated with qualitative and qualitative-quantitative combined research. Prerequisite:  EDPS 53300. Typically offered Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Increase knowledge of, analyze and comparatively evaluate the theoretical tenets and multiple traditions of qualitative research.
2.  Increase knowledge of, understand and evaluate the multiple methods of inquiry used in qualitative research.
3.  Understand the ethical and personal issues and concerns involved in qualitative research.
