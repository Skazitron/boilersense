EDCI 50900 - Writing In Middle And Secondary Schools

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Focuses on understanding philosophical and theoretical approaches, such as the writing process model, current reading/writing research, and how various approaches fit into existing school curriculum. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Trace recent history of secondary writing instruction while rethinking conventional theoretical perspectives such as the writing process model.
2.  Identify the influence of a variety of theories on pedagogical trends over the last several decades.
3.  Examine and evaluate an array of instructional and professional development materials for the teaching of writing.
