LC 36100 - Sound And Form In Language

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Examination of the nature of sound systems (phonology) and form systems (morphology) of language, as well as techniques of phonological and morphological analysis, using material from various languages.  Central focus on phonetic and phonological structure, and on inflectional and derivational morphology. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.
