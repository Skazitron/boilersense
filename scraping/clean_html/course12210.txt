EDCI 58600 - Topics In Educational Reform

Credit Hours: 3.00.  (EDFA 58600) Explore important and timely issues related to educational reform and restructuring. Topics include: school choice, curriculum reform, assessment, technology, and influences on the reform process. Typically offered Fall Spring.

    3.000 Credit hours


Syllabus Available

Schedule Types: Distance Learning, Lecture 


Offered By: College of Education 

Department: Curriculum and Instruction 


Course Attributes: Upper Division 


May be offered at any of the following campuses:      

      West Lafayette Continuing Ed

      West Lafayette


Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Explore and develop awareness of important and timely issues related to educational reform and change.
2.  Increase knowledge of important and timely topics in educational reform theory and practice.
3.  Develop skills of critical thinking and reflection in relationship to important and timely topics in educational reform theory and practice.
