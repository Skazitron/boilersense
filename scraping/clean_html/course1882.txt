THTR 15003 - Introduction To Rigging For Theatre

Credit Hours: 1.00.  An introduction to rigging techniques and equipment for theatre and live entertainment.  Permission of Instructor required.  Typically offered Fall.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Identify and demonstrate the proper use of single-purchase counterweight systems.
2.  Identify and demonstrate proper use of PPE (personal protective equipment) for rigging.
3.  Identify and demonstrate safe usage of typical static rigging equipment (basket, sling, turnbuckle, etc.).
4.  Demonstrate proper installation and safe usage of chain motors and truss rigging.
5.  Calculate safe working loads.
