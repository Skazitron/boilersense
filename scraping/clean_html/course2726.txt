TLI 36700 - Teaching Design And Innovation I

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course focuses on the development of appropriate engineering design knowledge and skill required to effectively teach middle and high school engineering/technology education courses by infusing engineering design into the curriculum. Students will use 3-D solid
modeling software, rapid prototyping, and other design technologies as they engage in the engineering design process through an open-ended engineering design challenge.  Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Display the ability to teach students to develop skills for living in a knowledge-based, technological society using design-based pedagogical approaches.
2.  Use the engineering design process to solve open-ended, ill-defined problems.
3.  Demonstrate the ability to teach basic engineering design techniques.
