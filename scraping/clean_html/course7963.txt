SLHS 30300 - Anatomy And Physiology Of The Speech Mechanism

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course provides an introduction to the anatomical and physiological bases of the speech mechanism with an emphasis on the neural, respiratory, laryngeal, and supralaryngeal subsystems.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Develop knowledge of the major subsystems underlying speech and language production.
2.  Identify major anatomical structures associated with speech production.
3.  Describe the physiology of speech production.
