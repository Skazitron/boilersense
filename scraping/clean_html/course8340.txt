ANTH 50600 - The Development Of Modern Anthropology

Credit Hours: 3.00.  The ways and kinds of thinking about the human species in pre-nineteenth-century Europe: nineteenth- and twentieth-century developments in Europe and North America; the central scientific paradigms; professional societies and journals; and national/international anthropologies will all be covered.
