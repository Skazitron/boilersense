EDCI 54900 - Assessment In STEM Education

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course is designed to help teachers of STEM recognize the link between productive assessment and productive instruction, using the STEM education standards for teacher competence in educational assessment. This course is designed to help teachers meet those professional standards and understand the public pressure as well as instructional need for effective formative and summative assessment.

Learning Outcomes: 
1. Choose appropriate assessment methods.
2. Develop appropriate assessment methods.
3. Administer scoring and interpreting assessment results.
4. Use assessment results to make decisions about individual students, plan teaching and develop curriculum.
5. Communicate assessment results to students, parents, and other educators.
6. Recognize inappropriate assessment methods and uses of assessment information.
