ECE 52800 - Measurement And Stimulation Of The Nervous System

Credit Hours: 3.00.  (BME 52800)  Engineering principles addressing questions of clinical significance in the nervous system:  neuroanatomy, fundamental properties of excitable tissues, hearing, vision, motor function, electrical and magnetic stimulation, functional neuroimaging, disorders of the nervous system, development and refinement of sensory prostheses.
