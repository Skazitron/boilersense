ECET 52500 - Applications In Forensic Engineering Technology

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An interdisciplinary consideration of the applications of forensics in the American jurisprudence system. Topics include: failure analysis of electrical and mechanical systems, accident reconstruction, product liability, codes and standards, ethics, and expert witness testimony.
