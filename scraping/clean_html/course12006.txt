CE 61500 - Statistical And Econometric Methods II

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Advanced statistical and econometric methods as applied to engineering-related problems extending the techniques covered in CE 61400. Topics include: seemingly unrelated regression, three-stage least squares, generalized extreme value models, nested logit models estimated by full information maximum likelihood, random parameters (mixed) logit models, models with fixed and random effects, and zero-inflated count-data models. Prerequisites:  CE 61400.
