ENGL 37700 - Modern And Contemporary Poetry

Credit Hours: 3.00.  A study of poetry in English from the twentieth century to the present. Typically offered Summer Fall Spring.

    3.000 Credit hours

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Identify the form and analyze the significances of modern and contemporary poems.
2.  Discuss modern and contemporary poems in relation to their historical, cultural, and aesthetic contexts.
3.  Produce well-reasoned and written arguments about modern and contemporary poems.
