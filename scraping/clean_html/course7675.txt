BIOL 61100 - Crystallography Of Macromolecules

Credit Hours: 3.00.  The special techniques required in the structure determination of biological macromolecules. Symmetry of macromolecules. Data collection and processing. The isomorphous replacement technique. The molecular replacement technique. Use of anomalous dispersion. Restraint and constraint refinement. Computational techniques. Prerequisite:  BIOL 51100. Permission of instructor required.
