MET 27300 - Study Abroad For Innovators

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Students will learn the rich history of inventions and technologies of the country, from early ages to the present. An Engineering Challenge activity provides students an opportunity to design and build a product. Students also learn and experience culture including arts, sports and sporting events, food and drinks, and politics. Lectures and field visits are required. Permission of department required. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Describe unique aspects of the country's culture, politics, sports, food, and arts.
2.  Demonstrate an understanding of cultural norms, interactions, societal ethics and the impact of culture on innovation, business, and industry.
3.  Describe the evolution of technological advancements, understand their working mechanisms, learn their evolution, and understand their impact on modern day life.
