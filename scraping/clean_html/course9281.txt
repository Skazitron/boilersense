ECET 29801 - Laboratory Teaching Aide In Electrical Engineering Technology

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  This course allows a student to serve as an undergraduate teaching aide in the laboratory. The student will work with and support a course instructor in the undergraduate teaching labs. Hours, subject matter, and credit to be arranged by faculty. Course content applies toward the EET Professional Experience requirement. Department and Instructor permission required.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Explain and communicate lab steps clearly.
2.  Recognize problems and implement troubleshooting techniques.
3.  Demonstrate operation and use of test equipment.
