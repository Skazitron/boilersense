PUBH 60700 - Public Health Practicum

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course integrates public health theory and practice in a professional setting. Students have the opportunity to apply concepts from core and concentration courses, conduct projects, and interact with a range of professionals in the designated setting. Permission of instructor required. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes:
