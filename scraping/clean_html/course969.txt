EAPS 39000 - Geologic Field Methods

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Introduction to geologic mapping principles on quadrangle (7 1/2 min., 15 min., 1:250,000 scales) maps. Application of aerial photography, GPS, and GIS techniques. Emphasis on construction of cross sections, measurement of stratigraphic sections, and preparation of geologic reports. Several weekend field exercises will be required.
