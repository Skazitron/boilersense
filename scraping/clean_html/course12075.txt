EDCI 35001 - Tanzania Seminar

Credit Hours: 1.00.  In this course, we will prepare for the Maymester in Tanzania study abroad program in a variety of ways, including investigating the history and culture of Tanzania, beginning to learn Kiswahili, conceptualizing service learning experiences, and collaborating on a plan for program logistics. Typically offered Spring.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Critically explore common conceptions of Africa.
2.  Investigate the history and culture of Tanzania.
3.  Describe the educational system of Tanzania.
4.  Read, write, and speak basic phrases and sentences in Kiswahili.
5.  Conceptualize and engage in service learning experiences.
