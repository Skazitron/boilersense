ENGR 39697 - Global Engineering Projects

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00. Students will work in teams to formulate solutions to engineering problems. Project topics will focus on a problem outside the U.S. and/or incorporate teammates from international universities. International teams may work remotely with the possibility for international travel and in-person collaboration. Permission of instructor required.
