COM 64400 - Strategic Personal Branding

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This graduate course will examine the theories and processes of personal brand development and self-marketing. The course will examine best practices, trends, and creative strategies used in contemporary personal branding.  An emphasis will be placed on experiential learning, self-awareness, image and perceptual management, career development, self-promotion, brainstorming, and effective communication.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Convey deep understanding of personal mission, vision, values, personality, skills, and other key attributes and differentiators.
2.  Develop a strong conceptual foundation for formulating distinctive, professional, and effective personal brands.
3.  Demonstrate an understanding of various personal branding principles, theories, tools, techniques, and models.
4.  Critique real-world personal brands for their authenticity, impact, and effectiveness.
5.  Develop and present a personal branding and self-marketing campaign.
