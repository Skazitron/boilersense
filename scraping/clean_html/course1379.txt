CHNS 34200 - Chinese Literature II: Modern Chinese Literature

Credit Hours: 3.00.  Study of selected poetry, drama, and fiction from the twentieth century. Students will read and discuss major authors and texts and will write critical essays on literary topics. Texts, discussion, and written assignments are largely in Chinese.
