STAT 10100 - Freshman Orientation Seminar

Credit Hours: 1.00.  A one-credit course for first year Statistics/Mathematics majors designed to develop concepts and skills in team building, time management, and professional development that complement the students' academic and research training.  Talks from a broad range of speakers are included.

1.  Demonstrate the ability to communicate with peers & speakers in classroom.
