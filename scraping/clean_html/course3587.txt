COM 55800 - Historical Trends In Mass Communication Research

Credit Hours: 3.00.  An examination of research on the process and effects of mass communication. Early studies on radio and film effects, propaganda, attitude formation and change, public opinion and voting, and the interpersonal dimensions of mass communication.
