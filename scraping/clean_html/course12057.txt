EDCI 31102 - Integrating Media In The 3-6 Classroom

Credit Hours: 1.00.  The goal of the course is for teacher candidates to understand how to choose and evaluate children's literature and media to use in the 3-6 classroom, develop ways to integrate literature and media throughout the curriculum, support students' understanding of literature and media using a variety of methods, select appropriate materials for individual student's needs, and assess students' learning. Typically offered Fall Spring Summer.

Learning Outcomes: 
1.  Evaluate both fiction and nonfiction resources for use in the classroom including identifying media which is broadly representative of the world in which we live.
2.  Determine cross-curricular connections, teaching ideas, and assessment methods of individual titles for use in the 3-6 classroom.
3.  Develop materials (i.e., graphic organizers, vocabulary activities, comprehension supports) to use with literature which may be differentiated for individual learner's needs.
4.  Research and develop a rationale for including social justice topics at part of the 3-6 curriculum.
