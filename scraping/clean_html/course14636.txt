AGR 59500 - Current Topics In Agriculture, Natural Resources, And Related Sciences

Credit Hours: 1.00 to 3.00.  Critical examination of developments in specialized and emerging areas of agriculture not taught in other courses. Some topics include lecture, lab, directed reading or independent study. Students must have graduate standing or obtain permission of instructor.
