CGT 27207 - User Experience Design Experience Studio II

Credit Hours: 3.00.  This course allows students to practice as a UX designer in a company-like environment. Students continue to develop their abilities and contribute significantly to projects. Projects utilize a human-centered design approach to provide a rich user experience.
