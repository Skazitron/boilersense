MSL 40200 - Officership

Credit Hours: 3.00 or 4.00.  Designed to explore topics relevant to second lieutenants entering the Army.  Describe legal aspects of decision making and leadership.  Analyze Army organization for operations from the tactical to strategic level. Assess administrative and logistics management functions. Permission of department required. Typically offered Spring.
